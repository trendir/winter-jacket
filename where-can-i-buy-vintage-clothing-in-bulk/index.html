Where Can I Buy Vintage Clothing In Bulk

Written by Petter Jun 12, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you looking to start your own vintage clothing business or add some unique pieces to your wardrobe? Buying vintage clothing in bulk can save you time and money, but finding the right place to buy can be a challenge.

Perhaps you have scoured your local thrift stores and flea markets, but haven't found the variety or quantity you need. Or maybe you've tried online marketplaces, but the prices and quality were not up to par.

One option for buying vintage clothing in bulk is to attend estate sales, auctions, and garage sales. These can be a great source for unique and authentic items, but it can also be time-consuming and unpredictable. Another option is to buy from a wholesale vintage clothing supplier.

Wholesale vintage clothing suppliers specialize in sourcing and selling vintage clothing in bulk. They often have a wide range of styles and eras to choose from, and can offer competitive prices. Here are some tips for finding reputable wholesale vintage clothing suppliers:

Attend Vintage Clothing Trade Shows

Attending vintage clothing trade shows can be a great way to meet wholesale vintage clothing suppliers in person, see their inventory, and make connections. Some popular trade shows include Vintage Fashion Expo, A Current Affair, and Inspiration LA.

Search Online

You can also search for wholesale vintage clothing suppliers online. Be sure to research each supplier thoroughly and read reviews from other customers. Some popular online vintage clothing wholesale suppliers include Beyond Retro Wholesale, Vintage Wholesale, and Tasha Apparel.

Consider the Quality

When buying vintage clothing in bulk, it's important to consider the quality of the items. Be sure to ask the supplier about any flaws or damage, and ask for detailed photos. You don't want to end up with items that are unsellable or unusable.

Check the Minimum Order Quantity

Most wholesale vintage clothing suppliers have a minimum order quantity, which is the minimum number of items you have to purchase. Be sure to check this before placing an order, as it can vary widely between suppliers.

Personal Experience

As someone who has started a vintage clothing business, I found attending trade shows to be the most effective way to find wholesale vintage clothing suppliers. I was able to see the quality of the items in person and make connections with suppliers that I still use today.

Question and Answer

Q: How can I be sure the vintage clothing is authentic?

A: Look for suppliers who specialize in sourcing vintage clothing and have a good reputation for authenticity. Ask for a certificate of authenticity if available.

Q: Is it possible to negotiate prices with wholesale vintage clothing suppliers?

A: It's always worth asking if a supplier is willing to negotiate on price, but keep in mind that many suppliers have already priced their items competitively.

Q: What should I do if I receive damaged items in my order?

A: Contact the supplier right away and provide photos of the damage. Most reputable suppliers will work with you to resolve the issue.

Q: Can I return items if I change my mind?

A: This can vary between suppliers, so be sure to check their return policy before placing an order.

Conclusion of Where Can I Buy Vintage Clothing in Bulk

Buying vintage clothing in bulk can be a great way to start a business or add unique pieces to your wardrobe. By attending trade shows, searching online, considering the quality, and checking the minimum order quantity, you can find reputable wholesale vintage clothing suppliers. Always do your research and ask questions to ensure you are getting the best value for your money.
