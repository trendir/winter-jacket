Designer #1: Chanel

Chanel is a French fashion house that was founded by Coco Chanel in 1909. Chanel is known for their iconic little black dress, quilted handbags, and classic tweed jackets. Coco Chanel was a pioneer in women's fashion, and is credited with creating the "modern woman" look. She believed that women should be comfortable and stylish at the same time, and created clothing that allowed them to move freely without sacrificing style.

Designer #2: Gucci
