What Do You Call The Traditional Indian Dress Explained In Relaxed English Language

Written by Daniel Oct 03, 2023 Â· 3 min read

India is a land of diverse cultures and traditions. One of the most prominent aspects of Indian culture is its traditional dresses. However, for those who are not familiar with Indian culture, it can be confusing to understand what to call the traditional Indian dress. In this blog post, we will explain the answer to the question "What Do You Call the Traditional Indian Dress?" in relaxed English language.

The confusion around what to call the traditional Indian dress arises due to the vast variety of styles and designs available in India. Additionally, there are different types of traditional dresses worn by men and women, making it even more difficult to understand.

The answer to the question "What Do You Call the Traditional Indian Dress?" is "It depends." The traditional Indian dress can vary based on the region, occasion, and gender. However, the most commonly known and recognized traditional Indian dress for women is the saree, and for men, it is the kurta pajama.

To summarize, the traditional Indian dress can vary based on the region, occasion, and gender. The most commonly known traditional Indian dress for women is the saree, and for men, it is the kurta pajama.

What is a Saree?

I grew up in India, and I have fond memories of my mother wearing a saree on special occasions. A saree is a long and elegant piece of cloth that is draped around the body and over the shoulder. It is typically worn with a blouse and a petticoat. Sarees come in different fabrics, designs, and colors, making them a popular choice among Indian women.

What is a Kurta Pajama?

My father used to wear a kurta pajama on formal occasions, and it always made him look regal. A kurta pajama is a traditional Indian attire for men. It consists of a long tunic-like shirt (kurta) and loose-fitting pants (pajama). Kurta pajamas are available in different fabrics, designs, and colors.

Types of Sarees

There are different types of sarees available in India, each with its unique style and design. Some of the most popular types of sarees are:

  • Chiffon Saree
  • Cotton Saree
  • Banarasi Saree
  • Kanjivaram Saree
  • Georgette Saree

How to Choose the Right Saree?

Choosing the right saree can be a daunting task, especially for those who are not familiar with Indian culture. Here are some tips to help you choose the right saree:

  • Consider the occasion
  • Choose the right fabric
  • Choose the right color
  • Consider the design

Conclusion of What Do You Call the Traditional Indian Dress

The traditional Indian dress is a beautiful and elegant attire that is deeply rooted in Indian culture. The most commonly known traditional Indian dress for women is the saree, and for men, it is the kurta pajama. However, there are different types of traditional dresses available in India, each with its unique style and design. We hope this blog post has helped you understand what to call the traditional Indian dress in relaxed English language.
