What Is Indian Garb Called

Written by Bardi Aug 18, 2023 Â· 4 min read

India is a land of diverse cultures and traditions, and its clothing is a reflection of the same. Indian garb is known for its vibrant colors, intricate designs, and comfortable fabrics. If you are curious about what is Indian garb called, you have come to the right place.

When it comes to Indian clothing, there are often misconceptions and confusion about what to call them. This can make it difficult to find the right clothing or even to talk about them without feeling awkward or ignorant.

The answer to what is Indian garb called is not straightforward, as there are many different types of clothing worn in India. However, the most common term used to describe Indian clothing is "ethnic wear" or "traditional wear."

In this article, we will explore what is Indian garb called and its different types, so you can better understand and appreciate the beauty of Indian clothing.

What is Indian Garb Called and Its Different Types?

Indian garb can be broadly categorized into two types - traditional wear and western wear.

Traditional wear includes sarees, salwar suits, lehenga-choli, and kurtis. Sarees are the most popular traditional Indian attire and are a long piece of cloth draped around the body. Salwar suits consist of a long tunic and loose pants, while lehenga-choli is a long skirt and blouse worn with a dupatta. Kurtis are shorter tunics worn with leggings or jeans.

Western wear, on the other hand, includes clothing that is influenced by western culture, such as jeans, t-shirts, and skirts.

Indian garb is often embellished with intricate embroidery, beadwork, and prints, making them a work of art. They are made from comfortable fabrics such as cotton, silk, and chiffon, making them ideal for the hot and humid climate of India.

Personal Experience with Indian Garb

As an Indian, I have grown up wearing traditional Indian clothing for weddings and festivals. I have fond memories of wearing bright-colored sarees and lehenga-cholis and feeling like a princess. The intricate details of the embroidery and the softness of the fabrics always amazed me.

However, I have also experienced the confusion of what to call Indian clothing when talking to people from different cultures. It can be challenging to explain the different types of clothing and their names, but I always take it as an opportunity to share my culture with others.

Why is Indian Garb Important?

Indian garb is not just clothing; it is a representation of the rich culture and heritage of India. It is a way of preserving traditions and passing them on to the next generation. Indian clothing is also a source of pride and identity for many Indians living abroad, as it reminds them of their roots.

Moreover, Indian clothing has gained popularity worldwide due to its unique designs and fabrics. It has become a fashion statement and is often seen on international runways.

Types of Indian Garb

Here are some of the most popular types of Indian garb:

  • Sarees
  • Salwar suits
  • Lehenga-choli
  • Kurtis
  • Anarkali suits
  • Dhoti-kurta
  • Sherwani
  • Churidar suits

How to Choose the Right Indian Garb?

Choosing the right Indian garb depends on the occasion, your body type, and personal style. Here are some tips to help you choose the perfect Indian outfit:

  • For weddings and formal occasions, opt for sarees or lehenga-cholis.
  • Salwar suits and kurtis are ideal for casual events or everyday wear.
  • If you have a curvy body type, go for sarees or lehenga-cholis that accentuate your curves.
  • If you have a petite body type, opt for salwar suits or kurtis that make you look taller.
  • Choose colors and fabrics that complement your skin tone and make you feel comfortable.

Question and Answer

Q: Is Indian garb only for women?

A: No, Indian garb is for both men and women. Men can wear dhoti-kurtas, sherwanis, and kurta-pajamas for weddings and formal events.

Q: Do I need to wear traditional Indian clothing for Indian weddings?

A: It is not mandatory to wear traditional Indian clothing for Indian weddings, but it is highly recommended. You can also opt for fusion wear, which is a blend of traditional and western wear.

Q: Can I wear Indian clothing to work?

A: Yes, you can wear Indian clothing to work, but it depends on the dress code of your workplace. Opt for subtle and comfortable Indian wear, such as kurtis or salwar suits.

Q: Is Indian garb expensive?

A: The cost of Indian garb varies depending on the type of clothing, the fabric used, and the amount of embellishment. However, there are options for every budget, from affordable cotton kurtis to luxurious silk sarees.

Conclusion of What is Indian Garb Called

In conclusion, Indian garb is known for its vibrant colors, intricate designs, and comfortable fabrics. The most common term used to describe Indian clothing is "ethnic wear" or "traditional wear." Indian clothing is not just clothing; it is a representation of the rich culture and heritage of India. By understanding what is Indian garb called and its different types, you can appreciate the beauty and significance of Indian clothing.
