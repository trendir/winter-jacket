How to Choose the Right Fit for Your Suit?

Choosing the right fit for your suit is crucial to look your best on your wedding day. Here are some tips on how to choose the right fit for your suit:

  • Make sure the suit fits comfortably on your body without being too tight or too loose.
  • The shoulder of the suit should fit perfectly without any wrinkles or bunching.
  • The sleeves of the suit should be long enough to cover your wrists but not too long.
  • The trousers of the suit should sit comfortably on your waist and not be too long or short.
  • The length of the jacket should be just below the hipbone.

How to Take Care of Your Suit?

Taking care of your suit is important to ensure that it lasts longer and looks good on your special day. Here are some tips on how to take care of your suit:

  • Hang your suit on a hanger after wearing it to avoid wrinkles.
  • Use a soft-bristled brush to remove any dirt or dust from the suit.
  • Avoid washing the suit in the washing machine as it can damage the fabric.
  • Take the suit to a professional dry cleaner for cleaning and maintenance.
