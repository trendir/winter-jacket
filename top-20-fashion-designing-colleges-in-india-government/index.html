Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: What is the eligibility criteria for fashion designing courses?

A: The eligibility criteria vary for different courses and colleges. Generally, candidates must have passed 10+2 from a recognized board with a minimum of 50% marks.

Q: What is the duration of fashion designing courses?

A: The duration of fashion designing courses varies from 1 year to 4 years, depending on the course and college.

Q: What is the career scope after completing a fashion designing course?

A: After completing a fashion designing course, you can work as a fashion designer, textile designer, fashion stylist, fashion merchandiser, or fashion illustrator. You can also start your own fashion brand or work as a freelancer.

Q: Is it necessary to have a degree in fashion designing to pursue a career in the fashion industry?

A: No, it's not necessary to have a degree in fashion designing to pursue a career in the fashion industry. However, a degree can give you an edge over others and help you gain knowledge and practical experience.
