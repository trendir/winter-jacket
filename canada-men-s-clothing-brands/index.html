Canada Goose

Canada Goose is a household name when it comes to winter wear. Their iconic parkas and jackets are designed to withstand even the harshest Canadian winters, making them a must-have for any fashion-forward man. Not only are they functional, but they also offer a sleek and stylish look that can be dressed up or down.

Personally, I love my Canada Goose parka - it keeps me warm and stylish during the colder months. The brand also offers a range of accessories, such as hats and gloves, to complete your winter look.

Roots

Roots is a Canadian brand that has been around since 1973. They are known for their casual and comfortable clothing, made with high-quality materials such as leather and cotton. Whether you're looking for a cozy hoodie or a classic leather jacket, Roots has something for everyone.

I own a few items from Roots and they have quickly become some of my go-to pieces for everyday wear. Not only are they comfortable, but they also offer a timeless and versatile style that can be dressed up or down.

Naked & Famous

Naked & Famous is a denim brand that is based in Montreal. They are known for their high-quality Japanese denim, as well as their unique and bold designs. If you're looking for a statement piece to add to your wardrobe, then Naked & Famous is the brand for you.

I recently purchased a pair of Naked & Famous jeans and was blown away by the quality and attention to detail. The brand also offers a range of fits and styles, so you're sure to find something that fits your body type and personal style.

Wings + Horns

Wings + Horns is a brand that focuses on creating timeless and functional clothing that is made to last. They use high-quality materials such as wool, cashmere, and leather to create their pieces, which are designed with both style and practicality in mind.

I recently invested in a Wings + Horns wool sweater and it has quickly become one of my favorite pieces. It's warm, comfortable, and offers a classic and versatile style that can be worn for any occasion.
