Discover The Best Antique Stores In London England

Written by Bardi Nov 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

London, England is a hub of culture and history, with antique stores scattered throughout the city. Finding the best antique stores can be a daunting task, as there are so many to choose from. Whether you are a seasoned antique collector or a newbie to the world of antiques, this guide will help you discover the best antique stores in London, England.

Are you looking for a unique piece of furniture to add character to your home? Or perhaps you are on the hunt for vintage clothing or antique jewelry? Maybe you just want to explore the history and stories behind each antique piece. Whatever your reason for seeking out antique stores in London, England, this guide will provide you with the information you need.

This article will provide you with the top antique stores in London, England, and offer some insight into what makes each store special. We will cover the history of each store, the types of antiques they offer, and what makes them stand out in the world of antiques.

The Old Cinema

The Old Cinema is a unique antique store in London, England, that offers a wide variety of vintage and antique furniture, lighting, and decorative items. This store is housed in a beautiful Art Deco building in Chiswick, West London. The store is known for its eclectic mix of antique and vintage items, and the knowledgeable staff are always happy to help you find the perfect piece for your home.

Grays Antiques

Grays Antiques is a well-known antique store in London, England, that has been in business for over 40 years. The store is located in Mayfair, and it offers a wide range of antiques, including jewelry, silverware, furniture, and art. The store is known for its high-quality pieces and its knowledgeable staff.

Portobello Road Market

The Portobello Road Market is a must-visit destination for antique lovers in London, England. This famous street market is located in the Notting Hill neighborhood and offers a wide variety of antiques, including vintage clothing, antique jewelry, and unique home decor items. The market is open every day except Sunday, and it is a great place to find unique and affordable antiques.

Bermondsey Antiques Market

The Bermondsey Antiques Market is another popular destination for antique lovers in London, England. This market is held every Friday morning in Bermondsey Square and offers a wide range of antiques, including silverware, furniture, and vintage clothing. The market is known for its friendly atmosphere and affordable prices.

LASSCO

LASSCO is a unique antique store in London, England, that specializes in architectural salvage and reclaimed materials. The store offers a wide variety of antique doors, windows, flooring, and other architectural elements. The store is known for its high-quality pieces and its commitment to sustainability.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for when shopping for antiques?

A: When shopping for antiques, it is important to look for quality pieces that are in good condition. Look for pieces that have a history and a story behind them.

Q: How can I tell if an antique is authentic?

A: Authentic antiques will have signs of wear and tear that are consistent with their age. Look for signs of age, such as patina, rust, or fading. Additionally, do your research and learn about the history and style of the pieces you are interested in.

Q: How can I negotiate the price of an antique?

A: When negotiating the price of an antique, be respectful and polite. Do your research and know the market value of the piece. Offer a fair price based on the condition and rarity of the piece.

Q: What should I do if I suspect an antique is a fake?

A: If you suspect an antique is a fake, do your research and seek the advice of a reputable antique dealer or appraiser. Be wary of buying antiques online or from sellers who do not have a proven track record of selling authentic antiques.

Conclusion of Best Antique Stores in London, England

London, England is home to some of the best antique stores in the world. Whether you are a seasoned collector or a newbie to the world of antiques, there is something for everyone in London. From unique vintage furniture to antique jewelry and everything in between, London's antique stores offer a glimpse into the city's rich history and culture. Use this guide to discover the best antique stores in London, England, and find your next treasure.
