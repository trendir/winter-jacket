Question and Answer:

Q: How do I choose the right size t shirt?

A: It's important to measure yourself before purchasing a t shirt. Look up the sizing chart for the brand you're interested in and measure your chest, waist, and hips. Make sure to choose the size that corresponds to your measurements.

Q: How do I know what material to choose?

A: Consider what you'll be wearing the shirt for. If you're looking for a t shirt to wear during exercise, choose a material that wicks away sweat. If you're looking for a t shirt for everyday wear, choose a material that feels comfortable against your skin.

Q: Can I wear a graphic tee to a formal event?

A: It's generally not recommended to wear a graphic tee to a formal event. Stick to a basic, solid-colored t shirt instead.

Q: How do I care for my t shirts?

A: Always check the care label on your t shirt before washing it. Most t shirts can be washed in cold water and tumble dried on low heat. Avoid using bleach or fabric softener, as they can damage the material.
