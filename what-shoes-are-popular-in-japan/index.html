What Shoes Are Popular In Japan

Written by Bardi Oct 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Japan is known for its unique fashion and style, and shoes are no exception. For those who are looking to keep up with the latest trends in Japan, it can be difficult to know what shoes are popular and where to find them.

Whether you're a fashion enthusiast or just want to know what shoes to wear during your next trip to Japan, it's important to understand the current shoe trends and what's driving them.

So, what shoes are popular in Japan right now? The answer is not as simple as you might think. Japanese fashion is known for its diversity, and there are a variety of shoe styles that are currently trending in Japan.

From sneakers to sandals, boots to loafers, Japanese fashionistas are always on the lookout for the next big thing. In this article, we'll explore some of the most popular shoe styles in Japan and what makes them so appealing to fashion lovers in the country.

Sneakers

When it comes to shoes that are popular in Japan, sneakers are definitely at the top of the list. From classic brands like Nike and Adidas to more niche Japanese labels like Onitsuka Tiger and Asics, there's no shortage of options for sneaker lovers in Japan.

Personally, I love wearing sneakers when I'm out and about in Tokyo. They're comfortable, versatile, and can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Plus, there's something about the clean, minimalist design of Japanese sneakers that really appeals to me.

Boots

Another popular shoe style in Japan is boots. From ankle boots to knee-high boots, there are a variety of styles to choose from depending on your personal taste and the season. In the fall and winter months, you'll see a lot of people wearing boots with thick socks and cozy knitwear.

Personally, I love wearing ankle boots with a chunky heel. They're stylish and comfortable, and they add a bit of height without being too difficult to walk in. Plus, they look great with a variety of outfits, from jeans and a sweater to a cute dress.

Sandals

While sandals might not be the first shoe that comes to mind when you think of Japan, they're actually quite popular in the summer months. From simple flip-flops to more elaborate designs with straps and buckles, there are a variety of sandal styles to choose from in Japan.

Personally, I love wearing sandals when I'm exploring Tokyo in the summer. They're comfortable and breathable, and they're easy to slip on and off when you're going in and out of buildings. Plus, there are so many cute and stylish sandal designs to choose from in Japan.

Loafers

Finally, loafers are another popular shoe style in Japan. These classic shoes are perfect for dressing up an outfit or adding a bit of sophistication to a casual look. From simple leather designs to more elaborate loafers with tassels or embellishments, there are a variety of styles to choose from.

Personally, I love wearing loafers with a pair of cropped pants or a midi skirt. They add a bit of polish to an outfit without looking too stuffy, and they're comfortable enough to wear all day.

Question and Answer

Q: What shoes are popular in Japan for men?

A: Sneakers and boots are both popular shoe styles for men in Japan. Many men also wear loafers or dress shoes for formal occasions.

Q: What are some popular Japanese shoe brands?

A: Some popular Japanese shoe brands include Onitsuka Tiger, Asics, and Mizuno. International brands like Nike and Adidas are also popular in Japan.

Q: Are high heels popular in Japan?

A: While high heels are certainly worn in Japan, they're not as popular as more comfortable shoe styles like sneakers and loafers.

Q: What shoes should I wear when visiting Japan?

A: Comfortable shoes are a must when visiting Japan, as there's often a lot of walking involved. Sneakers or comfortable sandals are a great choice, depending on the season.

Conclusion of What Shoes Are Popular in Japan

There are a variety of shoe styles that are currently popular in Japan, from sneakers to sandals, boots to loafers. Whether you're looking to keep up with the latest fashion trends or just want to know what shoes to wear during your next trip to Japan, it's important to understand what's currently driving the shoe market in the country.

By exploring some of the most popular shoe styles in Japan and learning what makes them so appealing to fashion lovers in the country, we hope that this article has given you a better understanding of what shoes are currently trending in Japan.
