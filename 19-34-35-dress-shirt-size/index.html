How to Measure for 19 34/35 Dress Shirt Size

If you're unsure of your dress shirt size, it's important to take accurate measurements to ensure the best fit. To measure your neck size, use a cloth tape measure and wrap it around the base of your neck, where your shirt collar would normally sit. Make sure to add a half-inch to your measurement to ensure a comfortable fit.

To measure your sleeve length, stand with your arms at your sides and have someone measure from the center of your back, just below your neck, down to your wrist. Make sure to keep your arm slightly bent to ensure an accurate measurement.

How to Care for Your Dress Shirts

Once you've found the right dress shirt size, it's important to take care of your shirts to ensure they last as long as possible. Here are a few tips for caring for your dress shirts:

  • Iron or steam your shirts to remove wrinkles
