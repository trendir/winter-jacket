If you're craving authentic North Indian cuisine, you might want to head to Mehrauli. The area is known for its rich history and diverse culture, and it's home to some of the best restaurants in Delhi. However, finding the best North Indian restaurants in Mehrauli can be a daunting task, especially if you're new to the area. In this blog post, we'll take a closer look at some of the top restaurants that serve delicious North Indian dishes.

If you're a foodie, you know how frustrating it can be to find the right restaurant that serves your favorite cuisine. Our goal is to help you narrow down your options and find the best North Indian restaurants in Mehrauli. Whether you're in the mood for biryani, kebabs, or butter chicken, we've got you covered.

In summary, this blog post will provide you with a comprehensive guide to the best North Indian restaurants in Mehrauli. We'll cover everything from the top-rated restaurants to the must-try dishes. So, without further ado, let's get started.

1. Gali Paranthe Wali

Located in the heart of Mehrauli, Gali Paranthe Wali is a must-visit restaurant for anyone who loves North Indian cuisine. The restaurant is famous for its parathas, which are made using traditional techniques and served with a variety of chutneys and curries.

I visited Gali Paranthe Wali with my friends, and we were blown away by the flavors of the parathas. We tried the aloo paratha, paneer paratha, and gobi paratha, and they were all delicious. The restaurant also has a great ambiance, with colorful decor and friendly staff.

2. Punjab Grill

If you want to indulge in some fine dining, Punjab Grill is the place to be. The restaurant serves authentic Punjabi cuisine, including butter chicken, dal makhani, and tandoori chicken.

I celebrated my sister's birthday at Punjab Grill, and it was a memorable experience. The staff was courteous, and the food was excellent. We especially loved the butter chicken, which was rich and creamy.

3. Qutub Minar

Qutub Minar is another great restaurant that serves North Indian cuisine. The restaurant has a beautiful outdoor seating area, which provides a stunning view of the Qutub Minar.

I visited Qutub Minar with my family, and we were impressed by the food and the ambiance. We tried the chicken tikka, seekh kebab, and biryani, and they were all flavorful and delicious.

4. Naivedyam

If you're looking for vegetarian options, Naivedyam is the perfect restaurant for you. The restaurant serves authentic South Indian cuisine, including dosas, idlis, and vadas.

I visited Naivedyam with my colleagues, and we were impressed by the quality of the food. The dosas were crispy and flavorful, and the chutneys were delicious. The restaurant also has a cozy ambiance, which adds to the overall dining experience.

If you're in Mehrauli and craving North Indian cuisine, you won't be disappointed. The area is home to some of the best restaurants in Delhi, and we've highlighted some of them in this blog post. From the parathas at Gali Paranthe Wali to the fine dining experience at Punjab Grill, there's something for everyone. So, head to Mehrauli and indulge in the flavors of North India.
