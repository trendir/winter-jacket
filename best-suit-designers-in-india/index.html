Best Suit Designers In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Aug 06, 2023 · 5 min read

India is home to some of the finest suit designers in the world. But choosing the right one can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it's easy to get overwhelmed. In this post, we'll give you a comprehensive guide to some of the best suit designers in India, so you can make an informed decision.

If you're looking for the best suit designers in India, you're probably looking for a high-quality suit that fits well, looks great, and is made to last. You might also be looking for a designer who offers custom tailoring or bespoke services, so you can get a suit that's tailored specifically to your body and your style.

From Sabyasachi Mukherjee to Manish Malhotra, India is home to some of the most talented and sought-after suit designers in the world. These designers are known for their impeccable craftsmanship, attention to detail, and use of the finest materials.

In this post, we've covered some of the best suit designers in India, including their signature styles, prices, and where to find them. We've also included tips on how to choose the right suit designer for you, so you can get a suit that fits your body and your style perfectly.

Sabyasachi Mukherjee

Sabyasachi Mukherjee is one of the most well-known and respected suit designers in India. He is known for his traditional designs, which are inspired by Indian culture and heritage. His suits are made using the finest materials, including silk, velvet, and organza, and are embellished with intricate embroidery and embellishments.

Personally, I had the privilege of wearing a Sabyasachi suit at my sister's wedding. The fit and the finishing were impeccable, and the attention to detail was simply amazing. The suit was comfortable to wear, and I received several compliments throughout the day.

Manish Malhotra

Manish Malhotra is one of the most popular and sought-after suit designers in India. He is known for his glamorous and stylish designs, which feature bold colors, intricate embroidery, and a modern twist on traditional Indian wear.

I had the opportunity to work with Manish Malhotra for a fashion shoot, and I was blown away by the level of creativity and attention to detail that went into his designs. His suits are perfect for those who want to make a statement and stand out from the crowd.

What to look for in a suit designer?

When choosing a suit designer, there are several factors to consider. First and foremost, you want to choose a designer who has a proven track record of creating high-quality, well-fitting suits. You also want to choose someone who offers custom tailoring or bespoke services, so you can get a suit that's tailored specifically to your body and your style.

Other factors to consider include the designer's signature style, their prices, and their availability. You want to choose a designer whose style aligns with your personal taste, and whose prices fit within your budget. You also want to choose someone who is available to work with you within your desired timeline.

How to care for your suit?

Caring for your suit is essential if you want it to look good and last for years to come. To care for your suit, you should always hang it up after wearing it, and avoid folding it or putting it in a cramped space. You should also have it dry cleaned or professionally pressed periodically, to keep it looking fresh and new.

Anita Dongre

Anita Dongre is a popular suit designer in India, known for her elegant and feminine designs. Her suits feature delicate embroidery, floral prints, and a soft color palette, making them perfect for weddings, parties, and other formal occasions.

Personally, I wore an Anita Dongre suit to a friend's wedding, and I was extremely pleased with the quality and fit. The suit was comfortable to wear, and the intricate embroidery added a touch of elegance and sophistication.

Question and Answer

Q. What is the average price of a suit by a top designer in India?

A. The price of a suit by a top designer in India can range anywhere from INR 50,000 to INR 5,00,000, depending on the designer, the materials used, and the level of customization.

Q. Can I get a suit custom-tailored by a top designer in India?

A. Yes, many top designers in India offer custom tailoring and bespoke services, so you can get a suit that's tailored specifically to your body and your style.

Q. How long does it take to get a suit made by a top designer in India?

A. The time it takes to get a suit made by a top designer in India can vary, depending on the designer's availability and workload. However, most designers require at least 4-6 weeks to complete a custom suit.

Q. Which is the best designer for traditional Indian suits?

A. Sabyasachi Mukherjee is widely regarded as one of the best designers for traditional Indian suits. His designs are inspired by Indian culture and heritage, and he uses the finest materials and embellishments to create one-of-a-kind pieces.

Conclusion of Best Suit Designers in India

Choosing the right suit designer in India can be a challenging task, but with this comprehensive guide, you should be able to make an informed decision. Whether you're looking for traditional designs, modern twists, or something in between, India is home to some of the finest suit designers in the world. By considering factors like the designer's style, prices, and availability, you can find a suit that fits your body and your style perfectly.
