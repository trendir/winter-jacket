The Most Expensive Sports Memorabilia Of All Time

Written by Oliver Sep 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a sports fan looking to add some unique and valuable items to your collection? Have you ever wondered what the highest priced sports memorabilia of all time is? Look no further, as we explore some of the most valuable and sought-after sports collectibles.

There's no doubt that sports memorabilia has become a hot commodity in recent years. With collectors and enthusiasts willing to pay top dollar for rare and historic items, the market for sports collectibles has only continued to grow.

So, what exactly is the highest priced sports memorabilia? The answer to that question is not so simple, as there are various items that have sold for millions of dollars over the years. However, we will take a look at some of the most expensive sports memorabilia items that have been sold at auction.

From the legendary Babe Ruth's New York Yankees jersey to the iconic Muhammad Ali gloves, we will dive into the world of sports collectibles and explore the stories behind these prized possessions.

Babe Ruth's New York Yankees Jersey

One of the most coveted items in sports history is Babe Ruth's New York Yankees jersey. In 2012, this iconic jersey was sold at auction for a whopping $4.4 million dollars. The jersey was worn by Ruth during the 1920 season when he hit a then-record 54 home runs.

The buyer of the jersey was none other than Lelands.com founder and chairman Josh Evans, who has been collecting sports memorabilia for over 30 years.

Muhammad Ali's Gloves

Another legendary item that has sold for a hefty price is Muhammad Ali's gloves. In 2014, a pair of gloves worn by Ali during his 1965 title fight against Floyd Patterson sold for $2.4 million dollars.

The buyer of the gloves was an anonymous collector, who beat out several other bidders to secure the prized possession.

Michael Jordan's Game-Worn Sneakers

One of the most iconic basketball players of all time, Michael Jordan's game-worn sneakers have also garnered a lot of attention in the sports collectibles world. In 2020, a pair of Jordan's game-worn sneakers from his rookie season sold for $560,000.

The buyer of the sneakers was collector Nick Fiorella, who has a passion for collecting rare and valuable sports memorabilia.

Tom Brady's Rookie Card

While not a physical item that was worn or used by a player, Tom Brady's rookie card has also become a highly sought-after sports collectible. In 2021, a rare Tom Brady rookie card sold for $1.32 million dollars.

The buyer of the card was collector James Park, who has been collecting sports cards for over 20 years.

Conclusion of Highest Priced Sports Memorabilia

Whether you're a serious collector or simply a sports fan interested in owning a piece of history, the world of sports memorabilia has something for everyone. From jerseys to gloves to game-worn sneakers, these highly coveted items continue to sell for millions of dollars.

So, the next time you're on the hunt for a unique and valuable addition to your collection, consider taking a closer look at some of the most expensive sports memorabilia of all time.
