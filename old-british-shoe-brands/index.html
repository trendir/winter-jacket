Church's Shoes: A Classic British Brand

My personal favorite old British shoe brand is Church's Shoes. Founded in Northampton, England in 1873, Church's is one of the oldest and most respected shoe brands in the world. They are known for their classic styles, attention to detail, and use of high-quality materials.

One of the things that sets Church's apart from other old British shoe brands is their handmade construction. Each shoe is crafted by skilled artisans, ensuring that every detail is perfect. They also use the Goodyear welt construction method, which makes the shoes more durable and easier to repair. Church's shoes are timeless, elegant, and perfect for any occasion.

Tricker's Shoes: A Heritage Shoe Brand

Tricker's Shoes is another iconic old British shoe brand that has been around for over 180 years. Founded in 1829, Tricker's is known for their rugged and durable footwear, which is perfect for the outdoorsman or adventurer.

Tricker's shoes are made using high-quality leather and are handcrafted in their Northampton factory. They are known for their brogue and derby shoes, which are both stylish and practical. Tricker's shoes are perfect for anyone who wants a classic and durable shoe that can withstand the elements.

Loake Shoes: A Family Business

Loake Shoes is a family-owned business that has been making shoes since 1880. They are known for their high-quality leather shoes, which are made using traditional methods and materials. Loake shoes are perfect for anyone who wants a classic shoe that is both stylish and comfortable.

One of the things that sets Loake apart from other old British shoe brands is their commitment to sustainability. They use environmentally friendly materials and processes to make their shoes, which is great for the planet and for your feet.

Crockett & Jones: A Luxury Brand

Crockett & Jones is a luxury shoe brand that has been around since 1879. They are known for their high-quality leather shoes, which are made using the finest materials and craftsmanship. Crockett & Jones shoes are perfect for anyone who wants a classic and elegant shoe that is both stylish and comfortable.

One of the things that sets Crockett & Jones apart from other old British shoe brands is their attention to detail. Each shoe is made by hand, ensuring that every detail is perfect. They also offer a range of styles and colors, so there is something for everyone.
