What To Wear With A Short Dress A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Oct 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Short dresses are perfect for any occasion, whether it's a casual day out or a night on the town. However, it can be challenging to find the right accessories and shoes to wear with a short dress. In this post, we'll guide you through what to wear with a short dress to make you feel confident and stylish.

What to Wear with a Short Dress

When it comes to styling a short dress, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider the occasion. Is it a casual or formal event? Second, think about the length and material of the dress. Is it a flowy summer dress or a fitted cocktail dress?

For a casual day out, pair a short dress with sneakers or sandals for a comfortable and effortless look. You can add a denim jacket or a cardigan for a layered look. For a night out, dress up your short dress with heels and statement jewelry. If you're going to a formal event, opt for a classic pair of pumps and elegant accessories.

Matching Shoes with a Short Dress

When choosing shoes to wear with your short dress, consider the length and style of the dress. For a mini dress, opt for shoes that elongate your legs like high heels or ankle boots. If you're wearing a midi dress, you can choose between heels or flats depending on the occasion. For a maxi dress, opt for sandals or espadrilles for a relaxed and effortless look.

Another thing to consider when choosing shoes is the color and material of the dress. If your dress is a bold color or pattern, opt for neutral shoes that won't clash with your outfit. If your dress is a neutral color, you can add a pop of color with your shoes.

Accessorizing a Short Dress

Accessories can make or break an outfit, and this is especially true for short dresses. When accessorizing a short dress, keep it simple and let the dress be the focal point. A few key accessories can elevate your outfit without overwhelming it.

For a casual look, add a statement necklace or a pair of earrings to your short dress. For a formal event, opt for a clutch and elegant earrings. If you're wearing a printed short dress, keep your accessories simple to avoid clashing with your outfit.

Makeup and Hairstyles that Complement a Short Dress

When it comes to makeup and hairstyles, it's essential to consider the occasion and style of the dress. For a casual day out, opt for natural makeup and a simple hairstyle like a messy bun or a ponytail. For a night out, you can add a bold lipstick or smoky eye to your makeup look. You can also style your hair in loose waves or a sleek updo.

For a formal event, opt for a classic makeup look and an elegant hairstyle like a chignon or a low bun. If you're wearing a short dress with a high neckline, consider styling your hair in an updo to show off the neckline.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear flats with a short dress?

A: Yes, you can wear flats with a short dress, especially if it's a casual look. Opt for a pair of sandals or ballet flats to keep your outfit comfortable and effortless.

Q: What kind of jewelry should I wear with a short dress?

A: When it comes to jewelry, keep it simple and let the dress be the focal point. You can add a statement necklace or a pair of earrings to your short dress for a casual look. For a formal event, opt for elegant earrings and a bracelet or watch.

Q: Can I wear a blazer with a short dress?

A: Yes, you can wear a blazer with a short dress for a layered look. Opt for a fitted blazer that complements the style and color of your dress.

Q: What kind of bag should I carry with a short dress?

A: When choosing a bag to wear with a short dress, consider the occasion and style of the dress. For a casual look, opt for a crossbody bag or a backpack. For a formal event, opt for a clutch or a small handbag.

Conclusion of What to Wear with a Short Dress

Short dresses are versatile and stylish, and with the right accessories and shoes, you can create a look that's perfect for any occasion. Remember to consider the occasion, length, and style of the dress when choosing what to wear with a short dress. Keep it simple and let the dress be the focal point, and you're sure to turn heads wherever you go.
