How to Clean Non Slip Shoes?

To clean non slip shoes, start by removing any loose dirt or debris with a soft-bristled brush. Then, mix a solution of mild soap and water, and use a clean cloth to wipe down the shoes. Be sure to rinse the shoes thoroughly with clean water, and allow them to air dry. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive materials, as these can damage the shoes and reduce their slip-resistant properties.

How to Break in Non Slip Shoes?

Breaking in non slip shoes can take some time, especially if the shoes are made with stiff materials. To break in non slip shoes, start by wearing them for short periods of time around the house. Gradually increase the amount of time you wear the shoes each day, until they feel comfortable enough to wear for a full day of work. Additionally, you can try using shoe stretchers or wearing thicker socks to help break in the shoes more quickly.
