Costly Dress Brands In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jul 06, 2023 · 4 min read

India is a country of diversity, and the fashion industry is no exception. However, with diversity comes a wide range of prices, and sometimes, the prices of high-end fashion brands can be incredibly steep. This can be frustrating for those who want to indulge in luxury fashion but can't afford to do so. In this article, we'll explore the world of costly dress brands in India, the reasons behind their high prices, and whether they're worth the investment.

If you're someone who loves fashion and wants to make a statement with your clothing, you've probably come across some of the most popular high-end fashion brands. While these brands offer gorgeous designs and impeccable quality, the prices can be a major deterrent for many people. It's no secret that luxury fashion comes at a price, and this can be a pain point for many fashion enthusiasts who don't have the budget to splurge on expensive clothing.

So, what exactly are the target customers for costly dress brands in India? Well, these brands cater to people who are willing to spend a substantial amount of money on clothing. They're usually made from the finest fabrics, with attention to detail and unique designs that set them apart from regular clothing brands.

In conclusion, costly dress brands in India are a great investment for those who can afford them. While the prices may be steep, the quality and design of the clothing are unmatched. If you're someone who loves fashion and is willing to splurge on clothing, these brands are definitely worth checking out.

1. Sabyasachi Mukherjee

When it comes to Indian fashion, Sabyasachi Mukherjee is a name that needs no introduction. He's known for his luxurious and intricate designs that are inspired by traditional Indian clothing. His clothing is made from the finest fabrics and embellished with handcrafted details that make each piece unique.

Personally, I had the pleasure of wearing a Sabyasachi lehenga for my wedding, and it was an experience like no other. The intricate hand embroidery and the soft, flowy fabric made me feel like a princess. While the price was steep, it was worth every penny.

2. Manish Malhotra

Manish Malhotra is another well-known name in the Indian fashion industry. He's known for his stunning designs that are a perfect blend of traditional and modern styles. His clothing is made from the finest fabrics and embellished with intricate embroidery and embellishments.

I had the pleasure of wearing a Manish Malhotra saree for a friend's wedding, and I received so many compliments. The saree was made from the softest silk and was adorned with beautiful embroidery. While the price was steep, it was worth it for the quality and design.

3. Rohit Bal

Rohit Bal is a designer who's known for his unique and contemporary designs. His clothing is made from the finest fabrics and is often adorned with intricate embroidery and embellishments. His designs are perfect for those who want to make a statement with their clothing.

4. Tarun Tahiliani

Tarun Tahiliani is a designer who's known for his luxurious and intricate designs. He's known for blending traditional Indian clothing with modern styles to create unique and beautiful designs. His clothing is made from the finest fabrics and is often adorned with intricate embroidery and embellishments.
