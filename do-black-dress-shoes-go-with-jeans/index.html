Do Black Dress Shoes Go With Jeans

Written by Daniel Sep 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old sneakers with your jeans and want to switch things up with a dressier shoe choice? However, you may be hesitant to wear black dress shoes with jeans because you're not sure if they'll look good together. Fear not! In this tutorial, we'll explore whether do black dress shoes go with jeans and give you tips on how to style them.

Many people feel that black dress shoes should be reserved for formal occasions and not paired with jeans. However, this is not entirely true. While black dress shoes may not be the first choice for a casual outfit, they can definitely work with jeans if styled correctly. The key is to find a balance between the dressiness of the shoes and the casualness of the jeans.

The answer is yes, black dress shoes can go with jeans. The trick is to make sure that the jeans are not too casual and the shoes are not too dressy. The best type of jeans to pair with black dress shoes are dark wash jeans. Light wash or ripped jeans are too casual and clash with the formality of the dress shoes.

Personal Experience with Black Dress Shoes and Jeans

I used to be hesitant to wear my black dress shoes with jeans because I thought it would look odd. However, after experimenting with different outfits, I discovered that black dress shoes can be a stylish and versatile choice. I usually pair my black dress shoes with dark wash jeans and a button-down shirt for a dressier look, or with a t-shirt and a blazer for a more casual outfit.

Tips for Styling Black Dress Shoes with Jeans

Here are some tips to keep in mind when styling black dress shoes with jeans:

Choose the Right Fit

Make sure that both the jeans and dress shoes fit properly. Ill-fitting clothes can ruin an outfit, no matter how stylish the individual pieces are.

Experiment with Different Styles

Don't be afraid to try different shoe styles with your jeans. For example, you can opt for sleek black leather dress shoes, or try suede or patent leather for a more unique look.

Can You Wear Black Dress Shoes with Light-Colored Jeans?

While it's best to stick with dark wash jeans when wearing black dress shoes, you can also wear them with light-colored jeans. The key is to make sure that the shoes are not too dressy and that the outfit looks cohesive. For example, you can pair black dress shoes with light-colored jeans and a blazer for a smart casual look.

Question and Answer

Q: Can you wear black dress shoes with ripped jeans?

A: It's not recommended to wear black dress shoes with ripped jeans as the dressiness of the shoes clashes with the casualness of the jeans.

Q: Should you tuck your shirt in when wearing black dress shoes with jeans?

A: It depends on the shirt and the occasion. A button-down shirt should be tucked in for a dressier look, while a t-shirt can be left untucked for a more casual outfit.

Q: Can black dress shoes be worn with a denim jacket?

A: Yes, black dress shoes can be paired with a denim jacket for a smart casual outfit.

Q: What color socks should you wear with black dress shoes and jeans?

A: It's best to wear black socks with black dress shoes and jeans for a cohesive look.

Conclusion of Do Black Dress Shoes Go with Jeans

In conclusion, black dress shoes can definitely go with jeans if styled correctly. Dark wash jeans are the best option, and it's important to strike a balance between the dressiness of the shoes and the casualness of the jeans. Don't be afraid to experiment with different shoe styles and outfit combinations to find the look that works best for you.
