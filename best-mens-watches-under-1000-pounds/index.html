1. Hamilton Khaki Field Watch

The Hamilton Khaki Field Watch is a classic military-style watch that offers durability and functionality without compromising on style. The watch features a rugged stainless steel case, a scratch-resistant sapphire crystal, and luminous hands and markers for easy reading in low-light conditions.

The watch is powered by a Swiss-made automatic movement and has a power reserve of up to 80 hours. The Khaki Field Watch is water-resistant up to 100 meters, making it suitable for swimming and snorkeling.

2. Seiko Prospex Automatic Diver's Watch

The Seiko Prospex Automatic Diver's Watch is a popular choice among watch enthusiasts for its reliability and affordability. The watch features a stainless steel case and bracelet, a unidirectional bezel, and a screw-down crown for added water resistance.

The watch is powered by a Japanese automatic movement and has a power reserve of up to 41 hours. The Prospex Diver's Watch is water-resistant up to 200 meters, making it suitable for diving.

3. Bulova Precisionist Chronograph Watch

The Bulova Precisionist Chronograph Watch is a precision timepiece that offers accuracy up to 1/1000th of a second. The watch features a stainless steel case and bracelet, a curved sapphire crystal, and a tachymeter scale on the bezel.

The watch is powered by a quartz movement and has a battery life of up to 3 years. The Precisionist Chronograph Watch is water-resistant up to 300 meters, making it suitable for diving.

4. Tissot Le Locle Powermatic 80 Watch

The Tissot Le Locle Powermatic 80 Watch is a classic dress watch that features a stainless steel case and bracelet, a scratch-resistant sapphire crystal, and a transparent case back to showcase the automatic movement.

The watch is powered by a Swiss-made automatic movement and has a power reserve of up to 80 hours. The Le Locle Powermatic 80 Watch is water-resistant up to 30 meters, making it suitable for everyday wear.
