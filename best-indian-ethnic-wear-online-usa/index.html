Traditional Sarees

One of the most popular and iconic Indian ethnic wear options is the saree. Sarees come in a wide variety of materials, styles, and colors, making them a versatile option for any occasion. A personal favorite website for saree shopping is Utsav Fashion. They offer a wide selection of sarees ranging from casual to formal, and their prices are reasonable. I recently purchased a stunning silk saree from Utsav for a wedding, and I received so many compliments on it.

Lehengas

Lehengas are another popular Indian ethnic wear option, especially for weddings and other formal events. They consist of a long skirt, a blouse, and a dupatta. A great website for lehenga shopping is Cbazaar. They offer a wide variety of lehengas in different styles and colors at competitive prices. I purchased a beautiful red and gold lehenga for a wedding from Cbazaar, and it was the perfect choice for the occasion.

Kurtis

If you are looking for a more casual Indian ethnic wear option, kurtis are a great choice. They are comfortable, stylish, and perfect for everyday wear. One of my go-to websites for kurti shopping is Indian Cloth Store. They offer a wide selection of kurtis in different styles and sizes, and their prices are very reasonable. I recently purchased a beautiful printed kurti from Indian Cloth Store, and I wear it all the time.

Salwar Suits

Salwar suits are another popular Indian ethnic wear option that is perfect for both formal and casual occasions. They consist of a long tunic, loose pants, and a dupatta. A great website for salwar suit shopping is Kalki Fashion. They offer a wide variety of salwar suits in different styles and colors, and their prices are competitive. I purchased a stunning pink and gold salwar suit from Kalki Fashion for a wedding, and I received so many compliments on it.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I determine my size when shopping for Indian ethnic wear online?

A: Every website has a size chart that you can use to determine your size. Be sure to measure yourself accurately and compare your measurements to the size chart before making a purchase.

Q: Are there any websites that offer free shipping to the USA?

A: Yes, many websites offer free shipping to the USA if you meet a certain purchase threshold. Be sure to check the website's shipping policy before making a purchase.

Q: How do I know if the Indian ethnic wear I am purchasing is authentic?

A: Look for websites that source their clothing directly from India and have good customer reviews. Authentic Indian ethnic wear should be made from high-quality materials and have intricate designs and embellishments.

Q: Can I return Indian ethnic wear if it doesn't fit or if I don't like it?

A: Most websites have a return policy that allows you to return or exchange items within a certain timeframe. Be sure to read the website's return policy before making a purchase.
