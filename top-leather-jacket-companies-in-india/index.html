Top Leather Jacket Companies In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 09, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're looking to buy a leather jacket in India, you might be overwhelmed by the number of options available. With so many companies claiming to offer the best quality and style, it can be hard to know where to start. In this post, we'll take a look at the top leather jacket companies in India and help you make an informed decision.

When it comes to leather jackets, you want to make sure you're getting a product that is high-quality, durable, and stylish. You also want to consider factors like price, customer service, and company reputation. With these factors in mind, we've researched and compiled a list of the top leather jacket companies in India.

Overall, the top leather jacket companies in India offer a range of styles and price points to suit different tastes and budgets. Some companies specialize in classic and timeless designs, while others offer more trendy and edgy options. Regardless of your preference, you are sure to find a leather jacket that suits your style and needs from one of these top companies.

1. Fastrack

Fastrack is a popular Indian brand that offers a range of leather jackets for men and women. The company is known for its trendy and youthful designs that are perfect for those who want to make a fashion statement. Fastrack's jackets are also affordable, making them a great option for those on a budget.

Personally, I have owned a Fastrack leather jacket for over a year now, and it has held up well. The leather is of good quality, and the jacket still looks great despite regular wear.

2. Woodland

Woodland is another popular Indian brand that offers a wide range of leather jackets. The company is known for its rugged and durable designs that are perfect for outdoor activities. Woodland's jackets are also reasonably priced, making them a great option for those who want a high-quality jacket without breaking the bank.

I recently purchased a Woodland leather jacket for a hiking trip, and it held up well against the elements. The leather is thick and durable, and the jacket is comfortable to wear for extended periods.

3. Puma

Puma is a well-known international brand that offers a range of leather jackets for men and women in India. The company is known for its stylish and high-quality designs that are perfect for those who want to make a fashion statement. Puma's jackets are also reasonably priced, making them a great option for those who want a designer jacket without paying designer prices.

A friend of mine owns a Puma leather jacket, and it looks great on him. The leather is soft and supple, and the jacket is well-constructed with attention to detail.

4. Blackberrys

Blackberrys is an Indian brand that offers a range of leather jackets for men. The company is known for its classic and timeless designs that are perfect for those who want a jacket that will never go out of style. Blackberrys' jackets are also reasonably priced, making them a great option for those who want a high-quality jacket without paying designer prices.

I purchased a Blackberrys leather jacket for my husband, and he loves it. The leather is soft and supple, and the jacket is well-made with attention to detail. It's a versatile jacket that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Question and Answer

Q: Are leather jackets expensive in India?

A: The price of leather jackets in India varies depending on the brand and quality. While some jackets can be quite expensive, there are also affordable options available from top companies like Fastrack and Woodland.

Q: How can I tell if a leather jacket is high-quality?

A: A high-quality leather jacket will be made from genuine leather and will have a soft and supple feel. The jacket should also be well-constructed with attention to detail, including sturdy zippers and stitching.

Q: How do I care for my leather jacket?

A: To keep your leather jacket looking its best, you should avoid exposing it to water or extreme heat. You should also condition the leather regularly with a leather conditioner to keep it soft and supple.

Q: Can leather jackets be worn year-round in India?

A: Leather jackets can be worn year-round in India, but they are best suited for cooler weather. In the summer months, you may find that a lighter jacket or vest is more comfortable.

Conclusion of Top Leather Jacket Companies in India

Whether you're looking for a classic and timeless leather jacket or a trendy and edgy option, there are plenty of top leather jacket companies in India to choose from. By considering factors like quality, style, and price, you can find a leather jacket that suits your needs and budget.
