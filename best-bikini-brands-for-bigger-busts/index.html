The Best Bikini Brands For Bigger Busts

Written by Petter Sep 11, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you tired of struggling to find the perfect bikini that fits your bigger bust? Do you feel like you have to sacrifice style for support? Look no further - this post is for you.

When it comes to swimwear, finding the right fit can be a challenge, especially for those with a larger bust. Many brands only cater to smaller sizes, leaving those with a bigger bust feeling frustrated and discouraged. But fear not, there are plenty of bikini brands out there that cater to bigger busts and offer both style and support.

In this post, we will share with you the best bikini brands for bigger busts, along with some personal experiences and tips on how to find the perfect fit.

Panache Swimwear

As a woman with a larger bust, finding swimwear that fits comfortably and supports me while still looking cute was always a struggle. That was until I discovered Panache Swimwear. Their bikinis are designed specifically for bigger busts and offer a range of cup sizes up to a K cup. Not only do they provide excellent support, but they also come in a variety of styles, from classic black to fun prints.

Freya Swimwear

Another brand that caters to bigger busts is Freya Swimwear. Their bikinis come in cup sizes up to a K cup and offer a variety of styles, from halter tops to bandeaus. They also have a range of cute prints and vibrant colors to choose from.

Tips for Finding the Perfect Fit

When shopping for a bikini, it's important to keep a few things in mind to ensure the perfect fit. First, always check the size chart and measure yourself to ensure you are buying the correct size. Next, look for bikinis that offer underwire or molded cups for added support. Finally, try on multiple styles to find what works best for your body type.

How to Care for Your Bikini

Once you've found the perfect bikini, it's important to take care of it to ensure it lasts. Always rinse your bikini after use to remove chlorine or saltwater, and avoid wringing it out as this can damage the fabric. Instead, gently squeeze out excess water and lay flat to dry.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I still wear a triangle bikini top if I have a bigger bust?

A: Yes, you can still wear a triangle bikini top, but look for ones that offer more coverage and support, such as those with thicker straps or underwire.

Q: Can I wear a strapless bikini top with a bigger bust?

A: Yes, but look for ones with molded cups or underwire for added support.

Q: Should I avoid bikinis with ruffles or embellishments if I have a bigger bust?

A: Not necessarily, but keep in mind that ruffles and embellishments can draw attention to your bust, so choose styles that flatter your body type.

Q: Can I wear a bikini with a high neck if I have a bigger bust?

A: Yes, high neck bikinis can be a great option for bigger busts, as they offer more coverage and support.

Conclusion of Best Bikini Brands for Bigger Busts

When it comes to finding the perfect bikini for a bigger bust, it's all about finding the right brand and style that offers both support and style. Panache Swimwear and Freya Swimwear are just a few of the brands that cater to bigger busts and offer a range of styles to choose from. Remember to always check the size chart, look for added support features, and try on multiple styles to find what works best for your body type. Happy shopping!
