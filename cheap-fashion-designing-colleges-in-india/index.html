Cheap Fashion Designing Colleges In India A Guide To Pursuing Your Passion

Written by Aprilia Nov 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to pursue a career in fashion designing but struggling to find an affordable college in India? Look no further. In this post, we will guide you through some of the best and cheapest fashion designing colleges in India, so you can pursue your passion without breaking the bank.

Many students face the challenge of finding a good college that is also budget-friendly. This is especially true for those looking to pursue a career in fashion designing, as the course fees can often be high. However, there are several colleges in India that offer quality education in fashion designing at an affordable price.

Now, let's answer the target of cheap fashion designing colleges in India. The target is to provide information and guidance to students who are interested in pursuing fashion designing but are unable to afford expensive colleges. This post aims to help such students find affordable and quality fashion designing colleges in India.

To summarize, this post will guide you through some of the best and cheapest fashion designing colleges in India. We will provide you with information about these colleges, their fees, courses offered, and other important details. Our aim is to help you pursue your passion for fashion designing without worrying about expensive fees.

Cheap Fashion Designing Colleges in India - Our Recommendations

When I was looking to pursue fashion designing, I faced the same problem of finding an affordable college. After doing thorough research, I found some excellent colleges that offer quality education at an affordable price. Here are our top recommendations:

1. National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT)

Established in 1986, NIFT is one of the most prestigious fashion designing colleges in India. With 16 campuses across India, it offers courses in fashion designing, fashion communication, fashion technology, and more. NIFT has a highly experienced faculty and a strong industry connect, making it one of the top choices for fashion designing students.

2. Pearl Academy

Pearl Academy is another top fashion designing college in India, with campuses in Delhi, Mumbai, Jaipur, and other cities. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in fashion designing, fashion business management, and more. Pearl Academy has collaborations with international universities and industry partners, providing students with a global perspective.

What are the eligibility criteria for these colleges?

The eligibility criteria for these colleges vary depending on the course and level of study. Generally, for undergraduate courses, students need to have completed their 10+2 with a minimum of 50% marks. For postgraduate courses, a bachelor's degree in any field is required. Additionally, some colleges may conduct an entrance exam or interview for admission.

How can I apply for these colleges?

Most colleges have an online application process for admission. You can visit their official website, fill in the application form, and submit the required documents. Some colleges may also require you to appear for an entrance exam or interview as part of the admission process.

3. Indian Institute of Fashion Technology (IIFT)

Founded in 2001, IIFT is a relatively new but highly reputed fashion designing college in India. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in fashion designing, fashion business management, and more. IIFT has a state-of-the-art infrastructure and a highly experienced faculty, making it a great choice for students looking for quality education at an affordable price.

Conclusion of Cheap Fashion Designing Colleges in India

In conclusion, pursuing a career in fashion designing doesn't have to be expensive. There are several affordable colleges in India that offer quality education in this field. By doing thorough research and considering factors such as course fees, faculty, and industry connect, you can find the best college for your needs. We hope this guide has helped you in your search for cheap fashion designing colleges in India.

Question and Answer

Q1. What is the average course fee for fashion designing in India?

A1. The average course fee for fashion designing in India ranges from INR 50,000 to INR 2,00,000 per annum.

Q2. Can I get a scholarship for fashion designing courses in India?

A2. Yes, several colleges in India offer scholarships to students based on their academic performance and financial background.

Q3. What are the career options after completing a course in fashion designing?

A3. After completing a course in fashion designing, you can work as a fashion designer, fashion stylist, fashion journalist, fashion merchandiser, or start your own fashion label.

Q4. Is it necessary to pursue a fashion designing course to become a fashion designer?

A4. While a fashion designing course can provide you with the necessary skills and knowledge, it is not necessary to pursue a course to become a fashion designer. Many successful fashion designers are self-taught and have learned through experience.
