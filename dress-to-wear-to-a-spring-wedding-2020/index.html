Dress To Wear To A Spring Wedding 2020 A Complete Guide

Written by Eveline Oct 19, 2023 · 4 min read

Spring is the perfect time of year for weddings, with blooming flowers and warm weather creating a beautiful backdrop for the big day. But with so many dress options available, it can be overwhelming to choose what to wear. In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know to find the perfect dress to wear to a spring wedding in 2020.

What to Keep in Mind When Choosing a Dress

Before you start shopping, there are a few things to keep in mind when choosing a dress for a spring wedding:

  • Consider the wedding venue and dress code
  • Think about the weather and choose appropriate fabrics
  • Choose a dress that flatters your body type and makes you feel confident
  • Accessorize appropriately with shoes, jewelry, and a clutch

With these tips in mind, you're ready to start shopping for the perfect dress!

Personal Experience: My Search for the Perfect Dress

When I received an invitation to a spring wedding, I immediately started searching for the perfect dress. I knew I wanted something feminine and flowy, but also appropriate for the dress code (semi-formal). After trying on several options, I finally found a beautiful floral dress that fit all of my criteria. I paired it with nude heels and simple jewelry, and felt confident and comfortable throughout the wedding.

Trending Styles for Spring Weddings

When it comes to spring wedding fashion, there are a few styles that are particularly popular this year:

  • Floral prints
  • Pastel colors
  • Maxi and midi length dresses
  • Off-the-shoulder and one-shoulder styles

However, it's important to remember that trends come and go, and ultimately you should choose a dress that makes you feel confident and comfortable.

The Best Fabrics for Spring Weddings

When choosing a dress to wear to a spring wedding, it's important to choose fabrics that are appropriate for the weather. Some of the best options include:

  • Cotton
  • Linen
  • Silk
  • Chiffon

These fabrics are lightweight and breathable, perfect for a warm spring day.

Tips for Accessorizing Your Spring Wedding Dress

Once you've chosen the perfect dress, it's time to accessorize! Here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose shoes that are comfortable for standing and dancing
  • Keep jewelry simple and elegant
  • Choose a clutch or small purse to carry essentials
  • Consider a light jacket or shawl for cooler evenings

Personal Experience: How I Accessorized My Dress

For the spring wedding I attended, I paired my floral dress with nude heels, a simple necklace, and a small clutch. I also brought a light cardigan in case I got chilly in the evening. The accessories added just the right amount of elegance to my outfit without overpowering the dress.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a white dress to a spring wedding?
A: It's best to avoid white dresses as they may be mistaken for a wedding dress.

Q: Can I wear a black dress to a spring wedding?
A: While black is typically associated with more formal events, it can be appropriate for a spring wedding if paired with colorful accessories and appropriate fabrics. However, it's best to check with the couple or wedding planner to ensure it's appropriate for the dress code.

Q: Is it appropriate to wear a short dress to a spring wedding?
A: Yes, short dresses can be appropriate for a spring wedding as long as they are not too casual and paired with appropriate accessories.

Q: Can I wear sandals to a spring wedding?
A: Sandals can be appropriate for a spring wedding, but it's best to choose dressier styles and avoid flip-flops or beach sandals.

Conclusion of Dress to Wear to a Spring Wedding 2020

When it comes to choosing a dress to wear to a spring wedding in 2020, there are a few key things to keep in mind. Consider the wedding venue and dress code, choose appropriate fabrics, and accessorize with elegant shoes, jewelry, and a clutch. Most importantly, choose a dress that makes you feel confident and comfortable. With these tips in mind, you're sure to find the perfect dress for any spring wedding.
