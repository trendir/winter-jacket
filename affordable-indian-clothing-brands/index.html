Affordable Indian Clothing Brands Where To Shop For Budget Friendly Clothing

Written by Bardi Aug 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Have you ever wanted to add some Indian flair to your wardrobe, but felt discouraged by the high prices of traditional clothing? If so, you're not alone. Many people are interested in incorporating Indian fashion into their style, but struggle with finding affordable options. However, there are several Indian clothing brands that offer budget-friendly options without compromising on quality or style.

Shopping for Indian clothing can be overwhelming, especially if you're not familiar with the different styles and materials. Additionally, the prices can vary greatly depending on the brand and the type of garment you're looking for. It's important to do your research and find brands that offer affordable options for the type of clothing you're interested in.

If you're looking for affordable Indian clothing brands, you've come to the right place. In this post, we'll be highlighting some of the best brands for budget-friendly Indian fashion. Whether you're looking for traditional sarees or trendy kurtis, we've got you covered.

Brand 1: Biba

As one of the largest ethnic wear brands in India, Biba is a great option for affordable Indian clothing. They offer a wide range of traditional and contemporary styles, including sarees, kurtis, and salwar suits. One of the best things about Biba is their range of sizes, which includes plus sizes up to 3XL. Additionally, they frequently have sales and discounts, making their already affordable prices even more budget-friendly.

I personally have purchased several kurtis from Biba and have been impressed with the quality and durability of the garments. They also offer free shipping within India, and international shipping at a reasonable cost.

Brand 2: W for Woman

Another great option for affordable Indian clothing is W for Woman. They offer a range of styles, from traditional to modern, and their prices are very reasonable. One of the things that sets W for Woman apart is their focus on sustainable and eco-friendly fashion. They use natural fibers like cotton, linen, and silk, and have a recycling program for their packaging materials.

I recently purchased a beautiful saree from W for Woman for a wedding, and was impressed with the quality and design of the garment. They also have a loyalty program that offers discounts and rewards for repeat customers.

The Benefits of Shopping for Affordable Indian Clothing

There are several benefits to shopping for affordable Indian clothing. First and foremost, it allows you to add some diversity to your wardrobe without breaking the bank. Additionally, many affordable Indian clothing brands offer high-quality garments that are just as beautiful and stylish as more expensive options.

Shopping for affordable Indian clothing can also be a more sustainable option. By opting for brands that use natural fibers and eco-friendly packaging, you can reduce your environmental impact and support ethical fashion practices.

Tips for Shopping for Affordable Indian Clothing

When shopping for affordable Indian clothing, it's important to do your research and compare prices and quality across different brands. Additionally, look for sales and discounts, and consider purchasing off-season items for even better deals.

Another tip is to consider purchasing unstitched fabrics and having them tailored to your specific measurements. This can often be cheaper than purchasing pre-made garments, and allows for a more customized fit.

Brand 3: Global Desi

If you're looking for trendy and contemporary Indian fashion, Global Desi is a great option. They offer a range of styles that blend traditional Indian motifs with modern silhouettes and prints. Their prices are also very reasonable, making them a great choice for budget-conscious shoppers.

I recently purchased a beautiful printed dress from Global Desi, and have received many compliments on it. The quality of the fabric and construction is impressive, especially considering the affordable price point.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if an Indian clothing brand is affordable?

A: The best way to determine if an Indian clothing brand is affordable is to compare their prices to other brands in the same category. Additionally, look for sales and discounts, and consider purchasing off-season items for even better deals.

Q: Can I find plus-size options at affordable Indian clothing brands?

A: Yes, many Indian clothing brands offer plus-size options. Some brands, like Biba, offer sizes up to 3XL.

Q: Is it better to purchase pre-made garments or unstitched fabrics?

A: It depends on your preferences and budget. Pre-made garments are convenient and often come in a wider range of sizes, but can be more expensive. Unstitched fabrics can be cheaper and allow for a more customized fit, but require tailoring.

Q: Are there any ethical and sustainable Indian clothing brands?

A: Yes, there are several Indian clothing brands that focus on sustainable and ethical fashion practices, including W for Woman and Fabindia.

Conclusion of Affordable Indian Clothing Brands

There are many affordable Indian clothing brands that offer beautiful and high-quality garments. By doing your research and comparing prices and quality across different brands, you can find budget-friendly options that fit your style and preferences. Additionally, shopping for affordable Indian clothing can be a more sustainable option, as many brands focus on natural fibers and eco-friendly practices. Happy shopping!
