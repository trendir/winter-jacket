How Much Does Interior Designer Make Per Year A Complete Guide

Written by Robby May 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you interested in pursuing a career in interior design but wondering how much you can earn as a professional designer? You are not alone. Many people are curious about how much interior designers make per year. Interior design is a highly competitive field, and it can be challenging to determine how much you can expect to earn.

Interior designers are responsible for creating functional and aesthetically pleasing spaces for their clients. They work on a wide range of projects, including residential, commercial, and hospitality projects. The main goal of an interior designer is to create a space that meets the client's needs while also being visually appealing.

The average salary for an interior designer in the United States is $55,510 per year. However, this can vary widely depending on factors such as location, experience, and type of employer. Some interior designers make much more than this average, while others make less.

In general, interior designers who work in metropolitan areas tend to earn more than those who work in rural areas. Additionally, those who work for large design firms or corporations tend to earn more than those who work for smaller firms or are self-employed.

Personal Experience

As a professional interior designer with over ten years of experience, I can attest that how much you earn as a designer depends on various factors. When I first started my career, I was working for a small design firm in a rural area, and my starting salary was around $35,000 per year. However, as I gained more experience and moved to larger cities, my salary increased significantly.

Factors That Affect Interior Designer Salaries

1. Location: As mentioned earlier, the location where you work can significantly impact your salary. Interior designers who work in metropolitan areas tend to earn more than those who work in rural areas.

2. Experience: Your level of experience as an interior designer can also impact your salary. Those who are just starting in the field can expect to earn less than those who have been working for many years.

3. Education: While a degree is not always required to work as an interior designer, those with a degree tend to earn more than those without one.

4. Type of Employer: Interior designers who work for large design firms or corporations tend to earn more than those who work for smaller firms or are self-employed.

How to Maximize Your Earnings as an Interior Designer

1. Gain experience: The more experience you have as an interior designer, the more you can expect to earn. Consider taking on internships or freelance projects to gain experience.

2. Specialize: Interior designers who specialize in a particular area, such as hospitality design or healthcare design, tend to earn more than those who work on a variety of projects.

3. Network: Building a strong network of contacts in the industry can help you find higher-paying jobs and opportunities for advancement.

4. Stay up-to-date: Keeping up with the latest design trends, software, and techniques can help you stand out from the competition and command higher salaries.

Question and Answer

Q: Do interior designers make good money?

A: Yes, interior designers can make good money, especially those with several years of experience and specialized expertise.

Q: Can you earn a six-figure salary as an interior designer?

A: Yes, it is possible to earn a six-figure salary as an interior designer, but it depends on factors such as location, experience, and type of employer.

Q: How much does an interior designer make in New York City?

A: Interior designers in New York City can expect to earn an average salary of around $65,000 per year, but this can vary widely depending on factors such as experience and type of employer.

Q: Is a degree required to work as an interior designer?

A: A degree is not always required to work as an interior designer, but having one can increase your earning potential and job opportunities.

Conclusion of How Much Does Interior Designer Make Per Year

In conclusion, the salary of an interior designer can vary widely depending on factors such as location, experience, and type of employer. It is possible to earn a good living as an interior designer, but it takes time, hard work, and dedication. By gaining experience, specializing, networking, and staying up-to-date with the latest trends and techniques, you can maximize your earning potential as an interior designer.
