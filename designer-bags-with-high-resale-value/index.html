Designer Bags With High Resale Value A Complete Guide

Written by Oliver Oct 24, 2023 · 5 min read

Do you love designer bags but hate the thought of spending a fortune on a bag that you might only use a few times? Or are you a savvy shopper looking to invest in a designer bag that you can later resell for a profit? Either way, you're in the right place. In this guide, we'll take a deep dive into the world of designer bags with high resale value.

If you're someone who loves designer bags, you know how expensive they can be. And even if you're willing to invest in one, you might worry that you won't get much use out of it before it's time to move on to the next trend. That's where the idea of buying a designer bag with a high resale value comes in. By investing in a bag that is in demand, you can use it for a while and then sell it for a profit when you're ready to move on to something else.

So, what exactly is a designer bag with a high resale value? Put simply, it's a bag that retains its value well over time, and is in demand on the secondhand market. Some bags can even appreciate in value, meaning that they are worth more now than when they were first sold.

In this guide, we'll cover everything you need to know about designer bags with high resale value. We'll go over some of the most popular bags, what to look for when shopping for a bag with resale value, and how to care for your bag to maintain its value.

Chanel Flap Bag

When it comes to designer bags with high resale value, the Chanel Flap Bag is a classic. This bag has been in production since the 1950s and is still one of the most sought-after bags on the market. The Flap Bag comes in a variety of sizes and materials, but the most popular is the medium-sized bag in black caviar leather with gold hardware.

Personally, I've owned a Chanel Flap Bag for several years and have been able to resell it for a profit. The key to the bag's resale value is its timeless design and high-quality construction. If you're in the market for a Chanel Flap Bag, look for one in good condition with minimal signs of wear and tear. And be prepared to pay a premium for this bag - it's not cheap!

Louis Vuitton Neverfull

The Louis Vuitton Neverfull is another bag with high resale value. This tote bag has been a customer favorite for years, thanks to its spacious interior and versatile design. The Neverfull comes in a variety of materials and prints, but the classic monogram canvas is the most popular.

I personally love my Louis Vuitton Neverfull for travel and work. It's roomy enough to hold all my essentials, but still looks chic and stylish. When shopping for a Neverfull, look for one in good condition with minimal signs of wear. And keep in mind that the monogram canvas version holds its value better than other materials.

Hermès Birkin

The Hermès Birkin is one of the most iconic bags in the world, and it's no surprise that it has high resale value. This bag is handmade from the finest materials and can take up to 48 hours to create. The Birkin comes in a variety of sizes, colors, and materials, but the most popular is the 35cm Birkin in classic colors like black, brown, and tan.

If you're in the market for a Birkin, be prepared to pay a premium. This bag is one of the most expensive in the world, and it's notoriously difficult to get your hands on one. But if you're able to score a Birkin, you can be sure that it will hold its value well over time. Look for one in good condition with all the original packaging and paperwork.

How to Care for Your Designer Bag

If you want your designer bag to maintain its resale value, it's important to take good care of it. Here are some tips for keeping your bag in top condition:

  • Store your bag in its dust bag when you're not using it
  • Keep your bag away from direct sunlight and heat
  • Avoid getting your bag wet or exposing it to moisture
  • Use leather conditioner to keep the leather soft and supple
  • Be gentle when cleaning your bag - use a soft, damp cloth to wipe it down

Conclusion of Designer Bags with High Resale Value

In conclusion, investing in a designer bag with high resale value can be a smart financial decision. By choosing a bag that is in demand, you can use it for a while and then sell it for a profit. Some of the most popular designer bags with high resale value include the Chanel Flap Bag, Louis Vuitton Neverfull, and Hermès Birkin. When shopping for a bag with resale value, look for one in good condition with all the original packaging and paperwork. And be sure to take good care of your bag to maintain its value over time.

Question and Answer Section

Q: What are some other designer bags with high resale value?

A: Some other bags with high resale value include the Gucci Marmont, Saint Laurent Sac de Jour, and Balenciaga City Bag.

Q: How much can I expect to make when reselling a designer bag?

A: The amount you can make when reselling a designer bag depends on a variety of factors, including the brand, style, and condition of the bag. Some bags can sell for close to their original retail price, while others may sell for less.

Q: Is it better to buy a new or used designer bag with high resale value?

A: It depends on the bag and your personal preference. Some people prefer to buy new bags to ensure they are in pristine condition, while others prefer to buy used bags to save money. Just be sure to do your research and look for a bag in good condition.

Q: Can I sell my designer bag anywhere?

A: You can sell your designer bag on a variety of platforms, including online marketplaces like eBay and Poshmark, consignment shops, and luxury resale websites like The RealReal.
