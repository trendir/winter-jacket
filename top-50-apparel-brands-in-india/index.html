Top 50 Apparel Brands in India: #1 - Fabindia

Fabindia is one of the most popular apparel brands in India that focuses on traditional Indian clothes. The brand is known for its unique designs that incorporate traditional Indian textiles and techniques. I have been a loyal customer of Fabindia for over five years now, and the quality of their clothes has never disappointed me.

Their pricing range is on the higher side, but the quality of their clothes justifies the cost. Moreover, their clothes are ethically made, which makes them a sustainable brand. If you are looking for traditional Indian clothes that are sustainable and of good quality, Fabindia is the brand to go for.

Top 50 Apparel Brands in India: #2 - Levi's

Levi's is a globally renowned apparel brand known for its denim jeans. The brand's popularity in India is no different. Levi's has been in the Indian market for over 25 years now and has established itself as one of the go-to brands for denim jeans.

What sets Levi's apart from other denim brands is its quality. The brand uses high-quality denim fabric that lasts long and doesn't fade easily. Moreover, their sizing system is accurate, which makes it easier for customers to find the right size. If you are looking for denim jeans that are of good quality and lasts long, Levi's is the brand to go for.

Top 50 Apparel Brands in India: #3 - Allen Solly

Allen Solly is a popular apparel brand in India that focuses on formal wear. The brand is known for its contemporary designs that cater to the modern working-class. I have been a customer of Allen Solly for over three years now, and I love their formal wear collection.

Their pricing range is on the higher side, but the quality of their clothes justifies the cost. Moreover, their clothes are trendy, which makes them a go-to brand for the modern working-class. If you are looking for formal wear that is trendy and of good quality, Allen Solly is the brand to go for.

Top 50 Apparel Brands in India: #4 - Biba

Biba is a popular apparel brand in India that focuses on traditional Indian wear for women. The brand is known for its colorful designs that incorporate traditional Indian textiles and techniques. I have been a loyal customer of Biba for over five years now, and I love their collection of kurtas and salwar suits.

Their pricing range is on the affordable side, which makes them a popular brand among women in India. Moreover, their clothes are comfortable to wear, which makes them a go-to brand for women who prefer traditional wear. If you are looking for traditional Indian wear that is affordable and comfortable, Biba is the brand to go for.
