Best Branded Suits In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Jun 26, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you in the market for a new suit? Maybe you have an important business meeting or wedding to attend and need to look your best. Choosing the right suit can be a daunting task, especially with so many brands and styles to choose from. In this post, we’ll guide you through the best branded suits in India to help you make an informed decision.

When it comes to buying a suit, there are a few pain points to consider. Firstly, suits can be expensive, so you want to make sure you’re investing in a quality product that will last. Secondly, it can be overwhelming to navigate the wide range of brands and styles available. And finally, it can be difficult to find a suit that fits perfectly, especially if you’re not familiar with the different cuts and sizes.

The target of this post is to provide a comprehensive guide to the best branded suits in India. We’ll cover some of the top brands, styles, and price points, as well as tips for finding the perfect fit.

To summarize, in this post, we’ll explore the best branded suits in India and provide you with all the information you need to make an informed decision. We’ll cover top brands, styles, and price points, as well as tips for finding the perfect fit.

Best Branded Suits in India: Top Brands

As one of the largest clothing markets in the world, India has a wide range of suit brands to choose from. Some of the top brands include Raymond, Louis Philippe, Van Heusen, Allen Solly, and Peter England. Each brand has its own unique style and target audience, so it’s important to do your research to find the right fit for you.

Personally, I’ve had a great experience with Louis Philippe suits. The brand offers a range of styles, from classic to contemporary, and the quality is top-notch. The suits are also available at a range of price points, so you can find something that fits your budget.

Best Branded Suits in India: Styles and Price Points

When it comes to styles, there are a few key options to consider. The classic two-piece suit is a timeless choice that will never go out of style. For a more contemporary look, you may want to consider a slim-fit or three-piece suit. It’s also important to consider the color and material of your suit. Classic colors like black, navy, and gray are always a safe choice, but you may want to opt for something more bold like a burgundy or green suit.

Price points for suits in India can vary widely. You can find budget-friendly options for under Rs. 5,000, but you can also spend upwards of Rs. 50,000 for a high-end suit. It’s important to consider your budget and what you’re looking for in a suit before making a purchase.

Best Branded Suits in India: Tips for Finding the Perfect Fit

Finding the perfect fit for your suit is crucial for looking your best. Here are a few tips to help you find the right fit:

- Know your measurements: Take accurate measurements of your chest, waist, and hips to ensure you’re buying the right size.

- Consider the cut: Different cuts will fit differently, so make sure you choose a cut that flatters your body type.

- Try it on: Always try on your suit before purchasing to ensure the fit is right.

Best Branded Suits in India: How to Care for Your Suit

Once you’ve invested in a quality suit, it’s important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips for caring for your suit:

- Hang it up: Always hang your suit on a wooden hanger to maintain its shape.

- Avoid dry cleaning: Dry cleaning can be harsh on your suit, so try to avoid it unless absolutely necessary.

- Brush it regularly: Use a soft-bristled brush to remove any dirt or debris from your suit.

Best Branded Suits in India: Final Thoughts

Choosing the right suit can be a daunting task, but with a little research and some careful consideration, you can find the perfect suit for you. Remember to consider your budget, style preferences, and fit when making a purchase.
