The Ultimate Guide to Top Plus Size Clothing Brands

As a plus size individual, finding stylish and comfortable clothing can be a frustrating experience. Many brands don't offer extended sizes or may not fit well. However, there are now several top plus size clothing brands that cater to the needs of larger individuals. In this guide, we'll explore some of the best brands for plus size clothing and what makes them stand out.

If you're someone who struggles to find clothing in your size, you're not alone. The fashion industry has long been criticized for its lack of inclusivity, particularly when it comes to plus size clothing. Many individuals have experienced the frustration of finding a cute outfit, only to discover that it doesn't come in their size. This can be a painful experience that can lead to feelings of inadequacy and shame.

The target of top plus size clothing brands is to provide fashionable and comfortable clothing options for individuals who wear larger sizes. These brands aim to create clothing that flatters larger bodies and makes individuals feel confident and stylish. They offer a wide range of sizes, styles, and colors, so that everyone can find something that suits their taste.

In summary, top plus size clothing brands are crucial for individuals who wear larger sizes and want to feel confident and fashionable. These brands offer a wide range of clothing options that are designed to flatter larger bodies and provide comfort and style. In the following sections, we'll explore some of the best plus size clothing brands in more detail.

1. Lane Bryant

Lane Bryant is a well-known name in the plus size clothing industry. The brand has been around for over 100 years and is known for its stylish and comfortable clothing options. Lane Bryant offers a wide range of clothing options, including dresses, tops, pants, and more. Their sizes range from 12 to 28, so they cater to a broad range of individuals.

As someone who wears a size 18, I've found Lane Bryant to be one of the best plus size clothing brands out there. Their clothing is stylish, comfortable, and fits well. I particularly love their dresses, which are perfect for work or a night out. Plus, their sales are fantastic, so you can often find great deals on their clothing.

2. Torrid

Torrid is another popular plus size clothing brand that offers a wide range of clothing options. They cater to sizes 10 to 30 and offer clothing that is trendy and fashionable. Torrid is known for its edgy and playful clothing options, which are perfect for individuals who want to make a statement.

I've always admired Torrid's clothing from afar, but I recently purchased a dress from them, and I'm in love. The dress fits like a dream and is incredibly comfortable. I also appreciate that their clothing is trendy and not frumpy, which can be a common issue with plus size clothing.

3. Eloquii

Eloquii is a relatively new plus size clothing brand but has quickly made a name for itself in the industry. The brand offers clothing in sizes 14 to 28 and is known for its stylish and modern clothing options. Eloquii is dedicated to inclusivity and offers clothing that is designed to flatter larger bodies.

I recently purchased a pair of pants from Eloquii, and I was blown away by the quality of the clothing. The pants fit perfectly and were incredibly comfortable. I also appreciate that the brand offers a wide range of clothing options, from workwear to casual clothing.

4. Universal Standard

Universal Standard is another newer plus size clothing brand that is making waves in the industry. The brand offers clothing in sizes 00 to 40 and is dedicated to inclusivity. Universal Standard is known for its minimalist and modern clothing options, which are perfect for individuals who prefer a more understated look.

While I haven't personally purchased anything from Universal Standard, I've heard great things about the brand's clothing quality and fit. I appreciate that they offer a wide range of sizes and that their clothing is designed to be timeless and versatile.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I know what size to buy from these brands?

A: Each brand typically has its own size chart that you can consult to determine your size. It's important to measure yourself accurately and compare your measurements to the brand's size chart to ensure a proper fit.

Q: Are these brands expensive?

A: It depends on the brand and the specific item you're looking to purchase. Some of these brands can be more expensive than others, but they often offer sales and promotions. It's always a good idea to sign up for a brand's email list to stay updated on sales and deals.

Q: Can I return items if they don't fit?

A: Yes, most of these brands have a return policy that allows you to return items if they don't fit or aren't to your liking. It's important to check the return policy before making a purchase.

Q: Do these brands offer sustainable clothing options?

A: Some of these brands, such as Universal Standard, are dedicated to sustainability and offer eco-friendly clothing options. However, not all of these brands offer sustainable options, so it's important to do your research if sustainability is a priority for you.

Conclusion of Top Plus Size Clothing Brands

Overall, top plus size clothing brands are essential for individuals who wear larger sizes and want to feel stylish and confident. These brands offer a wide range of clothing options that are designed to flatter larger bodies and provide comfort and style. Whether you're looking for workwear, casual clothing, or something more trendy, there's a plus size clothing brand out there for you. By doing your research and trying out different brands, you're sure to find something that makes you feel great.
