Types of Indian Clothing

There are many different types of what is India clothing, each with its own unique style and history. Here are some of the most popular types of Indian clothing:

  • Sarees - a long piece of fabric that is draped around the body and worn with a blouse and petticoat.
  • Salwar Kameez - a two-piece outfit consisting of a tunic top and pants.
  • Lehenga Choli - a three-piece outfit consisting of a long skirt, a blouse, and a dupatta (a long scarf).
  • Kurtas - a long shirt or tunic that is worn with pants or a skirt.
  • Dhotis - a long piece of fabric that is wrapped around the waist and legs, often worn by men.
