My Favorite Second Hand Clothing Stores in Saskatoon

Here are some of my favorite second hand clothing stores in Saskatoon:

The Better Good

The Better Good is a sustainable lifestyle store that offers a variety of eco-friendly and ethical products, including second hand clothes, shoes, and accessories. They have a wide selection of items for men and women, and their prices are very reasonable. They also sell locally made products, which is a great way to support the community.

Value Village

Value Village is a chain of thrift stores that offers a huge selection of second hand clothes, furniture, and household items. They have a loyalty program that allows you to earn points for each purchase, and they often have sales and discounts. While the quality of their items may vary, you can always find something interesting and unique.

Out of the Closet

Out of the Closet is a non-profit thrift store that supports the fight against HIV/AIDS. They have a great selection of second hand clothes, shoes, and accessories, as well as a pharmacy and health clinic. Their prices are very affordable, and you can feel good about supporting a good cause.

The Retro Den

The Retro Den is a vintage clothing store that specializes in 80s and 90s fashion. They have a great selection of denim jackets, graphic tees, and funky accessories. Their prices can be a bit higher than other second hand stores, but the quality of their items is worth it.
