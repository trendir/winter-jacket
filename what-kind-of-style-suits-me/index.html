What Kind Of Style Suits Me

Written by Daniel Aug 11, 2023 · 5 min read

Have you ever stood in front of your closet, staring at your clothes and thinking, "I have nothing to wear!"? Or have you ever looked at someone else's outfit and thought, "I wish I could pull off that look"? Finding your personal style can be a challenge, but it doesn't have to be. With a few tips and tricks, you can discover what kind of style suits you and feel confident in your fashion choices.

Are you someone who struggles with figuring out what to wear each day? Perhaps you are tired of buying clothes that don't fit right or don't match your personal style. It can be frustrating to spend money on clothing that you don't end up wearing. Maybe you feel like you have no sense of fashion, or you simply don't know where to start. These are all common pain points when it comes to finding your personal style.

When it comes to determining what kind of style suits you, it's essential to start with your own preferences. You can begin by identifying what colors, patterns, and styles you are naturally drawn to. Look for inspiration in your favorite clothing items, accessories, or even in your home decor. Consider what clothing items make you feel confident and comfortable, and what styles or colors make you feel less comfortable. This will help you to understand the type of style that suits you best.

In summary, finding your personal style is all about discovering what makes you feel confident and comfortable. By identifying your preferences and what works for your body type, you can create a wardrobe that is uniquely you. Keep in mind that your style may change over time, and that's okay. The most important thing is to feel good in what you wear.

Understanding Your Body Type

One essential aspect of finding your personal style is understanding your body type. Everyone's body is unique, and finding clothing that fits and flatters can be a challenge. Start by identifying your body shape, such as pear, apple, hourglass, or rectangle. This will help you to determine the types of clothing that will look best on you.

For example, if you have a pear-shaped body, you may want to focus on clothing that emphasizes your upper body, such as tops with interesting details or statement jewelry. If you have an hourglass figure, you may want to highlight your waist with a belt or form-fitting clothing. By understanding your body type, you can find clothing that fits well and flatters your shape.

Building Your Wardrobe

Once you have identified your personal style and body type, it's time to start building your wardrobe. Start by investing in some basic pieces that you can mix and match, such as a well-fitting pair of jeans, a few solid-colored tops, and a versatile jacket. From there, you can add in some statement pieces that reflect your personal style, such as bold prints, unique accessories, or trendy shoes.

When it comes to shopping for clothing, it's important to keep your personal style and body type in mind. Avoid buying clothes just because they are trendy or on sale. Instead, focus on finding pieces that you truly love and that fit well. This will help you to create a wardrobe that you can feel confident in.

Accessorizing Your Outfits

Accessories can take your outfits to the next level and make them feel more complete. When it comes to accessorizing, it's important to keep your personal style in mind. If you prefer a more minimalistic look, opt for simple jewelry and neutral-colored accessories. If you love bold prints and bright colors, don't be afraid to mix and match different patterns and colors.

Accessories can also be a great way to switch up your look without buying new clothing. Consider adding a statement necklace, scarf, or hat to an outfit to make it feel fresh and new.

Caring for Your Clothing

Once you have built your wardrobe and found your personal style, it's important to take care of your clothing. Follow the care instructions on each garment to ensure that it lasts as long as possible. This may mean washing certain items in cold water, avoiding the dryer, or hanging certain pieces to dry.

It's also a good idea to invest in high-quality clothing pieces that are made to last. While they may be more expensive upfront, they will save you money in the long run by lasting longer and not needing to be replaced as often.

Q&A

Q: How do I know what colors look best on me?

A: Start by looking at your skin tone and identifying whether you have warm or cool undertones. Once you know this, you can choose colors that complement your skin tone. For example, if you have warm undertones, you may look best in earthy tones like browns, greens, and oranges. If you have cool undertones, you may look best in blues, purples, and pinks.

Q: How do I find my personal style?

A: Start by identifying the types of clothing, colors, and patterns that you are naturally drawn to. Look for inspiration in your favorite clothing items, accessories, or even in your home decor. Consider what clothing items make you feel confident and comfortable, and what styles or colors make you feel less comfortable.

Q: How can I dress up a casual outfit?

A: Adding a few key accessories can instantly dress up a casual outfit. Consider adding a statement necklace, a pair of heels, or a clutch purse to elevate your look.

Q: How can I make my outfits feel more cohesive?

A: Stick to a color palette that complements your personal style. This will help you to mix and match different clothing items without feeling like they don't go together. You can also invest in a few statement pieces that tie your outfits together, such as a patterned scarf or a unique jacket.

Conclusion of What Kind of Style Suits Me

Finding your personal style can be a journey, but it doesn't have to be overwhelming. By identifying your personal preferences, understanding your body type, and investing in quality clothing pieces, you can create a wardrobe that reflects your unique style. Remember, the most important thing is to feel confident and comfortable in what you wear.
