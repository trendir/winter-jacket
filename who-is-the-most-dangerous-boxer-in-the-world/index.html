When we talk about the most dangerous boxer in the world, we're really talking about a few different things. We're also talking about a fighter who has a proven track record of success in the ring. This means they've beaten tough opponents, won titles, and consistently performed at a high level. As one of the most popular boxers in the world, Canelo Alvarez has become a household name among fight fans. But it's not just his popularity that makes him dangerous.
