Located in the heart of downtown, Bee Coffee Roasters is a trendy spot that's perfect for coffee enthusiasts and Instagram influencers alike. With a modern-industrial feel and plenty of natural light, this coffee shop is the perfect spot to catch up with friends or get some work done.

What sets Bee Coffee Roasters apart from other coffee shops in Indy is their commitment to sustainability and community. Located in the historic Athenaeum building in downtown Indy, Coat Check Coffee is a cozy spot that's perfect for a quiet afternoon with a good book. With exposed brick walls, vintage decor, and plenty of seating options, this coffee shop has a laid-back atmosphere that's perfect for relaxation.

From their signature "Coat Check Mocha" to their seasonal drinks like the "Pumpkin Spice Chai," there's always something new and exciting to try at Coat Check Coffee.
