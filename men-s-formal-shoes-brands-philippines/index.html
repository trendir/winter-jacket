Q: How do I choose the right size dress shoe?

A: It's important to try on dress shoes before purchasing to ensure a proper fit. Make sure to measure your feet and compare them to the size chart provided by the brand. If you're unsure, it's always a good idea to go up a half-size to ensure a comfortable fit.

Q: What style of dress shoe should I choose?

A: The style of dress shoe you choose will depend on your personal taste and the occasion. Classic leather oxfords are a great choice for formal events, while slip-on shoes are more casual and can be worn with a variety of outfits.
