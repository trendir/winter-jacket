What is the Smart Casual Dress Code for the Gherkin?

When I visited the Gherkin for a business meeting, I was unsure what to wear. I didn't want to be overdressed, but I also didn't want to be underdressed. I decided to go for smart casual, which is a safe bet for most occasions. For men, smart casual means wearing a collared shirt, trousers, and dress shoes. For women, it means a dress or a blouse with trousers or a skirt. Avoid wearing jeans, t-shirts, or sneakers, as these are too casual for the Gherkin Dress Code.

What Should You Avoid Wearing for the Gherkin Dress Code?

It's important to avoid wearing anything too casual or revealing for the Gherkin Dress Code. This means avoiding jeans, t-shirts, shorts, flip-flops, and sneakers. What Should You Wear for a Business Meeting?

For a business meeting at the Gherkin, it's important to dress professionally. This means wearing a suit, dress shoes, and a tie for men.
