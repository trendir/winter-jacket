Question and Answer

Q: How do I know what fabric to choose for my upholstery project?
A: It depends on the type of project you're working on. For example, if you're working on a sofa, you'll need a durable fabric that can withstand daily use. Look for fabrics that are labeled as "heavy-duty" or "contract grade."

Q: How much fabric do I need for my upholstery project?
A: The amount of fabric you need depends on the size of your project. Most fabric stores will provide a fabric calculator tool that will help you determine how much fabric you need.

Q: How do I care for my upholstery fabric?
A: The care instructions for your upholstery fabric will depend on the type of fabric you choose. Most fabrics can be cleaned with a damp cloth or a mild detergent. Be sure to follow the care instructions provided by the manufacturer.

Q: Can I return upholstery fabric if it's not the right fit?
A: It depends on the store's return policy. Most online stores will allow you to return fabric if it's not the right fit, but be sure to check the store's return policy before making a purchase.
