How to Choose the Best Running Shoes in India Under 20000?

Choosing the best running shoes within your budget can be tricky. Here are some factors that you should consider before making a decision:

  • Type of Running: The type of running you do can influence the type of shoes you need. If you run on the road, you need shoes with good cushioning and support. If you run on the trail, you need shoes with good traction and stability.
  • Foot Type: Your foot type can also influence the type of shoes you need. If you have flat feet, you need shoes with good arch support. If you have high arches, you need shoes with good cushioning.
