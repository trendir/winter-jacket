Indian Men s Suit Name A Guide To Popular Styles

Written by Bardi Aug 28, 2023 Â· 5 min read

When it comes to Indian men's fashion, the suit is a classic staple. Whether it's for a wedding, a business meeting, or a formal event, a well-fitted suit can make all the difference. However, with so many different styles and names, it can be overwhelming to navigate the world of Indian men's suit wear. In this guide, we'll break down the most popular suit styles and their names.

Have you ever been confused by the names of different Indian men's suit styles? Do you struggle to understand the difference between a sherwani and a bandhgala? You're not alone. Many people find it difficult to keep track of the various names and styles, making it challenging to know what to wear for different occasions.

This guide aims to provide a comprehensive overview of the most popular Indian men's suit styles and their names. By the end of this article, you'll have a better understanding of what to wear for different events and how to choose a suit that suits your style and personality.

The Sherwani

The sherwani is a long coat-like garment that is typically worn for formal occasions such as weddings or religious ceremonies. It is often made of silk or brocade and features intricate embroidery or embellishments. The sherwani is usually paired with a pair of churidar pants, a long scarf known as a dupatta, and a pair of leather shoes known as juttis.

My personal experience with the sherwani was at my brother's wedding. I wore a cream-colored sherwani with gold embroidery, paired with a maroon dupatta and matching juttis. I felt regal and sophisticated, and received many compliments on my outfit.

The Bandhgala

The bandhgala, also known as the Jodhpuri suit, is a formal suit that originated in the Jodhpur region of Rajasthan. It features a high collar and a buttoned-up front, and is typically made of silk or wool. The bandhgala is often paired with trousers or churidar pants, and is a popular choice for weddings and other formal events.

For my cousin's wedding, I opted for a navy blue bandhgala with gold buttons and a matching pocket square. I paired it with black trousers and black leather shoes, and felt confident and stylish throughout the event.

The Nehru Jacket

The Nehru jacket is a sleeveless jacket that is often worn over a shirt or kurta. It features a high collar and a buttoned-up front, and is a popular choice for semi-formal events. The Nehru jacket is named after India's first Prime Minister, Jawaharlal Nehru, who was known for wearing this style of jacket.

I wore a beige Nehru jacket over a white kurta for a friend's engagement party. I paired it with blue jeans and brown leather shoes, and felt comfortable and stylish throughout the event.

How to Choose the Right Suit

When it comes to choosing the right suit, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, consider the occasion. Is it a formal event like a wedding, or a semi-formal event like a dinner party? This will help you determine the level of formality required for your outfit.

Next, consider your personal style. Do you prefer classic, traditional styles like the sherwani, or more modern styles like the Nehru jacket? Choose a suit that reflects your personality and makes you feel confident and comfortable.

The Kurta-Pajama

The kurta-pajama is a traditional Indian outfit that consists of a long tunic-style shirt known as a kurta, and loose-fitting pants known as pajamas. The kurta-pajama is a popular choice for casual events or everyday wear, and is often made of cotton or linen.

For a friend's birthday party, I wore a navy blue kurta-pajama with white embroidery. I paired it with brown leather sandals and a watch, and felt comfortable and stylish throughout the event.

Conclusion of Indian Men's Suit Name

Indian men's suit wear can be confusing, but by understanding the different styles and names, you can choose the right outfit for any occasion. Whether you prefer traditional styles like the sherwani or more modern styles like the Nehru jacket, there is a suit out there for everyone. So next time you're invited to a wedding or formal event, feel confident in your suit choice and rock it with style.
