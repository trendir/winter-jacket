Discover The Latest Iris Dubai Prices And Enjoy A Memorable Experience

Written by Oliver Nov 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you planning to have a fun night out in Dubai? Do you want to experience the city's vibrant nightlife scene? Iris Dubai is the perfect destination for you. However, one of the biggest concerns for many people is the cost. In this blog post, we will explore everything that you need to know about Iris Dubai prices, so you can plan your night out without any worries.

When it comes to nightlife in Dubai, it can be quite expensive. From drinks to food, the costs can quickly add up. This is why it's important to know the prices before you head out to your destination. You don't want to be surprised by the bill at the end of the night.

The target of Iris Dubai prices is to provide you with a detailed breakdown of the costs associated with a night out at this popular venue. This includes the prices of drinks, food, and any additional charges that you might incur.

In summary, Iris Dubai prices are an essential consideration if you plan to spend a night out in the city. Knowing the prices in advance can help you make informed decisions and avoid any unpleasant surprises.

Drinks Prices at Iris Dubai

When it comes to drinks, Iris Dubai has an impressive selection of cocktails, wines, and beers. Prices start at AED 70 for a glass of wine and AED 35 for a bottle of beer. Cocktails are priced at AED 75, while mocktails cost AED 50. If you want to try something different, you can opt for a signature cocktail priced at AED 105.

Personally, I had a great experience trying out the different cocktails at Iris Dubai. The bartenders are skilled and can create bespoke cocktails that cater to your tastes. You can also enjoy happy hour deals from 6 pm to 8 pm on weekdays. During this time, you can enjoy discounted drinks prices.

Food Prices at Iris Dubai

Iris Dubai is known for its delicious food menu that offers a mix of international and local cuisines. Prices for starters range from AED 45 to AED 85, while main courses cost between AED 95 and AED 260. You can also share platters with friends, which are priced at AED 195.

I have to say that the food at Iris Dubai is excellent. The dishes are beautifully presented and taste amazing. I particularly enjoyed the seafood platter, which had a variety of seafood options that were all cooked to perfection.

Additional Charges at Iris Dubai

It's worth noting that there are additional charges that you might incur when you visit Iris Dubai. For instance, you might be charged a minimum spend fee of AED 500 per table. This means that you have to spend a minimum of AED 500 on food and drinks to secure a table.

How to Book a Table at Iris Dubai

If you want to secure a table at Iris Dubai, you can make a reservation online or via phone. The process is straightforward and easy to follow. You can also request a table with a view or one that's close to the bar.

Conclusion of Iris Dubai Prices

Iris Dubai is a fantastic destination for anyone who wants to experience the city's nightlife scene. However, it's important to be aware of the prices before you visit. This blog post has provided you with a detailed breakdown of the prices associated with a night out at Iris Dubai. From drinks to food, you now know what to expect. So go ahead and plan your night out at Iris Dubai, and enjoy a memorable experience.
