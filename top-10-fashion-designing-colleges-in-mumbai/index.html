Top 10 Fashion Designing Colleges In Mumbai

Written by Oliver Sep 01, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you interested in pursuing a career in fashion designing and looking for the top 10 fashion designing colleges in Mumbai? If yes, then you have landed on the right page. Mumbai is known as the fashion capital of India, and it is home to some of the best fashion designing colleges in the country. Choosing the right college can be overwhelming, but this article will help you make an informed decision.

The fashion industry is highly competitive, and getting a degree from a reputable fashion designing college can boost your chances of success. However, finding the right college can be a daunting task. You may have several questions in your mind, such as which college is the best, what are the admission requirements, what is the fee structure, and many more. In this article, we will try to answer all your queries related to the top 10 fashion designing colleges in Mumbai.

Now, let's take a look at the top 10 fashion designing colleges in Mumbai and understand what makes them stand out from the rest.

Tier 1 Colleges

1. National Institute of Fashion Technology (NIFT)

NIFT is one of the most prestigious fashion designing colleges in India, and it has a branch in Mumbai as well. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fashion designing, fashion communication, textile design, and many more. The college has state-of-the-art infrastructure and experienced faculty members. The admission process is based on a written entrance exam followed by a personal interview.

2. Pearl Academy

Pearl Academy is another popular fashion designing college in Mumbai, and it is known for its industry-oriented curriculum. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fashion designing, fashion styling, fashion business management, and many more. The college has tie-ups with several leading fashion brands, which provides students with excellent internship and placement opportunities. The admission process is based on a written entrance exam followed by a personal interview.

3. Indian School of Design and Innovation (ISDI)

ISDI is a relatively new fashion designing college in Mumbai, but it has quickly gained popularity due to its innovative curriculum and industry collaborations. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fashion designing, fashion communication, fashion management, and many more. The college has tie-ups with several leading fashion brands, which provides students with excellent internship and placement opportunities. The admission process is based on a personal interview and portfolio review.

Tier 2 Colleges

4. SNDT Women's University

SNDT Women's University is a renowned women's university in Mumbai, and it offers several courses in fashion designing. The college has experienced faculty members and excellent infrastructure. The admission process is based on a written entrance exam followed by a personal interview.

5. Raffles Design International

Raffles Design International is a Singapore-based design college that has a branch in Mumbai as well. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fashion designing, fashion marketing, and management, and many more. ITM Institute of Design and Media is a design college in Mumbai, and it offers several courses in fashion designing, interior designing, and many more. Question and Answer

Q1. What is the eligibility criteria for admission to a fashion designing college in Mumbai?

A1. The eligibility criteria may vary from college to college, but in general, candidates must have passed 10+2 or an equivalent examination with a minimum of 50% marks. The admission process is based on a written entrance exam followed by a personal interview and portfolio review.

Q2. What are the career prospects after completing a fashion designing course?

A2. After completing a fashion designing course, you can work as a fashion designer, stylist, fashion journalist, fashion merchandiser, fashion photographer, and many more. The fashion industry is highly competitive, but if you have the right skills and knowledge, you can excel in this field.

Q3. What is the fee structure for a fashion designing course in Mumbai?

A3. The fee structure may vary from college to college, but in general, it can range from INR 50,000 to INR 5 lakh per annum.

Q4. How can I prepare for the entrance exam of a fashion designing college in Mumbai?

A4. You can prepare for the entrance exam by practicing previous years' question papers, taking mock tests, and improving your general knowledge and current affairs knowledge. You can also join coaching classes to get expert guidance.

Conclusion of Top 10 Fashion Designing Colleges in Mumbai

In conclusion, Mumbai has several reputed fashion designing colleges that offer excellent education and exposure to the fashion industry. Choosing the right college can be challenging, but with proper research and guidance, you can make an informed decision. We hope this article has helped you understand the top 10 fashion designing colleges in Mumbai and their admission process.
