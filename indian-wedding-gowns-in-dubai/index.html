The Different Styles of Indian Wedding Gowns in Dubai

Indian wedding gowns come in a variety of styles, each with its own unique features and cultural significance. Some of the popular styles of Indian wedding gowns in Dubai include:

Lehenga Gowns

A Lehenga gown is a fusion of a traditional Indian lehenga and a modern western gown. It consists of a flowing skirt and a fitted bodice, usually adorned with intricate embroidery or embellishments.

Anarkali Gowns

An Anarkali gown is a fusion of a traditional Indian Anarkali suit and a western gown. It consists of a long, flowing dress with a fitted bodice and a flared skirt, usually made of silk or chiffon.

Saree Gowns

A Saree gown is a fusion of a traditional Indian saree and a western gown. It consists of a draped saree with a fitted bodice and a flowing skirt, usually made of georgette or silk.

Where to Buy Indian Wedding Gowns in Dubai

There are several places in Dubai where you can buy Indian wedding gowns, including:

  • Bridal Boutiques: There are several bridal boutiques in Dubai that specialize in Indian wedding gowns, such as Esposa, The Bridal Showroom, and The Rack by Kachins.
  • Designer Stores: Many Indian designers have stores in Dubai, such as Manish Malhotra, Sabyasachi, and Anita Dongre.
  • Online Stores: There are several online stores that sell Indian wedding gowns in Dubai, such as Aza Fashions, Utsav Fashion, and Pernia's Pop-Up Shop.

Tips for Choosing the Perfect Indian Wedding Gown in Dubai

Here are some tips to keep in mind while shopping for your dream Indian wedding gown in Dubai:

  • Do your research beforehand and have an idea of what style and design you are looking for.
  • Take the time to try on different styles and designs to find the one that suits you best.
  • Consider the venue and theme of your wedding when choosing your gown.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for alterations or customizations to make the gown fit you perfectly.

Question and Answer

Q. How much does an Indian wedding gown cost in Dubai?

A. The cost of an Indian wedding gown in Dubai can range from AED 5,000 to AED 50,000, depending on the designer, style, and fabric.

Q. How long does it take to get an Indian wedding gown in Dubai?

A. It can take anywhere from 2 weeks to 6 months to get an Indian wedding gown in Dubai, depending on the designer and customization options.

Q. Can I rent an Indian wedding gown in Dubai?

A. Yes, there are several places in Dubai where you can rent Indian wedding gowns, such as The Bridal Showroom and Designer-24.

Q. What accessories should I wear with my Indian wedding gown in Dubai?

A. You can accessorize your Indian wedding gown with traditional Indian jewelry, such as a maang tikka, bangles, and earrings, as well as a veil or dupatta.

Conclusion of Indian Wedding Gowns in Dubai

Indian wedding gowns in Dubai offer a unique blend of traditional and modern styles, making them the perfect choice for brides who want to showcase their cultural heritage while also being fashionable and stylish. With so many options available, finding the perfect gown can be overwhelming, but with the tips and suggestions in this article, you are sure to find your dream dress.
