Best Men s Dress Shoes For Wide Feet A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Nov 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing shoes that pinch your feet or leave you with blisters after a long day at work? Finding the best men's dress shoes for wide feet can be a daunting task, but it doesn't have to be. In this guide, we'll explore the pain points of men with wide feet when it comes to dress shoes and provide you with some options to consider.

Before we dive into the best men's dress shoes for wide feet, let's first define what we mean by "wide feet." Generally, men with wide feet have a foot width of 3.9 inches or more. If you find that regular shoes feel tight around the ball of your foot or the toes, you likely have wide feet.

Now, let's get into the best men's dress shoes for wide feet. We'll cover the types of shoes to consider, the materials to look for, and some brands that specialize in wide-width shoes.

Loafers

Loafers are a great option for men with wide feet because they have a wider opening and offer more room for your feet to breathe. Additionally, they're versatile and can be dressed up or down. A personal experience I had with loafers was when I wore a pair of Cole Haan loafers to a wedding. I was on my feet for hours, but my feet never felt cramped or uncomfortable.

Oxfords

Oxfords are a classic dress shoe that can elevate any outfit. When looking for Oxfords for wide feet, be sure to look for ones that have a wider toe box and a thicker sole. You'll want to avoid shoes that are too narrow or have a pointed toe. A brand that specializes in wide-width Oxfords is Allen Edmonds. Their Park Avenue shoe is a popular option for men with wide feet.

Materials to Look For

When it comes to materials, look for shoes made from high-quality leather or suede. These materials will stretch and conform to your feet over time, providing you with a comfortable fit. Additionally, leather and suede are durable and will last you for years to come. Be sure to avoid shoes made from synthetic materials, as they tend to be less breathable and less forgiving when it comes to fit.

Tips for Finding the Right Fit

When trying on dress shoes for wide feet, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure the shoe is wide enough. You should be able to wiggle your toes and have some room around the ball of your foot. Second, pay attention to the arch support. Shoes with good arch support will help prevent foot fatigue and provide you with added comfort. Finally, don't be afraid to try on multiple sizes and styles. Finding the right shoe can take some trial and error.

Brands that Specialize in Wide-Width Shoes

There are several brands that specialize in shoes for men with wide feet. Some of our favorites include:

  • New Balance
  • Rockport
  • Clarks
  • Cole Haan
  • Allen Edmonds

Each of these brands offers a wide selection of dress shoes that cater to men with wider feet. They also offer a range of sizes, so you're sure to find something that fits.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear dress shoes if I have wide feet?

A: Yes, absolutely. There are plenty of dress shoe options available for men with wide feet. Look for shoes that have a wider toe box and a thicker sole for added comfort.

Q: What materials should I look for in dress shoes for wide feet?

A: Look for shoes made from high-quality leather or suede. These materials will stretch and conform to your feet over time, providing you with a comfortable fit.

Q: How can I find the right fit when shopping for dress shoes for wide feet?

A: Pay attention to the width of the shoe, the arch support, and don't be afraid to try on multiple sizes and styles.

Q: What are some brands that specialize in wide-width shoes?

A: Some of our favorites include New Balance, Rockport, Clarks, Cole Haan, and Allen Edmonds.

Conclusion of Best Men's Dress Shoes for Wide Feet

When it comes to finding the best men's dress shoes for wide feet, it's all about finding the right fit. Look for shoes with a wider toe box and a thicker sole, made from high-quality materials like leather or suede. Don't be afraid to try on multiple sizes and styles, and consider brands that specialize in wide-width shoes. With these tips in mind, you'll be sure to find a pair of dress shoes that are both stylish and comfortable.
