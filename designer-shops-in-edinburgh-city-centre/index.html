Discover The Best Designer Shops In Edinburgh City Centre

Written by Petter Jun 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a fashion enthusiast looking to explore designer shops in Edinburgh city centre? Do you want to upgrade your wardrobe with stylish outfits from high-end brands? Edinburgh has a wealth of designer shops that cater to every fashion taste and budget. From luxury boutiques to high-street stores, there's something for everyone. However, finding the right designer shops can be overwhelming, especially if you're new to the city or don't know where to start. In this post, we'll guide you through the best designer shops in Edinburgh city centre and help you make informed choices.

Designer shops in Edinburgh city centre are a haven for fashion lovers who want to stay on-trend and look fashionable. However, with so many options to choose from, it can be challenging to find the right store that caters to your style and budget. Moreover, designer shops can be expensive, and you want to make sure that you're getting value for your money. In this blog post, we'll provide you with useful information about designer shops in Edinburgh city centre, including their location, products, and price range.

If you're looking for designer shops in Edinburgh city centre, you're in luck. The city boasts of a vibrant fashion scene, with a wide range of designer shops that cater to every fashion taste and budget. Whether you're looking for luxury brands or affordable high-street stores, Edinburgh has it all. From Princes Street to George Street, there are plenty of designer shops to explore in the city centre. In the next section, we'll highlight some of the best designer shops in Edinburgh city centre and what they offer.

Harvey Nichols

Harvey Nichols is a luxury department store located on St. Andrew Square in the heart of Edinburgh city centre. The store stocks a wide range of high-end brands, including Alexander McQueen, Balenciaga, and Gucci. Harvey Nichols is known for its exceptional customer service and personal shopping experience. The store also has a beauty hall, restaurant, and bar, making it a one-stop-shop for all your fashion and lifestyle needs.

Jenners

Jenners is a historic department store located on Princes Street, Edinburgh's main shopping street. The store has been in operation since 1838 and stocks a wide range of designer brands, including Ted Baker, Tommy Hilfiger, and Radley. Jenners is known for its impressive architecture, including a beautiful stained-glass dome and marble staircase. The store also has a restaurant and a champagne bar, making it a perfect place to unwind after a shopping spree.

Victoria Street

Victoria Street is a picturesque street located in Edinburgh's Old Town, known for its colorful buildings and boutique shops. The street is home to several fashion boutiques, including Godiva Boutique, which stocks a range of vintage-inspired clothing and accessories. Other shops on Victoria Street include Walker Slater, which specializes in tweed clothing, and Miss Katie Cupcake, which sells retro-inspired clothing and accessories.

Grassmarket

Grassmarket is a vibrant area located in Edinburgh's Old Town, known for its independent shops, pubs, and restaurants. The area is home to several fashion boutiques, including Armstrong's Vintage Emporium, which stocks a range of vintage clothing and accessories. Other shops in the area include Totty Rocks, which specializes in bespoke dresses, and Ladyboss Collective, which sells sustainable and ethical clothing.

Conclusion of Designer Shops in Edinburgh City Centre

Edinburgh city centre is a fashion lover's paradise, with a wide range of designer shops to explore. From luxury department stores to independent boutiques, there's something for everyone. Whether you're looking for high-end brands or affordable fashion, Edinburgh has it all.
