Different Types Of Collars On Men s Dress Shirts

Written by Christine Oct 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same type of dress shirt collar every day? Do you want to switch up your style and experiment with different types of collars? Look no further, as we will be discussing the various types of collars on men's dress shirts and how they can enhance your overall look.

Have you ever felt uncomfortable or restricted while wearing a dress shirt? The culprit may be the collar. Different types of collars can affect the overall fit and comfort of a dress shirt, and choosing the wrong type for your body type can be a frustrating experience.

The purpose of this article is to provide a comprehensive guide on the different types of collars on men's dress shirts, their target audience, and how to style them to achieve a polished look.

In summary, different types of collars on men's dress shirts can greatly impact your overall appearance and comfort. It's important to know which collar is best suited for your body type and style preferences to achieve the perfect fit and look.

1. Spread Collar

As someone who has a larger neck, I often struggle to find dress shirts that fit comfortably without feeling constricted around the neck area. That's where the spread collar comes in. This collar type is characterized by the distance between the collar points, which are spread wide apart. It's a versatile collar that can be worn with or without a tie, making it perfect for both formal and casual occasions.

For a formal look, pair a spread collar dress shirt with a classic tie and suit. For a more casual look, leave the top button undone and roll up the sleeves.

2. Button-Down Collar

The button-down collar is a classic and timeless collar style that is perfect for a preppy or casual look. It is characterized by small buttons that hold down the collar points, preventing them from flapping in the wind.

I personally love wearing button-down collar dress shirts with a pair of chinos or jeans for a smart-casual look. It's also a great option for those who want to wear a tie without having to worry about the collar points getting in the way.

3. Point Collar

The point collar is a sharp and classic collar style that is perfect for those with a slim or narrow face. It is characterized by collar points that are close together, creating a streamlined look.

This collar style is best suited for formal occasions when paired with a classic tie and suit. It's also a great option for those who want to elongate their face, as the collar points draw the eye downwards.

4. Club Collar

The club collar is a unique and stylish collar type that is characterized by rounded collar points. It was popularized in the early 1900s and has since made a comeback in modern fashion.

This collar type is best suited for those with a round or square face, as the rounded collar points soften the sharp angles of the face. It's a great option for those who want to add a touch of personality to their outfit without going too over-the-top.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a tie with a button-down collar dress shirt?

A: Yes, you can wear a tie with a button-down collar dress shirt. However, make sure to choose a tie that is not too wide, as the collar points may get in the way.

Q: What collar type is best suited for a larger neck?

A: The spread collar is best suited for a larger neck, as the wider collar points provide more room and comfort around the neck area.

Q: Can I wear a club collar dress shirt to a formal occasion?

A: While the club collar is a stylish and unique collar type, it may not be appropriate for formal occasions. Stick to classic collar types such as the point or spread collar for formal events.

Q: How do I know which collar type is best suited for my face shape?

A: Generally, those with a slim or narrow face should opt for a point collar, while those with a round or square face should go for a club collar. However, feel free to experiment with different collar types to find the one that suits you best.

Conclusion of Different Types of Collars on Men's Dress Shirts

In conclusion, the different types of collars on men's dress shirts can greatly impact your overall style and comfort. From the classic point collar to the unique club collar, there is a collar type for every occasion and body type. It's important to experiment with different collar types to find the one that suits you best and enhances your overall look.
