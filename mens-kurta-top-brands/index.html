Top Mens Kurta Brands For A Classic And Comfortable Look

Are you tired of wearing the same old boring clothes every day? Do you want to add a touch of ethnicity to your wardrobe? Look no further than mens kurta tops. These tops are not only comfortable and stylish, but they also add a touch of traditional elegance to your look. However, with so many brands available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this post, we will discuss the top mens kurta brands that you can consider for a classic and comfortable look.

When it comes to mens kurta tops, there are a few pain points that everyone faces. Firstly, it can be challenging to find the right size, as kurta tops come in various sizes and fits. Secondly, it can be tough to find a brand that offers quality fabric and durability. Lastly, finding a brand that offers a variety of designs and patterns can be a challenge.

Top Mens Kurta Brands and Their Target

Let's start with the top mens kurta brands that you can consider for a classic and comfortable look:

1. Manyavar

Manyavar is one of the most popular mens kurta brands in India. Their kurta tops are made of high-quality fabrics such as silk, cotton, and linen. Manyavar offers a wide variety of designs, patterns, and colors to choose from, making it a go-to brand for every occasion. Their target is for men who want to add a touch of ethnicity to their wardrobe while keeping it comfortable and fashionable.

2. Fabindia

Fabindia is a brand that offers a mix of traditional and modern designs. Their kurta tops are made of high-quality cotton and linen fabrics, making them comfortable and breathable. Fabindia offers a wide range of designs, from plain solid colors to intricate patterns, making it a versatile brand for every occasion. Their target is for men who want a blend of traditional and contemporary designs.

3. Raymond

Raymond is a brand that needs no introduction. They are known for their quality fabrics and durability. Their kurta tops are made of premium quality cotton and linen fabrics, making them comfortable and long-lasting. Raymond offers a range of designs, from plain solid colors to subtle patterns, making it a go-to brand for every occasion. Their target is for men who prioritize quality and durability over everything else.

4. W for Men

W for Men is a relatively new brand but has gained popularity in a short span of time. Their kurta tops are made of high-quality fabrics such as cotton and silk, making them comfortable and stylish. W for Men offers a wide range of designs, from plain solid colors to intricate patterns, making it a versatile brand for every occasion. Their target is for men who want to experiment with their look while keeping it comfortable and stylish.

5. Manyavar Mohey

Manyavar Mohey is a sub-brand of Manyavar that offers a range of ethnic wear for women. Their kurta tops are made of high-quality fabrics such as silk, cotton, and linen, making them comfortable and elegant. Manyavar Mohey offers a wide range of designs, from simple and elegant to intricate and heavy, making it a go-to brand for every occasion. Their target is for men who want to match their attire with their partner's ethnic wear.

How to Choose the Right Mens Kurta Brand?

Choosing the right mens kurta brand can be a challenge, but here are a few factors that you can consider:

1. Fabric

Choose a brand that offers high-quality fabrics such as cotton, linen, and silk. These fabrics are comfortable, breathable, and long-lasting.

2. Design and Patterns

Choose a brand that offers a wide range of designs and patterns to choose from. This will give you the freedom to experiment with your look and match your attire with the occasion.

3. Size and Fit

Choose a brand that offers a variety of sizes and fits. This will ensure that you find the perfect fit and look your best.
