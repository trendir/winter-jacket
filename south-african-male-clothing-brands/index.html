ALC Man is another South African male clothing brand that's worth checking out. They offer a range of clothing options for men, including t-shirts, jackets, and pants. What sets ALC Man apart from other brands is their focus on modern, minimalist designs. 3. Simon and Mary

Simon and Mary is a South African male clothing brand that specializes in hats and accessories. They offer a range of hats, including fedoras, beanies, and caps, as well as scarves and gloves. What sets Simon and Mary apart from other brands is their focus on quality and craftsmanship. Their hats are made using traditional techniques and high-quality materials, ensuring that they last for years to come.

Personally, I love Simon and Mary's range of fedoras. They're perfect for adding some sophistication to any outfit, and they're incredibly comfortable to wear. Plus, their hats are made using ethically sourced materials, ensuring that they're both stylish and sustainable.

2Bop is a Johannesburg-based clothing brand that specializes in streetwear-inspired clothing for men. They offer a range of clothing options, including t-shirts, hoodies, and jackets. What sets 2Bop apart from other brands is their focus on South African culture and aesthetics.
