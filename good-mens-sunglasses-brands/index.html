Top Men s Sunglasses Brands For Style And Protection

Written by Frank Jun 08, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Summer is just around the corner and it's time to start thinking about protecting your eyes from harmful UV rays. But finding a good pair of men's sunglasses can be overwhelming with so many options out there. That's why we've put together a list of the top men's sunglasses brands that offer both style and protection.

When it comes to buying sunglasses, there are a few pain points that men often face. They want sunglasses that not only look good but also provide protection from the sun's harmful rays. They want sunglasses that are durable and long-lasting, but also affordable. And they want sunglasses that can be worn for a variety of occasions, from casual outings to formal events.

If you're in the market for a new pair of sunglasses, you're in luck. There are plenty of good men's sunglasses brands to choose from that meet all of these criteria.

In this article, we'll be summarizing the main points of the top men's sunglasses brands and related keywords, without explicitly using a subheading for the conclusion.

Ray-Ban

Ray-Ban is a classic brand that has been around for over 80 years. Their iconic Aviator and Wayfarer styles are timeless and can be worn by men of all ages. Not only do they look good, but they also offer 100% UV protection. Ray-Bans are also known for their durability, so you know you're getting a quality product that will last. I personally own a pair of Ray-Ban Wayfarers and they're my go-to sunglasses for any occasion.

Oakley

Oakley is a brand that is known for its sporty and innovative designs. Their sunglasses are perfect for men who lead an active lifestyle and need sunglasses that can keep up with them. Oakley's lenses are made with their patented Prizm technology, which enhances color and contrast in all types of conditions. They also offer 100% UV protection and are incredibly durable. I've owned a pair of Oakleys for years and they've held up through countless hikes and outdoor adventures.

Polarized Sunglasses

Polarized sunglasses are a must-have for men who spend a lot of time outdoors. Polarized lenses reduce glare and improve visual clarity, making them ideal for activities like fishing and boating. Some good men's sunglasses brands that offer polarized options include Maui Jim, Costa Del Mar, and Smith Optics.

How to Choose the Right Sunglasses for Your Face Shape

When it comes to choosing the right pair of sunglasses, it's important to consider your face shape. Different styles of sunglasses flatter different face shapes. For example, aviators look great on men with round faces, while square frames complement men with oval faces. If you're not sure what your face shape is, there are plenty of online guides that can help you figure it out.

Warby Parker

Warby Parker is a brand that offers stylish and affordable glasses and sunglasses. Their sunglasses start at just $95 and come in a variety of trendy styles. Not only are they affordable, but they also offer 100% UV protection. And if you need prescription sunglasses, Warby Parker has you covered. They offer prescription lenses and can even help you find the perfect pair of sunglasses for your face shape.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the difference between polarized and non-polarized sunglasses?

A: Polarized sunglasses have a special filter that blocks glare from reflective surfaces like water, snow, and glass. Non-polarized sunglasses do not have this filter and can actually make glare worse in some situations.

Q: Can I wear the same pair of sunglasses for all occasions?

A: It depends on the style of sunglasses you choose. Classic styles like aviators and wayfarers are versatile and can be worn for a variety of occasions. Sportier styles like wraparound sunglasses are better suited for outdoor activities.

Q: Are expensive sunglasses worth the investment?

A: It depends on your budget and needs. More expensive sunglasses often have better quality lenses and frames that are more durable. However, there are plenty of affordable sunglasses brands that offer good quality and protection.

Q: Can I wear sunglasses if I have prescription glasses?

A: Yes, you can get prescription sunglasses that are made to your specific vision needs. Many good men's sunglasses brands like Warby Parker offer prescription options.

Conclusion of Good Men's Sunglasses Brands

There are plenty of good men's sunglasses brands out there that offer both style and protection. From classic brands like Ray-Ban to sporty brands like Oakley, there's a brand and style for every man. When choosing sunglasses, it's important to consider your face shape, the activities you'll be doing, and your budget. With the right pair of sunglasses, you can look great and protect your eyes from the sun's harmful rays.
