Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if a shirt is too tight?

A: If a shirt is too tight, you may notice that it feels uncomfortable, restricts your movement, or causes creases in the fabric.

Q: How do I know if a shirt is too loose?

A: If a shirt is too loose, you may notice excess fabric around the waist, chest, or shoulders. It may also look baggy or unflattering.

Q: How do I know if the sleeves are the right length?

A: The sleeves should end at your wrist bone. If they're too short, they may ride up when you move your arms. If they're too long, they may bunch up or look sloppy.

Q: How do I know if the neck size is right?

A: The neck size should be snug but not too tight. You should be able to fit one or two fingers between your neck and the collar. If the collar feels tight or uncomfortable, it may be too small.
