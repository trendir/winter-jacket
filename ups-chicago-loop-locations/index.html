Here are some of the main points you should know about UPS Chicago Loop locations:

UPS Stores in the Loop

If you're looking for a UPS store in the Loop, you can find several options that are conveniently located near major landmarks and transportation hubs. For instance, the UPS Store at 180 N Wacker Dr is just a few steps away from the Chicago Riverwalk and the Ogilvie Transportation Center, while the one at 400 E Randolph St is close to Millennium Park and the Lakefront Trail. These stores offer a variety of services such as printing, copying, mailbox rental, notary, and passport photos, in addition to shipping.

UPS Access Point Locations

If you're not able to receive packages at your home or office, or you prefer to pick them up at a nearby location, you can use UPS Access Point locations. These are retail stores, convenience stores, or other businesses that partner with UPS to provide a secure and convenient delivery option. You can choose the Access Point that is most convenient for you and receive a notification when your package is ready for pickup. Some of the Access Points in the Chicago Loop include Walgreens, CVS, 7-Eleven, and Michaels.

UPS Drop Boxes

If you need to send a package after hours or you don't have time to visit a UPS store, you can use a UPS drop box. These are self-service kiosks that allow you to drop off your package and have it shipped the next business day. You can find UPS drop boxes in various locations throughout the city, including the Chicago Loop. To locate a drop box near you, you can use the UPS website or mobile app.

How to Prepare Your Package for Shipping

Before you visit a UPS Chicago Loop location, make sure you've prepared your package properly. This can help ensure that your shipment arrives safely and on time. Here are some tips:

  • Use a sturdy box or envelope that is appropriate for the size and weight of your item
  • Wrap fragile items with bubble wrap or foam peanuts
  • Securely seal the box or envelope with packing tape
  • Attach the shipping label and fill out the necessary forms
  • Choose the shipping method that best suits your needs and budget
  • Track your package online to monitor its status and estimated delivery date
