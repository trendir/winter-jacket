Exploring Fashion Designing Subjects In Nift

Written by Daniel Jun 01, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you dreaming of a career in fashion designing? Are you curious about the subjects you will study in NIFT? Fashion designing is a fascinating field that offers a wide range of career opportunities. However, pursuing a degree in fashion design requires a deep understanding of the subjects taught in NIFT. In this article, we will explore the fashion designing subjects in NIFT and help you understand what you can expect from this exciting course.

Studying fashion designing subjects in NIFT can be challenging, especially for students who are new to the field. It is essential to have a clear understanding of the course objectives and goals to succeed in this field.

NIFT offers a comprehensive course in fashion design that covers different subjects related to the fashion industry. The course aims to provide students with practical and theoretical knowledge of fashion designing, including the latest trends, technologies, and techniques used in the industry.

In this article, we will cover the main fashion designing subjects taught in NIFT, including fashion illustration, textiles, fashion merchandising, fashion communication, and fashion marketing. We will provide a detailed overview of each subject, its objectives, and its importance in the fashion industry.

The Art of Fashion Illustration

One of the essential subjects taught in NIFT is fashion illustration. It is the art of depicting fashion designs through sketches and drawings. The primary objective of this subject is to help students develop their drawing skills and create unique designs that reflect their creativity and vision. Fashion illustration is an essential tool for fashion designers, as it helps them communicate their ideas to clients, manufacturers, and other stakeholders in the industry.

Personally, I was fascinated by fashion illustration when I first joined NIFT. I had always loved to draw, and the opportunity to draw fashion designs was a dream come true. I learned different techniques, such as pencil shading, watercolor, and digital illustration, which helped me create stunning designs that caught the attention of my professors and classmates.

The World of Textiles

Textiles are an integral part of the fashion industry, and understanding their properties and characteristics is essential for fashion designers. In NIFT, students learn about different types of fabrics, their manufacturing processes, and their unique properties. This subject helps students choose the right fabric for their designs and understand how fabrics can affect the fit, drape, and overall look of a garment.

During my time in NIFT, I learned about different fabrics, such as cotton, silk, wool, and polyester. I also learned about the different weaves, prints, and finishes used in the industry. This knowledge helped me create designs that were both aesthetically pleasing and comfortable to wear.

The Business of Fashion Merchandising

Fashion merchandising is a subject that focuses on the business side of the fashion industry. It covers topics such as retail management, buying and sourcing, and supply chain management. This subject is essential for students who want to pursue a career in fashion retail or entrepreneurship.

During my studies in NIFT, I learned about the different aspects of fashion merchandising, such as visual merchandising, inventory management, and pricing strategies. This knowledge helped me understand how the fashion industry works and how to create a successful fashion business.

The Art of Fashion Communication and Marketing

Fashion communication and marketing are essential subjects that teach students how to promote fashion brands and products. This subject covers topics such as advertising, public relations, social media, and event management. It is essential for students who want to pursue a career in fashion marketing or fashion communication.

During my studies in NIFT, I learned about the different marketing channels used in the fashion industry, such as print ads, television commercials, and social media campaigns. I also learned about the importance of branding and how to create a brand identity that resonates with the target audience.

Career Opportunities in Fashion Designing

Fashion designing is a vast field that offers a wide range of career opportunities. Some of the popular career options for fashion design graduates include fashion designer, fashion merchandiser, fashion marketer, fashion buyer, and fashion stylist. Graduates can also pursue careers in fashion journalism, fashion photography, and fashion event management.

Question and Answer

Q. What is the duration of the fashion design course in NIFT?

A. The fashion design course in NIFT is a four-year undergraduate program.

Q. Is it necessary to have drawing skills to pursue fashion designing?

A. While having drawing skills can be helpful, it is not a prerequisite for pursuing fashion designing. NIFT provides students with the necessary training and resources to develop their drawing skills.

Q. What is the average salary of a fashion designer?

A. The average salary of a fashion designer in India is around Rs. 4 lakhs per annum. However, the salary can vary depending on the experience, skills, and location of the designer.

Q. What are the job prospects for fashion design graduates?

A. Fashion design graduates can find job opportunities in different sectors of the fashion industry, such as fashion retail, fashion marketing, fashion buying, and fashion styling.

Conclusion of Fashion Designing Subjects in NIFT

NIFT is a prestigious institution that offers a comprehensive course in fashion design. The course covers different subjects related to the fashion industry, such as fashion illustration, textiles, fashion merchandising, fashion communication, and fashion marketing. By pursuing a degree in fashion design, students can gain practical and theoretical knowledge of the fashion industry and pursue exciting career opportunities in this field.
