What Men s Suits Are In Style A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Aug 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old suit to every formal event? Do you want to stand out in a stylish and trendy suit that showcases your personality? Look no further, because we have got you covered with the latest trends in men's suits. In this guide, we will walk you through what men's suits are in style and how to rock them like a pro.

It can be frustrating to keep up with the latest styles and trends in men's suits. With so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one that suits your body type, style, and occasion. But don't worry, we understand your pain points and are here to help you find the perfect suit that makes you look and feel confident.

So, what men's suits are in style? The answer is simple - classic, timeless, and versatile suits are still in style. However, with a modern twist. The latest trends include slim-fit suits, colorful suits, double-breasted suits, and patterned suits. Let's dive deeper into each trend and see how you can incorporate them into your wardrobe.

Slim-Fit Suits

Slim-fit suits have been in style for a while now and show no signs of going out of fashion. These suits have a narrower cut that follows the lines of your body, creating a sleek and modern look. They are perfect for leaner body types and make you look taller and slimmer. You can choose from a variety of colors and fabrics, ranging from classic black to bold blues and greens.

Personally, I love wearing slim-fit suits to formal events. They make me feel confident and stylish, and I always receive compliments on my outfit. I pair them with a crisp white shirt, a skinny tie, and a pair of classic leather shoes. The key is to keep it simple and let the suit do the talking.

Colorful Suits

Gone are the days when black and navy were the only acceptable colors for suits. Nowadays, men are embracing bold and bright hues, such as red, yellow, and green. These suits are perfect for summer weddings or outdoor events and make you stand out in a sea of black suits.

I recently attended a friend's wedding wearing a bright blue suit, and I have never felt more confident. The key is to choose a color that complements your skin tone and pair it with neutral accessories, such as a white shirt and a pair of brown shoes. Trust me, you won't regret adding a colorful suit to your wardrobe.

Double-Breasted Suits

Double-breasted suits are making a comeback in the fashion industry. These suits have two rows of buttons on the front, creating a sophisticated and elegant look. They are perfect for formal events such as weddings and black-tie galas.

I recently bought a double-breasted suit in a classic black color and paired it with a white shirt and a bow tie. I felt like a million bucks and received compliments all night. The key is to choose a well-tailored suit that fits you perfectly, as a poorly-fitted suit can ruin the entire look.

Patterned Suits

If you want to add some personality to your outfit, patterned suits are the way to go. These suits have unique designs and prints that make you stand out in a crowd. You can choose from a variety of patterns, such as stripes, checks, and florals.

I recently wore a striped suit to a fashion event, and I received so many compliments on my outfit. The key is to choose a pattern that complements your body type and pair it with neutral accessories, such as a white shirt and black shoes. Remember, the suit should be the focal point of your outfit.

Question and Answer

Q. Can I wear a colorful suit to a job interview?

A. It depends on the company culture and dress code. If you are applying for a creative position or a startup company, a colorful suit can showcase your personality and creativity. However, if the company has a strict dress code, it's best to stick to a classic navy or black suit.

Q. Can I wear a patterned suit to a wedding?

A. Yes, you can. However, make sure the pattern is not too bold or distracting. Stick to subtle patterns such as stripes or checks and pair it with neutral accessories. You don't want to steal the bride and groom's thunder.

Q. How do I choose the right fit for my suit?

A. The right fit is crucial when it comes to suits. A well-tailored suit should fit you perfectly, without any sagging or pulling. Make sure the sleeves and pants are the right length, and the jacket fits snugly around your shoulders. If you are unsure about your size, visit a tailor who can guide you through the process.

Q. Can I wear sneakers with a suit?

A. Yes, you can. However, make sure the sneakers are clean and minimalist in design. Stick to classic colors such as white, black, or grey, and pair them with a casual suit, such as a linen or cotton suit. It's a great way to add a modern touch to your outfit.

Conclusion of What Men's Suits are in Style

In conclusion, men's suits are still in style, but with a modern twist. You can choose from a variety of styles, colors, and patterns that showcase your personality and style. Remember, the key is to choose a well-tailored suit that fits you perfectly and pair it with neutral accessories that complement your outfit. So, go ahead and experiment with different styles and trends, and find the perfect suit that makes you look and feel confident.
