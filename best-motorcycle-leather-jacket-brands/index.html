Top Motorcycle Leather Jacket Brands

When it comes to purchasing a leather jacket for motorcycle riding, there are a few brands that stand out from the rest. These brands have a reputation for producing high-quality products that offer both style and protection. Let's take a closer look at some of the top motorcycle leather jacket brands.

1. Schott NYC

Schott NYC is one of the most iconic leather jacket brands in the world. Founded in 1913, the company has been producing high-quality leather jackets for over 100 years. Schott NYC is best known for its Perfecto jacket, which was made famous by Marlon Brando in the 1950s. Today, the company produces a wide variety of leather jackets, all of which are made in the USA.

2. Alpinestars

Alpinestars is a leading manufacturer of motorcycle gear, including leather jackets. The company was founded in 1963 and has since become a staple in the motorcycle community. Alpinestars produces a wide range of leather jackets, from classic styles to more modern designs. All of their jackets are made with high-quality materials and are designed to offer maximum protection while riding.

3. Dainese

Dainese is an Italian company that specializes in motorcycle gear, including leather jackets. The company was founded in 1972 and has since become a leader in the industry. Dainese's leather jackets are known for their unique designs and high-quality materials. They offer a range of styles, from classic to more modern looks.

4. Roland Sands Design

Roland Sands Design is a California-based company that produces high-quality motorcycle gear, including leather jackets. The company was founded in 2005 by former professional motorcycle racer Roland Sands. Their leather jackets are known for their unique designs and high-quality materials.

Why Invest in a High-Quality Motorcycle Leather Jacket?

Investing in a high-quality motorcycle leather jacket is essential for any rider. A good leather jacket will not only keep you safe while riding, but it will also provide you with comfort and style. Here are a few reasons why you should invest in a high-quality motorcycle leather jacket:

1. Protection

A high-quality leather jacket will provide you with protection while riding. Leather is a durable and abrasion-resistant material that can help protect you in the event of an accident. A good leather jacket will also have impact-absorbing padding to protect your elbows, shoulders, and back.

2. Comfort

A good leather jacket will keep you comfortable while riding. Leather is a breathable material that will keep you warm in colder temperatures and cool in warmer weather. A well-fitted leather jacket will also allow for a full range of motion while riding.

3. Style

A high-quality leather jacket will also provide you with style while riding. A good leather jacket can make you look and feel like a true motorcycle enthusiast. With so many different styles and designs to choose from, you're sure to find a leather jacket that fits your personal style.

How to Choose the Right Motorcycle Leather Jacket?

Now that you know why it's important to invest in a high-quality motorcycle leather jacket, let's take a look at how to choose the right one for you. Here are a few things to consider when choosing a motorcycle leather jacket:

1. Style

There are many different styles of motorcycle leather jackets to choose from, including classic designs like the Perfecto and more modern looks. Consider what style fits your personal taste and what will look best on you.

2. Material

When choosing a motorcycle leather jacket, you want to make sure you're investing in a high-quality material. Look for jackets made from genuine leather, as this will provide the best protection and durability.

3. Fit

It's important to choose a motorcycle leather jacket that fits you well. A well-fitted jacket will provide the best protection and comfort while riding. Consider trying on different sizes and styles to find the perfect fit.

How to Care for Your Motorcycle Leather Jacket?

Once you've invested in a high-quality motorcycle leather jacket, it's important to take care of it properly. Here are a few tips for caring for your motorcycle leather jacket:

1. Clean it regularly

You should clean your leather jacket regularly to keep it looking and feeling its best. Use a leather cleaner and conditioner to keep the leather soft and supple.

2. Store it properly

When you're not wearing your leather jacket, make sure to store it properly. Hang it on a padded hanger in a cool, dry place.

3. Avoid getting it wet

Leather jackets should be kept dry to avoid damage. If your jacket gets wet, hang it up to dry at room temperature.
