The Coolest Shops In Columbus A Guide To Shopping In Ohio

Written by Daniel Jul 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a shopaholic looking for some retail therapy in Columbus? Do you want to explore the coolest shops in town but not sure where to start? Look no further! In this post, we’ve put together a guide to help you find some of the coolest shops in Columbus.

If you’re tired of the same-old chain stores, you’re not alone. Many people are looking for unique, eclectic shops that offer something different. Columbus has a thriving small business scene, with many independent stores that offer one-of-a-kind items you won’t find anywhere else. But with so many options, it can be overwhelming to figure out where to start.

This guide is targeted towards anyone who is interested in discovering the coolest shops in Columbus. Whether you’re a local or just visiting, you’ll find something new and exciting to explore.

In this post, we’ll take a look at some of the coolest shops in Columbus, from vintage clothing stores to artisanal bakeries. We’ll cover the best places to find unique gifts, trendy fashion, and handmade crafts. If you’re looking for a shopping experience that’s off the beaten path, you’ve come to the right place.

Experience the Thrill of Vintage Shopping at Rag-O-Rama

As someone who loves vintage clothing, I was thrilled to discover Rag-O-Rama. This store is a treasure trove of vintage clothing, accessories, and home decor. You’ll find everything from 80s prom dresses to 60s mod jackets, all at affordable prices.

What sets Rag-O-Rama apart from other vintage stores is the quality of the items. Everything is in great condition, and you’re sure to find something unique that fits your style. The staff is friendly and knowledgeable, and they’re always happy to help you find what you’re looking for.

Indulge Your Sweet Tooth at Pistacia Vera

If you’re a fan of French pastries and baked goods, you won’t want to miss Pistacia Vera. This bakery is a Columbus institution, known for its delicious macarons, croissants, and tarts.

What makes Pistacia Vera stand out is the quality of its ingredients. Everything is made from scratch using locally sourced, organic ingredients. You can taste the difference in every bite. The bakery also has a cozy atmosphere, with plenty of seating for enjoying your treats.

Find Unique Gifts at Wholly Craft

Wholly Craft is a must-visit for anyone looking for unique, handmade gifts. This store features a wide variety of items, from jewelry to home decor to baby clothes.

Everything in the store is made by independent artists and designers, so you’re sure to find something that’s truly one-of-a-kind. The staff is also incredibly friendly and helpful, and they’ll be happy to help you find the perfect gift for any occasion.

Learn a New Skill at Craftin’ Outlaws

If you’re interested in learning a new craft, be sure to check out Craftin’ Outlaws. This store offers a wide variety of workshops and classes, from knitting to screen printing to soap making.

The classes are taught by local artists and designers, so you’re getting top-notch instruction. And the store itself is a great place to browse for unique handmade items.

Conclusion of Coolest Shops in Columbus

Columbus has a lot to offer when it comes to shopping. Whether you’re looking for vintage clothing, artisanal baked goods, or handmade crafts, you’re sure to find something unique and exciting in this city.

So next time you’re in Columbus, be sure to check out some of these coolest shops. You won’t be disappointed!

A: Prices vary depending on the store, but many of these shops offer affordable options.

A: Yes! These shops offer a wide variety of items, so you’re sure to find something for everyone on your list.
