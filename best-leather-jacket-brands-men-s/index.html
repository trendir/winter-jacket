Best Leather Jacket Brands For Men

Written by Bardi Jul 22, 2023 · 5 min read

Leather jackets are timeless pieces of clothing that can elevate any outfit. They are versatile, durable, and can be worn in any season. But with so many brands and styles to choose from, it can be overwhelming to find the perfect one. In this article, we'll explore the best leather jacket brands for men and help you find the one that suits your style and budget.

When it comes to leather jackets, the most common pain points men face are finding the right fit, style, and quality. With so many options available, it can be challenging to find a leather jacket that ticks all the boxes. Additionally, there is always the concern of spending a significant amount of money on a jacket that may not last long or go out of style quickly.

The target of this article is to help men find the best leather jacket brand that suits their needs and preferences. We will provide an overview of the top brands, their style offerings, and the quality of their products.

In summary, this article will explore the best leather jacket brands for men and provide insights into their styles, quality, and affordability.

1. Schott NYC

Schott NYC is a brand that has been around since 1913 and is known for its high-quality leather jackets. Their jackets are made in the USA and are known for their durability and classic styles. The brand offers a wide range of jackets, from the classic Perfecto to the more modern Café Racer.

Personally, I own a Schott NYC Perfecto jacket, and I can attest to its quality and durability. It's been my go-to jacket for years and still looks as good as new. The leather is thick and supple, and the jacket is well-constructed, making it a worthwhile investment.

2. Belstaff

Belstaff is a British brand that has been around since 1924 and is known for its rugged and stylish leather jackets. The brand's jackets are made from high-quality materials and are designed to withstand harsh weather conditions.

I recently purchased a Belstaff Trialmaster jacket, and I am impressed with its quality and attention to detail. The jacket is made from waxed cotton and leather and has a timeless style that can be dressed up or down.

3. AllSaints

AllSaints is a brand that offers a range of leather jackets that cater to different styles and budgets. The brand's jackets are made from high-quality leather and are designed with a modern twist. AllSaints offers a range of jackets, from classic biker jackets to more modern bomber jackets.

I recently purchased an AllSaints biker jacket, and I love how versatile it is. I can wear it with jeans and a t-shirt for a casual look, or dress it up with a shirt and trousers for a night out.

4. Acne Studios

Acne Studios is a Swedish brand that offers a range of leather jackets that are designed with a minimalist aesthetic. The brand's jackets are made from high-quality materials and are designed to be timeless and versatile.

While I haven't personally owned an Acne Studios jacket, I have tried on a few in-store and was impressed with the quality and fit. The jackets are well-constructed and made from buttery soft leather, making them a worthwhile investment.

How to Choose the Right Leather Jacket

When choosing a leather jacket, there are a few things to keep in mind. Firstly, consider your budget and how much you are willing to spend. Leather jackets can range from a few hundred to a few thousand dollars, so it's essential to set a budget.

Secondly, consider the style of jacket you want. Do you want a classic biker jacket, a more modern bomber jacket, or something in between? Think about how you will wear the jacket and what outfits it will go with.

Lastly, consider the quality of the jacket. Look for jackets that are made from high-quality materials, have a good fit, and are well-constructed. A good leather jacket can last you for years, so it's worth investing in one that will stand the test of time.

Conclusion of Best Leather Jacket Brands for Men

When it comes to finding the perfect leather jacket, there are many factors to consider, from the brand to the style and quality. The brands we've mentioned in this article are known for their high-quality leather jackets that are designed to be timeless and versatile, making them a worthwhile investment.

Question and Answer:

Q. What is the most important thing to consider when buying a leather jacket?

A. The most important thing to consider when buying a leather jacket is the quality of the jacket. Look for jackets that are made from high-quality materials, have a good fit, and are well-constructed.

Q. How much should I expect to spend on a leather jacket?

A. Leather jackets can range from a few hundred to a few thousand dollars, depending on the brand and quality. It's essential to set a budget and choose a jacket that fits your needs and preferences.

Q. What style of leather jacket should I choose?

A. The style of leather jacket you choose depends on your personal style and how you plan to wear the jacket. Classic biker jackets are timeless and versatile, while bomber jackets offer a more modern twist. Choose a style that fits your needs and preferences.

Q. How do I care for my leather jacket?

A. To care for your leather jacket, avoid exposing it to direct sunlight or heat sources, as this can cause the leather to dry out and crack. Use a leather conditioner to keep the leather supple and moisturized, and avoid using harsh chemicals or cleaners that can damage the leather.
