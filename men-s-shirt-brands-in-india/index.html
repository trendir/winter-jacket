Men s Shirt Brands In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Aug 20, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of going to stores and not finding the perfect shirt? Do you want to know about the best men's shirt brands in India? Look no further, we have got you covered!

When it comes to men's fashion, finding the right shirt can be a daunting task. With so many options available in the market, it can be overwhelming to choose the right brand that suits your style and budget.

In this article, we will discuss the top men's shirt brands in India, their target audience, price range, and what makes them stand out from the rest.

Let's dive in and explore the world of men's shirt brands in India!

Van Heusen

Van Heusen is one of the most popular men's shirt brands in India. They offer a wide range of shirts that cater to different occasions and styles. Their target audience is men who are looking for classic and sophisticated shirts that are perfect for formal events or business meetings.

Personally, I have been a loyal customer of Van Heusen for years. Their shirts are made with high-quality fabric and have a perfect fit that complements my body shape. Their price range starts from ₹1,000 and goes up to ₹4,000, which is reasonable considering the quality they offer.

Arrow

Arrow is another top-notch men's shirt brand in India that has been around for over 70 years. Their shirts are known for their versatility and durability. Arrow's target audience is men who prefer a classic and timeless style that is suitable for both formal and casual occasions.

I recently purchased an Arrow shirt for my friend's wedding, and I was impressed by its quality and comfort. Their price range starts from ₹1,500 and goes up to ₹5,000. Although they are slightly expensive, their shirts are worth every penny.

Peter England

Peter England is a well-known Indian brand that offers affordable and stylish shirts for men. Their target audience is men who are looking for trendy and comfortable shirts that are perfect for daily wear or casual outings.

I have a few Peter England shirts that I wear regularly to work, and I love how they fit me perfectly. Their price range starts from ₹500 and goes up to ₹2,500, making them an excellent choice for men who are on a tight budget.

Indian Terrain

Indian Terrain is a men's clothing brand that offers a wide range of shirts, including casual, formal, and semi-formal. Their target audience is men who prefer a relaxed and comfortable style that is perfect for outdoor activities or weekend getaways.

I recently purchased an Indian Terrain shirt for my trip to Goa, and I was amazed by its quality and comfort. Their price range starts from ₹1,500 and goes up to ₹4,000, which is reasonable considering the quality they offer.

Conclusion of Men's Shirt Brands in India

Choosing the right men's shirt brand in India can be overwhelming, but with this comprehensive guide, we hope to make your shopping experience easier. From classic and sophisticated to trendy and casual, these top men's shirt brands in India offer a wide range of options that cater to different styles and budgets. Whether you are looking for a shirt for a formal event or a weekend getaway, these brands have got you covered. Happy shopping!
