Types Of Traditional Saree Border A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Frank Aug 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a saree lover but confused about the various types of traditional saree borders? Do you want to know how to choose the right saree border for your occasion? Look no further, because we have got you covered!

In this article, we will discuss the various types of traditional saree borders and their significance. We will also provide tips on how to choose the right saree border for your occasion.

So, let's dive into the world of traditional saree borders!

To summarize, in this article, we will cover:

  • What are traditional saree borders?
  • Importance of saree borders in Indian culture
  • Types of traditional saree borders and their significance
  • Tips for choosing the right saree border for your occasion

Zari Border

One of the most popular types of traditional saree borders is the zari border. It is made of metallic threads, usually gold or silver, woven into the saree fabric. The zari border adds a touch of glamour and elegance to the saree, making it perfect for weddings and formal events.

I remember wearing a beautiful red saree with a zari border for my sister's wedding. I received so many compliments on my saree, and it made me feel confident and beautiful.

Kanjeevaram Border

The Kanjeevaram border is a traditional saree border from South India. It is made of pure silk and is known for its intricate designs and vibrant colors. The Kanjeevaram border is usually wider than other saree borders, making it perfect for grand occasions like weddings and festivals.

When I visited Chennai, I bought a Kanjeevaram saree with a beautiful peacock design on the border. I wore it to a friend's wedding, and it was a hit!

Paithani Border

The Paithani border is a traditional saree border from Maharashtra. It is made of pure silk and is known for its unique motifs and designs. The Paithani border is usually small and narrow, making it perfect for everyday wear.

When I visited Pune, I bought a Paithani saree with a beautiful peacock motif on the border. I wear it to work, and it adds a touch of elegance to my everyday look.

How to Choose the Right Saree Border

Choosing the right saree border can be overwhelming, but it doesn't have to be. Here are some tips to help you choose the right saree border for your occasion:

  • Consider the occasion: Is it a formal event or a casual gathering?
  • Consider your body type: Do you want to accentuate your curves or hide problem areas?
  • Consider your personal style: Do you prefer bold and vibrant colors or subtle and elegant designs?

Brocade Border

The brocade border is a traditional saree border from North India. It is made of silk or cotton and is known for its intricate designs and patterns. The brocade border is usually wider than other saree borders, making it perfect for grand occasions like weddings and festivals.

When I attended my cousin's wedding in Delhi, I wore a beautiful pink saree with a brocade border. It was a hit, and I felt like a princess!

Question and Answer

Q: What is the significance of saree borders in Indian culture?

A: Saree borders are an integral part of Indian culture and tradition. They add a touch of elegance and glamour to the saree, making it perfect for special occasions. Saree borders also represent the region and culture from where they originate.

Q: How do I maintain the saree border?

A: To maintain the saree border, avoid washing it with harsh chemicals or hot water. Instead, hand wash it with mild detergent in cold water. Also, avoid rubbing the saree border vigorously while washing or drying.

Q: Can I mix and match saree borders?

A: Yes, you can mix and match saree borders to create a unique and personalized look. However, make sure that the colors and designs complement each other.

Q: How do I store the saree with a border?

A: To store the saree with a border, fold it neatly and wrap it in a muslin cloth. Avoid storing it in plastic covers as it can damage the saree border.

Conclusion of Types of Traditional Saree Border

Traditional saree borders are an essential part of Indian culture and tradition. They add a touch of elegance and glamour to the saree, making it perfect for special occasions. With this comprehensive guide, you can now choose the right saree border for your next event and rock the traditional look!
