Different Types Of Women s Dress Collars A Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of wearing the same old dresses with the same old collars? Do you want to add some variety to your wardrobe? Look no further! In this guide, we’ll be discussing the different types of women’s dress collars that you can experiment with.

Choosing the right collar for your dress can make all the difference in your outfit. A collar can draw attention to your face, elongate your neck, or add a touch of elegance to your look. However, choosing the wrong collar can ruin your outfit and make you feel uncomfortable.

If you’re looking to add variety to your wardrobe, but don’t know where to start, keep reading! We’ll be discussing the different types of women’s dress collars and how to choose the right one for you.

In this article, we’ll be discussing the following types of women’s dress collars: Peter Pan collar, Mandarin collar, Shawl collar, Sailor collar, and Cowl neck. We’ll also touch on some other types of collars that you might come across.

Peter Pan Collar

The Peter Pan collar is a round collar that curves flat against the neckline. It is a cute and playful collar that can add a touch of whimsy to your outfit. This collar is a great option for dresses with high necklines, as it can add some interest to an otherwise plain look.

Personally, I love the Peter Pan collar because it reminds me of my childhood. It’s a great way to add a touch of nostalgia to my outfits.

Mandarin Collar

The Mandarin collar is a short, stand-up collar that is typically found on Chinese-inspired garments. It is a great option for dresses with a V-neckline, as it can add some structure to the overall look. This collar is also a great option for women with shorter necks, as it can elongate your neck.

I recently purchased a dress with a Mandarin collar, and I love how it adds some sophistication to my outfit. It’s a great way to add a touch of elegance to a simple dress.

Shawl Collar

The Shawl collar is a collar that rolls over and creates a draped effect on the neckline. It is a great option for dresses with low necklines, as it can add some coverage to the chest area. This collar is also a great option for women with broad shoulders, as it can balance out the overall look.

I’ve worn a dress with a shawl collar to a formal event, and I loved how it added some drama to my outfit. It’s a great way to make a statement without going overboard.

Sailor Collar

The Sailor collar is a flat, stand-up collar that is typically found on sailor-inspired garments. It is a great option for dresses with a boat neckline, as it can add some interest to the overall look. This collar is also a great option for women with longer necks, as it can balance out the overall look.

I’ve worn a dress with a Sailor collar to a beach wedding, and I loved how it added some nautical vibes to my outfit. It’s a great way to add some personality to your look.

Cowl Neck

The Cowl neck is a collar that drapes over the neckline and creates a soft, draped effect. It is a great option for dresses with a simple neckline, as it can add some interest to the overall look. This collar is also a great option for women with smaller chests, as it can add some volume to the chest area.

I’ve worn a dress with a Cowl neck to a fancy dinner, and I loved how it added some elegance to my outfit. It’s a great way to add some sophistication to your wardrobe.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a Peter Pan collar to work?

A: Yes, you can! Pair a Peter Pan collar dress with a blazer and some heels for a professional look.

Q: Can I wear a Shawl collar to a casual event?

A: Of course! Pair a Shawl collar dress with some sandals and a denim jacket for a casual, yet put-together look.

Q: Can I wear a Mandarin collar to a wedding?

A: Absolutely! A Mandarin collar dress can add some elegance to your wedding outfit.

Q: Can I wear a Sailor collar to the office?

A: It depends on the dress code of your office. If your office is business casual, a Sailor collar dress can add some personality to your outfit. If your office is more formal, a Sailor collar dress might not be appropriate.

Conclusion of Different Types of Women's Dress Collars

Choosing the right collar for your dress can make all the difference in your outfit. Whether you’re looking to add some sophistication to your wardrobe with a Cowl neck, or add some whimsy with a Peter Pan collar, there’s a collar out there for everyone. Experiment with different types of collars and find the one that makes you feel confident and comfortable.
