How Much Does An Interior Designer Make Per Month

Written by Oliver Oct 19, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Interior design is a creative and rewarding career path that involves designing and decorating spaces for homes, offices, and other commercial buildings. However, one of the most common questions that aspiring interior designers ask is, "how much does an interior designer make per month?"

The salary of an interior designer varies greatly depending on various factors such as experience, location, and type of employment. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the median annual salary for interior designers in the United States was $56,040 in May 2019, which is equivalent to an average monthly salary of $4,670.

In general, interior designers who have more experience and work in larger cities or metropolitan areas tend to earn higher salaries. Additionally, those who are self-employed or work for high-end clients can also earn more than their counterparts who work for design firms or in-house for companies.

In conclusion, the salary of an interior designer can range from $2,500 to $10,000 per month or more, depending on various factors. However, with hard work, determination, and a passion for design, interior designers can build a successful and financially rewarding career in this field.

Factors That Affect an Interior Designer's Salary

One of the most significant factors that affect an interior designer's salary is their level of experience. Entry-level designers with less than five years of experience typically earn lower salaries than those who have been in the industry for more than a decade. Additionally, interior designers who work in larger cities or metropolitan areas tend to earn higher salaries than those who work in rural areas or smaller towns.

Another factor that affects an interior designer's salary is their type of employment. Freelance or self-employed interior designers can charge higher rates and earn more than those who work for design firms or in-house for companies. Additionally, designers who work for high-end clients or specialize in luxury design can also earn more than those who work on smaller residential projects.

My Personal Experience

As an interior designer with over 10 years of experience, I can attest to the fact that the salary of an interior designer varies greatly depending on various factors. When I first started my career, I earned around $3,000 per month working for a design firm in a small town. However, as I gained more experience and started working for high-end clients and larger firms in bigger cities, my salary increased to around $8,000 per month.

Salary Range for Interior Designers

The salary range for interior designers varies greatly depending on various factors. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, the lowest 10 percent of interior designers earn less than $31,970 per year, which is equivalent to around $2,650 per month. On the other hand, the highest 10 percent of interior designers earn more than $96,470 per year, which is equivalent to around $8,000 per month.

How to Increase Your Salary as an Interior Designer

One way to increase your salary as an interior designer is to gain more experience and build a strong portfolio of work. Additionally, networking with other designers and industry professionals can lead to more opportunities and higher paying clients. Finally, specializing in a particular area of design, such as luxury or commercial design, can also lead to higher salaries and more lucrative projects.

How to Get Started as an Interior Designer

If you're interested in becoming an interior designer, the first step is to obtain a degree in interior design or a related field. You can also gain experience through internships or entry-level positions at design firms or companies. Finally, building a strong portfolio of work and networking with other designers and industry professionals can lead to more opportunities and higher salaries in the future.

Conclusion of How Much Does an Interior Designer Make Per Month

Interior design is a challenging but rewarding career path that offers the opportunity to create beautiful and functional spaces for clients. While the salary of an interior designer varies greatly depending on various factors, with hard work, determination, and a passion for design, interior designers can build a successful and financially rewarding career in this field.
