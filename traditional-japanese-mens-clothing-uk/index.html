Types of Traditional Japanese Mens Clothing UK

One of the most iconic pieces of traditional Japanese mens clothing is the kimono. However, there are several other types of clothing that are popular in Japan and can be incorporated into your wardrobe. These include:

Hakama: A type of traditional Japanese pants that are wide-legged and pleated. They are often worn with a kimono or with a traditional Japanese shirt called a haori.

Yukata: A lightweight, casual version of the kimono that is typically worn in the summer months. They are made from cotton or synthetic fabrics and are often decorated with bright, colorful patterns.

Jinbei: A type of casual Japanese clothing that consists of a short-sleeved shirt and shorts. They are often worn in the summer months and are made from lightweight, breathable fabrics.

Samue: A type of traditional Japanese workwear that consists of a jacket and pants. They are made from heavy-duty cotton or linen and are often worn by Buddhist monks or those working in traditional Japanese industries.

If you're interested in incorporating traditional Japanese mens clothing into your wardrobe, it's important to choose pieces that you feel comfortable wearing and that suit your personal style.
