What To Wear With A Black Dress To A Wedding

Written by Bardi Nov 10, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Wedding season is upon us and you've found the perfect black dress to wear. But now you're wondering, what do I wear with it? Don't worry, we've got you covered.

When it comes to weddings, there are a few things to consider when choosing what to wear with a black dress. You don't want to be too casual or too formal. It's important to find the right balance and make sure you don't upstage the bride.

The first thing to consider when choosing what to wear with a black dress to a wedding is the dress code. If the wedding is black-tie, you'll want to choose dressier accessories. If it's a more casual affair, you can be a bit more relaxed with your choices.

To create a cohesive look, choose accessories that complement the style and color of your dress. You can add pops of color with your shoes, jewelry, and clutch. Or, you can keep it simple and elegant with metallic accents.

Accessorizing a Black Dress for a Wedding

When I attended my friend's wedding last summer, I opted for a black midi dress with a floral print. To add some color, I paired it with red strappy heels and matching earrings. I completed the look with a metallic clutch and bangle bracelet. The accessories added some fun and personality to my outfit, while still keeping it appropriate for the occasion.

Choosing Shoes for a Black Dress

When it comes to shoes, you want to choose something comfortable, yet stylish. If you're wearing a shorter dress, strappy heels or pumps are a great option. If you're wearing a longer dress, you can opt for heels or flats. Just make sure they don't clash with your dress.

Choosing Jewelry for a Black Dress

When choosing jewelry for a black dress, less is more. You don't want to overpower your outfit with too many accessories. A simple necklace or earrings can go a long way in adding a touch of elegance to your look. If you're wearing a statement necklace, keep the rest of your jewelry minimal.

Choosing a Bag for a Black Dress

When it comes to bags, a clutch is a great option for a black dress. It's small and easy to carry, while still being stylish. You can choose a metallic clutch to add some shine to your outfit, or a bold colored clutch to add a pop of color.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear black to a wedding?

A: Yes, you can wear black to a wedding. Just make sure to dress it up with some colorful accessories and avoid anything too casual.

Q: Can I wear sandals with a black dress to a wedding?

A: It depends on the dress code and style of the sandals. If the wedding is more casual and the sandals are dressy, then yes. But if the wedding is more formal, it's best to opt for heels.

Q: Can I wear a denim jacket with a black dress to a wedding?

A: No, a denim jacket is too casual for a wedding. Try a blazer or cardigan instead.

Q: Can I wear a hat with a black dress to a wedding?

A: It depends on the style of the hat and the dress code. If the wedding is more casual and the hat is dressy, then yes. But if the wedding is more formal, it's best to avoid hats.

Conclusion of What to Wear with a Black Dress to a Wedding

When it comes to choosing what to wear with a black dress to a wedding, it's important to consider the dress code and style of the event. You want to find the right balance between casual and formal, while still adding some personality to your outfit with accessories. Remember, less is more when it comes to jewelry and bags. And most importantly, don't upstage the bride!
