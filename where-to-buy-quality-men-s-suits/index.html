Where To Buy Quality Men s Suits A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Jul 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of settling for ill-fitting, poorly-made suits? Do you want to invest in a quality suit that will make you look and feel your best? If so, you're in luck! In this guide, we'll explore the best places to buy quality men's suits that are both stylish and affordable. Whether you're shopping for a special occasion or looking to upgrade your work wardrobe, we've got you covered.

When it comes to buying a quality men's suit, there are a few pain points to consider. First and foremost, many men struggle to find suits that fit well, especially if they have a non-standard body type. Additionally, many department stores and fast fashion retailers offer cheaply-made suits that don't hold up over time. Finally, buying a suit can be expensive, especially if you're looking for high-quality materials and construction.

So, where can you go to find a quality men's suit that meets your needs and budget? Let's take a look.

To start, it's important to note that there are a variety of places to buy men's suits, from department stores to specialty retailers to online shops. Each option has its pros and cons, so it's important to do your research and consider your priorities before making a purchase.

Department Stores

Department stores like Macy's, Nordstrom, and Bloomingdale's are a popular choice for men's suits. They offer a wide range of styles and price points, and often have sales and promotions that can make a suit more affordable. However, department stores can be overwhelming to navigate, and it can be difficult to find a suit that fits well without the help of a knowledgeable salesperson.

Specialty Retailers

Specialty retailers like Men's Wearhouse, Jos. A. Bank, and Suit Supply offer a more curated selection of men's suits, with a focus on classic styles and quality construction. These retailers often have experienced salespeople who can help you find the perfect fit and style for your needs. However, specialty retailers can also be expensive, and may not offer as many options as department stores.

Online Retailers

Online retailers like Indochino, Suitsupply, and Black Lapel offer the convenience of shopping from home, along with a wide range of styles and price points. Many online retailers also offer custom-made suits that are tailored to your exact measurements, ensuring a perfect fit. However, it can be difficult to gauge the quality of a suit from online photos, and returning a suit can be a hassle if it doesn't fit or meet your expectations.

Bespoke Tailors

If you're looking for the ultimate in fit and customization, a bespoke tailor may be the best choice for you. A bespoke suit is made entirely from scratch, with every detail tailored to your preferences and measurements. While bespoke suits can be expensive, they offer unparalleled fit and quality, and can last for years with proper care.

My Personal Experience

As someone who has struggled to find suits that fit well in the past, I've had the most success with specialty retailers like Men's Wearhouse and Suit Supply. Both retailers offer a range of sizes and styles, and have knowledgeable salespeople who can help you find the perfect fit. Additionally, I've had good experiences with online retailers like Indochino, which offers custom-made suits at an affordable price point.

Tips for Buying a Quality Men's Suit

No matter where you choose to buy your suit, there are a few tips to keep in mind to ensure you're getting the best quality and fit possible:

  • Choose a classic style that will never go out of fashion.
  • Invest in quality materials like wool or cashmere.
  • Make sure the suit fits well in the shoulders and chest.
  • Consider getting the suit tailored to your exact measurements.
  • Don't be afraid to ask for help from a salesperson or tailor.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average price range for a quality men's suit?

A: The price range for a quality men's suit can vary widely depending on where you shop and what materials and construction you're looking for. Generally, you can expect to pay anywhere from $300 to $1500 for a quality suit.

Q: How can I make sure my suit fits well?

A: The best way to ensure a good fit is to try on multiple sizes and styles, and get feedback from a salesperson or tailor. Additionally, consider getting the suit tailored to your exact measurements for a perfect fit.

Q: How do I care for my men's suit?

A: To keep your suit looking its best, make sure to follow the care instructions on the label. Generally, it's best to dry clean your suit only when necessary, and to store it on a wide-shouldered hanger in a breathable garment bag.

Q: Can I wear a suit for casual occasions?

A: Absolutely! A suit can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion. Consider pairing a suit jacket with jeans and sneakers for a casual yet stylish look.

Conclusion

When it comes to buying a quality men's suit, there are a variety of options to consider, from department stores to online retailers to bespoke tailors. By doing your research and considering your priorities, you can find a suit that fits well, looks great, and lasts for years to come.
