What to Look for in Trekking Shoes Under 2000

When looking for the best trekking shoes in India under 2000, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, look for shoes with a sturdy outsole that provides good grip on various terrains. How to Care for Your Trekking Shoes Under 2000

To make sure your trekking shoes under 2000 last as long as possible, it's important to take care of them. After each trek, clean your shoes with a damp cloth and let them air dry. Avoid using harsh chemicals or a washing machine, as this can damage the shoes. You can also apply a waterproofing spray to the shoes to protect them from moisture.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use running shoes for trekking?

A: Running shoes are not recommended for trekking, as they do not provide the necessary support and grip for tough terrains.

Q: How should trekking shoes fit?

A: Trekking shoes should fit snugly but not be too tight. Make sure there is enough room in the toe box for your toes to move and wiggle, and that the heel is securely in place.

Q: Can I wear sandals for trekking?

A: Sandals are not recommended for trekking, as they do not provide adequate support and protection for your feet.

Q: How long do trekking shoes last?

A: The lifespan of trekking shoes depends on how often they are used and the conditions they are exposed to. Generally, trekking shoes can last anywhere from 6 months to a few years.
