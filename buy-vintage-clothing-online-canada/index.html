Question and Answer

Q: How do I know if a vintage piece is authentic?

A: Look for labels or tags that indicate the era the piece is from, and do some research on vintage clothing brands. You can also ask the seller for more information about the piece, such as its history and provenance.

Q: How do I determine the fit of a vintage piece?

A: Vintage sizing can be different from contemporary sizing, so be sure to check the measurements provided by the seller. You can also ask the seller for more information about how the piece fits.

Q: Is it safe to buy vintage clothing online?

A: As with any online purchase, it's important to do your research and buy from reputable sellers. Look for sellers with positive reviews and a history of selling authentic vintage pieces.

Q: How do I care for vintage clothing?

A: Vintage clothing can be delicate, so it's important to handle it with care. Follow the care instructions provided by the seller, and consider having the piece professionally cleaned.
