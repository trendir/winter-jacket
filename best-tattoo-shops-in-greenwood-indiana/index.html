Question and Answer Section

Q: How do I know if a tattoo shop is safe and hygienic?

A: Look for a shop that follows all of the proper safety and hygiene protocols, including using new needles for each customer, sterilizing equipment between uses, and following proper hand-washing and cleaning procedures.

Q: How much does a tattoo cost?

A: The cost of a tattoo can vary depending on a number of factors, including the size and complexity of the design, the location of the tattoo, and the experience level of the artist. It's a good idea to get a quote from your artist before your appointment so you know what to expect.

Q: Does getting a tattoo hurt?

A: Yes, getting a tattoo can be uncomfortable, but most people find that the pain is manageable. Your artist can also offer tips for managing pain during the tattooing process.

Q: How do I take care of my tattoo after getting it?

A: Your artist will give you instructions for aftercare, which typically include keeping the tattoo clean and moisturized, avoiding direct sunlight and soaking, and avoiding tight clothing that may rub against the tattoo.
