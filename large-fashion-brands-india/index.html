Discover The Top Large Fashion Brands In India

Written by Eveline Oct 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

India has a vibrant fashion industry with a rich cultural heritage that has been influencing fashion for centuries. However, finding the best large fashion brands in India can be a daunting task, especially for those who are new to the country or are not familiar with the local fashion scene.

If you are looking for stylish and affordable clothing options, then you are in the right place. In this article, we will guide you through the top large fashion brands in India that are known for their quality, affordability, and unique style.

Whether you are looking for traditional Indian clothing or western wear, these brands have something for everyone. So, without further ado, let's get started!

1. FabIndia

FabIndia is a well-known brand that specializes in traditional Indian clothing with a modern twist. With over 300 stores across India, FabIndia is the go-to brand for those who love to wear ethnic wear. Their collection includes sarees, kurtas, salwar kameez, and other traditional Indian clothing that is both stylish and comfortable.

What sets FabIndia apart from other brands is their focus on sustainability and promoting the work of local artisans. They source their fabrics and materials from rural areas and work directly with artisans, providing them with a livelihood while preserving traditional crafts.

2. H&M

H&M is a Swedish brand that has made a name for itself in the Indian fashion industry. They offer a wide range of western wear for men, women, and children at affordable prices. From casual t-shirts and jeans to formal wear, H&M has something for everyone.

What sets H&M apart from other brands is their focus on sustainability. They use eco-friendly materials and have launched several initiatives to reduce waste and promote ethical practices in the fashion industry.

3. Zara

Zara is a Spanish brand that has become popular in India for its trendy and affordable clothing. They offer a wide range of western wear for men, women, and children, including casual wear, formal wear, and accessories.

What sets Zara apart from other brands is their focus on fast fashion. They launch new collections every week, keeping up with the latest trends and styles.

4. Allen Solly

Allen Solly is an Indian brand that is known for its casual wear for men and women. They offer a wide range of clothing options, from t-shirts and jeans to dresses and jackets.

What sets Allen Solly apart from other brands is their focus on comfort and style. Their clothing is designed to be comfortable and easy to wear, while still looking stylish and trendy.

5. Biba

Biba is a well-known Indian brand that specializes in traditional Indian clothing for women. They offer a wide range of sarees, kurtas, salwar kameez, and other ethnic wear that is both stylish and comfortable.

What sets Biba apart from other brands is their focus on affordable clothing. They offer high-quality clothing at reasonable prices, making it accessible to everyone.

6. Levi's

Levi's is a popular American brand that has become a favorite among Indians for its high-quality jeans and casual wear. They offer a wide range of clothing options for men and women, including t-shirts, jeans, and jackets.

What sets Levi's apart from other brands is their focus on sustainability. They use eco-friendly materials and have launched several initiatives to reduce waste and promote ethical practices in the fashion industry.

Conclusion of Large Fashion Brands in India

India has a diverse fashion industry with many large brands that offer unique and affordable clothing options. Whether you prefer traditional Indian clothing or western wear, these brands have something for everyone. So, the next time you are looking for stylish and affordable clothing, make sure to check out these top large fashion brands in India.
