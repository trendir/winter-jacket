Best Shoes For Navy Blue Dress A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Oliver May 16, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you struggling to find the perfect shoes to match your navy blue dress? Look no further! In this article, we will explore the best shoe options for navy blue dresses and provide tips on how to choose the right pair for your outfit.

Trying to find the right shoes to match your navy blue dress can be a daunting task. You want to find a pair that compliments the dress without overpowering it, and one that also fits the occasion. It's not uncommon to feel overwhelmed by the number of options available. But don't worry, we've got you covered.

The best shoes for a navy blue dress are those that complement the dress without taking away from its elegance. The goal is to find a pair of shoes that accentuate the dress while also being comfortable to wear. There are several types of shoes that work well with navy blue dresses, and we'll explore them in this article.

Classic Black Pumps

When in doubt, go for classic black pumps. Black pumps are versatile and go with almost any outfit. They are a timeless choice that will never go out of style. Black pumps are a perfect choice for formal occasions or business meetings. They add a touch of sophistication to your outfit and create a classic look.

I remember the first time I wore a navy blue dress to a formal event. I was confused about what shoes to wear, and my friend suggested I wear black pumps. I was hesitant at first, but when I tried them on, they looked perfect. The black pumps not only matched my dress but also made me feel confident and elegant.

Nude Heels

Nude heels are another excellent option for a navy blue dress. Nude heels are a great choice for daytime events, weddings, and summer parties. They add a touch of femininity to the outfit and make your legs appear longer. Nude heels complement the dress while also creating a stylish look.

I once attended a summer wedding wearing a navy blue dress and nude heels. The combination was perfect for the occasion. The nude heels were comfortable to wear, and they matched my dress without overpowering it.

Metallic Shoes

Metallic shoes are a bold choice for a navy blue dress. They bring a touch of glamour and sparkle to the outfit, making it perfect for evening events, parties, and weddings. Metallic shoes come in various shades, such as silver, gold, and rose gold. They add a touch of excitement to the outfit and make you stand out from the crowd.

One of my favorite outfits is a navy blue dress paired with silver metallic shoes. The combination creates a stunning look that is perfect for a night out with friends or a formal event.

Strappy Sandals

Strappy sandals are a great choice for a casual, summer look. They are comfortable to wear and add a touch of elegance to the outfit. Strappy sandals come in various styles, such as flat, wedge, or high heel. They are a perfect choice for a beach wedding or a daytime event.

I once wore a navy blue dress with strappy sandals to a beach wedding. The combination was perfect for the occasion. The strappy sandals were comfortable to wear, and they complemented the dress without overpowering it.

Conclusion of Best Shoes for Navy Blue Dress

Choosing the right shoes to match your navy blue dress can be a challenge, but it doesn't have to be. You can never go wrong with classic black pumps or nude heels. Metallic shoes and strappy sandals are also excellent options, depending on the occasion. When choosing the perfect pair of shoes, consider comfort, style, and the occasion. With these tips, you're sure to find the perfect pair of shoes to match your navy blue dress.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear navy shoes with a navy blue dress?

A: It's not recommended to wear navy shoes with a navy blue dress. The colors may clash, and the outfit may appear too matchy-matchy. Instead, try pairing your navy blue dress with shoes in a contrasting color, such as black, nude, metallic, or white.

Q: What type of shoes should I wear with a navy blue cocktail dress?

A: For a cocktail event, you can wear classic black pumps or metallic shoes. If the event is during the day, you can opt for nude heels or strappy sandals.

Q: Can I wear boots with a navy blue dress?

A: It depends on the style of the dress and the boots. Knee-high boots may work with a maxi or midi dress, but ankle boots may not be the best choice. It's best to avoid boots with a navy blue dress for formal events or weddings.

Q: Are flats a good option for a navy blue dress?

A: Flats can work well for a casual look or daytime event. However, they may not be the best option for formal events or weddings. If you choose to wear flats with a navy blue dress, try to find a pair that will complement the dress without overpowering it.
