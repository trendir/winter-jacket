Discovering The World Of Small Cycling Brands Why You Should Consider Them

Written by Oliver Sep 06, 2023 · 5 min read

If you're an avid cyclist, you know the importance of having quality gear and equipment to enhance your performance. While there are many big-name brands out there, it's worth considering the benefits of purchasing from small cycling brands. These companies often offer unique, high-quality products that are designed with care and attention to detail.

A common pain point for small cycling brands is a lack of recognition and exposure in the market. With so many well-established companies dominating the industry, it can be challenging for smaller brands to stand out and gain a customer base. However, with the rise of social media and e-commerce platforms, small cycling brands now have more opportunities to reach a wider audience.

If you're looking for something different from the usual big-name cycling brands, you might want to check out some small cycling brands. These companies often specialize in specific areas, such as custom bikes, handmade components, or sustainable materials. By supporting small cycling brands, you can discover unique products that align with your values and preferences.

In this article, we'll explore the world of small cycling brands and why you should consider them. We'll also highlight some of the best small cycling brands out there and what makes them stand out.

Custom Bikes from Breadwinner Cycles

One of the best small cycling brands out there is Breadwinner Cycles. Founded in 2013 in Portland, Oregon, this company specializes in custom steel bikes that are built to last. The team at Breadwinner Cycles believes that every rider deserves a bike that fits them perfectly, so they offer a range of options for customization.

As a personal experience, I had the chance to ride one of their bikes, and it was a game-changer. The quality of the materials and the attention to detail in the design made for an incredibly smooth and comfortable ride. Plus, knowing that my bike was made specifically for me added an extra level of connection and pride in my ride.

Handmade Components from Paul Component Engineering

Another small cycling brand worth checking out is Paul Component Engineering. Based in Chico, California, this company has been making high-quality bike components by hand since 1989. Their products are known for their durability, reliability, and unique designs.

As a personal experience, I have a set of Paul Component Engineering brake levers on my bike, and they have been nothing but reliable and smooth. The attention to detail in the design and construction is evident in their performance, and the unique look adds a touch of personality to my bike.

The Benefits of Supporting Small Cycling Brands

Aside from discovering unique and high-quality products, there are many benefits to supporting small cycling brands. By purchasing from these companies, you're supporting independent businesses and individuals who are passionate about what they do. You're also contributing to local economies and helping to create a diverse and vibrant cycling community.

How to Find Small Cycling Brands

If you're interested in exploring the world of small cycling brands, there are many ways to find them. Social media platforms like Instagram are a great place to discover new brands and products. You can also check out cycling forums, blogs, and online marketplaces that specialize in independent and handmade products.

Sustainable Materials from Green Guru Gear

Green Guru Gear is another small cycling brand that stands out for its commitment to sustainability. Based in Boulder, Colorado, this company specializes in creating products from upcycled and recycled materials, such as bike bags, backpacks, and accessories.

As a personal experience, I have a Green Guru Gear bike tube bag that has held up incredibly well over the years. Knowing that it was made from materials that would have otherwise gone to waste adds an extra level of satisfaction to my purchase.

Question and Answer

Q: Are small cycling brands more expensive than big-name brands?

A: It depends on the brand and the product. While some small cycling brands may charge a premium for their unique and handmade products, others may offer more affordable options. It's worth doing your research and comparing prices to find a brand that fits your budget.

Q: How can I be sure of the quality of products from small cycling brands?

A: Many small cycling brands offer warranties or guarantees on their products, so you can have peace of mind in your purchase. You can also look for reviews and testimonials from other customers to get an idea of the quality and performance of the product.

Q: Can I customize products from small cycling brands?

A: Many small cycling brands offer customization options for their products, such as color choices or sizing. Some brands even offer fully custom designs for bikes or components.

Q: How can I support small cycling brands?

A: The best way to support small cycling brands is by purchasing their products and spreading the word. You can also follow them on social media and engage with their content to help boost their visibility.

Conclusion of Small Cycling Brands

When it comes to cycling gear and equipment, there are many big-name brands out there. However, it's worth considering the benefits of supporting small cycling brands. These companies often offer unique, high-quality products that are designed with care and attention to detail. By purchasing from small cycling brands, you're supporting independent businesses and individuals who are passionate about what they do. So why not give them a try and discover something new?
