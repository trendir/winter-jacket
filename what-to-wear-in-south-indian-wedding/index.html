What To Wear In South Indian Wedding A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 06, 2023 · 4 min read

South Indian weddings are known for their grandeur and opulence. From the vibrant decor to the mouth-watering cuisine, everything about these weddings is larger than life. However, when it comes to attending a South Indian wedding, one of the biggest dilemmas is what to wear. With so many rituals and ceremonies to attend, choosing the perfect outfit can be quite challenging. In this blog post, we will walk you through everything you need to know about what to wear in a South Indian wedding.

Attending a South Indian wedding can be overwhelming, especially if you are not familiar with the customs and traditions. However, one of the biggest concerns for most guests is what to wear. With so many ceremonies and events to attend, choosing the right outfit can be quite a challenge.

When it comes to South Indian weddings, traditional attire is always the way to go. Women can opt for a saree or a lehenga, while men can choose from a dhoti-kurta or a sherwani. The key is to choose an outfit that is comfortable and allows you to move around freely.

To sum it up, when it comes to what to wear in a South Indian wedding, traditional attire is always the safest choice. However, it is important to choose an outfit that is comfortable and allows you to move around freely.

What to Wear in South Indian Wedding: Saree or Lehenga?

As mentioned earlier, when it comes to South Indian weddings, traditional attire is always the way to go. For women, the most popular choices are sarees and lehengas. While both these outfits are stunning, they have their own unique appeal.

Personally, I prefer wearing sarees to weddings. There is something about the grace and elegance of a saree that makes it perfect for weddings. However, lehengas are also a great option, especially if you prefer a more modern look. The key is to choose an outfit that makes you feel comfortable and confident.

What to Wear in South Indian Wedding: Dhoti-Kurta or Sherwani?

For men, the most popular choices are dhoti-kurtas and sherwanis. While dhoti-kurtas are more traditional, sherwanis are a more modern twist on the classic Indian attire. Personally, I prefer dhoti-kurtas for weddings. They are comfortable, elegant, and perfect for all the dancing and festivities.

What to Wear in South Indian Wedding: Jewelry and Accessories

When it comes to jewelry and accessories, less is always more. Women can opt for simple gold jewelry or statement earrings, but it is important to avoid going overboard. As for men, a simple watch and cufflinks are all you need to complete the look.

What to Wear in South Indian Wedding: Footwear

When it comes to footwear, comfort should be your top priority. Women can opt for traditional juttis or sandals, while men can choose from mojris or formal shoes. The key is to choose footwear that is comfortable and allows you to dance the night away.

Conclusion of What to Wear in South Indian Wedding

Attending a South Indian wedding is a truly magical experience. However, choosing the perfect outfit can be quite a challenge. When it comes to what to wear in a South Indian wedding, traditional attire is always the safest choice. Whether you choose a saree or a lehenga, or a dhoti-kurta or a sherwani, the key is to choose an outfit that is comfortable and allows you to move around freely. With these tips in mind, you are sure to look stunning at your next South Indian wedding.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear a western outfit to a South Indian wedding?

A: While it is not recommended, you can wear a western outfit to a South Indian wedding. However, it is important to keep in mind that traditional attire is always the safer choice.

Q: What colors should I avoid when choosing an outfit for a South Indian wedding?

A: It is best to avoid wearing black or white to a South Indian wedding. Instead, opt for vibrant colors such as red, pink, or orange.

Q: Is it okay to wear jeans to a South Indian wedding?

A: No, jeans are not an appropriate attire for a South Indian wedding. Traditional attire such as sarees, lehengas, dhoti-kurtas, or sherwanis are the way to go.

Q: Can I wear heavy makeup to a South Indian wedding?

A: While it is okay to wear makeup, it is important to avoid going overboard. Keep it simple and elegant, and let your natural beauty shine through.
