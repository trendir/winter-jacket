Clothes Names List: Shirts and Tops

When it comes to tops, there are many different styles and cuts to choose from. Here are some of the most common types of shirts:

Blouse: A dressy top with a collar and buttons down the front.

Button-Down: A shirt with buttons down the front that can be dressed up or down.

Skirt: A garment worn on the lower half of the body that hangs from the waist.
