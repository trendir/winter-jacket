Black Shirt Price In Bangladesh A Complete Guide

Written by Frank Aug 06, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking to buy a black shirt in Bangladesh but don't know the current market price? Or do you want to know where to find the best deals on black shirts in Bangladesh? Look no further because we have got you covered.

Black shirt price in Bangladesh varies depending on several factors such as the fabric quality, brand, and design. However, the pain point for most people is finding a good quality black shirt at an affordable price.

The average price range for a black shirt in Bangladesh is between BDT 500 to BDT 2500. The price may vary depending on the fabric quality, brand, and design. Generally, cotton shirts are more affordable than silk or linen shirts.

Factors Affecting Black Shirt Price in Bangladesh

As mentioned earlier, several factors affect the price of black shirts in Bangladesh. Let's dive deeper into these factors:

Fabric Quality

The fabric quality is one of the most significant factors affecting the price of a black shirt. High-quality fabrics like silk or linen will cost more than cotton or polyester.

Brand

The brand name of the shirt also affects the price. Popular brands like Arong or Aarong will cost more than lesser-known brands.

Design

The design of the shirt is another factor that affects the price. Shirts with intricate designs or embroidery will cost more than plain shirts.

Where to Buy Black Shirts in Bangladesh

Now that you have an idea about the price range and factors affecting black shirt price in Bangladesh, you may wonder where to find them. You can find black shirts at any local market or shopping mall in Bangladesh. However, if you are looking for a good quality shirt at an affordable price, we recommend checking out these places:

Online Stores

Online stores like Daraz, Ajkerdeal, and Bagdoom offer a wide range of black shirts at affordable prices. You can compare prices and designs and choose the one that fits your budget and style.

Local Markets

Local markets like New Market in Dhaka, Chawk Bazaar in Chittagong, or Sylhet Main Market offer a variety of black shirts at reasonable prices. You can bargain with the shopkeepers and get a good deal.

Conclusion

Black shirt price in Bangladesh varies depending on several factors such as fabric quality, brand, and design. You can find a good quality black shirt at an affordable price by checking out online stores or local markets in Bangladesh. Always compare prices and designs before making a purchase.
