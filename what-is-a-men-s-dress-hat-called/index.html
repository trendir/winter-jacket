What Is A Men s Dress Hat Called

Written by Frank Aug 21, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you ever wonder what the fancy hats that men wear to formal events are called? Perhaps you've seen them in movies or on the red carpet, but you're not quite sure what to call them. Well, wonder no more! In this tutorial, we'll be discussing what a men's dress hat is called and everything you need to know about it.

For some, attending formal events can be a nerve-wracking experience, and not being familiar with the terminology can only add to that stress. You might be worried about looking out of place or not knowing what to call certain items of clothing. But don't worry, we've got you covered.

A men's dress hat is commonly referred to as a fedora. Although the fedora is often associated with men's fashion, it was originally designed for women in the late 1800s. However, it soon became popular among men and has remained a staple in fashion ever since. The fedora is a versatile accessory that can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

In summary, a men's dress hat is called a fedora. It is a timeless accessory that can add a touch of sophistication to any outfit. Now that we've answered the question of what a men's dress hat is called, let's dive deeper into its history, styles, and how to wear it.

History of the Fedora

As mentioned earlier, the fedora was originally designed for women. However, it was quickly adopted by men as a stylish accessory. The name "fedora" was first introduced in the late 1800s by a play called "Fedora" in which the main character wore a hat similar to the one we know today.

During the 1920s and 1930s, the fedora became a popular accessory for gangsters and movie stars alike. It was often associated with the likes of Al Capone and Humphrey Bogart. However, it wasn't until the 1980s and 1990s that the fedora experienced a resurgence in popularity thanks to television shows like "Miami Vice" and "Seinfeld."

Styles of the Fedora

While the fedora may have a classic look, there are several styles to choose from. The most common types of fedoras include:

  • The classic fedora
  • The trilby fedora
  • The pork pie fedora
  • The homburg fedora

The classic fedora has a wide brim and a creased crown. The trilby fedora has a shorter brim and a more tapered crown. The pork pie fedora has a flat top and a curled-up brim. Finally, the homburg fedora has a stiff brim and a creased crown.

How to Wear a Fedora

Now that you know what a men's dress hat is called and its different styles, it's time to learn how to wear one. Here are some tips:

  • Make sure the hat fits properly. It should sit comfortably on your head without being too tight or too loose.
  • Choose a style that complements your face shape. For example, those with round faces may want to opt for a fedora with a taller crown to elongate their face.
  • Pair the fedora with a well-fitted suit or dressy outfit. It's important to keep the overall look polished and put-together.
  • Feel confident! Wearing a fedora is a bold fashion statement, so be sure to own it.

Common Questions about Men's Dress Hats

Q: Can women wear fedoras?

A: Absolutely! While the fedora is traditionally associated with men's fashion, women can rock this accessory as well.

Q: Are fedoras only worn to formal events?

A: While the fedora is often seen at formal events, it can also be worn as a casual accessory. Pair it with a t-shirt and jeans for a more laid-back look.

Q: How do I clean my fedora?

A: The best way to clean a fedora is to use a soft-bristled brush to remove any dust or debris. For more stubborn stains, use a damp cloth and a mild soap. Avoid getting the hat too wet and be sure to let it air dry.

Q: Can I wear a fedora with any color suit?

A: While the fedora can be paired with almost any suit color, it's best to choose a hat that complements the overall outfit. For example, a black or grey fedora would pair well with a navy suit.

Conclusion of What is a Men's Dress Hat Called

In conclusion, a men's dress hat is called a fedora. This versatile accessory has a rich history and comes in several styles to choose from. Whether you're attending a formal event or just want to add a touch of sophistication to your outfit, the fedora is a great choice. With the tips provided in this tutorial, you'll be able to confidently wear a fedora and make a bold fashion statement.
