Top 5 Formal Shirt Brands In India

Written by Daniel Aug 29, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of searching for the perfect formal shirt that fits well, looks stylish, and is comfortable to wear? Do you often find yourself settling for mediocre quality shirts that don't meet your expectations? If so, then you're in luck because we've curated a list of the top 5 formal shirt brands in India that are sure to meet all your needs and more!

If you're a working professional, then you know the importance of dressing well and making a good impression. Formal shirts are an essential part of any professional wardrobe, but it can be challenging to find the right one that checks all the boxes. That's where our list comes in – we've done the research and testing so that you don't have to!

Our target is to introduce you to the top 5 formal shirt brands in India that offer the best quality, style, and comfort. We understand that each individual has a unique taste and preference, so we've included a variety of brands that cater to different needs and budgets.

1. Van Heusen

I have personally used Van Heusen shirts for years, and they never disappoint. Their shirts are made with high-quality fabrics that are comfortable to wear and last long. They offer a wide range of styles and colors, making it easy to find the perfect shirt for any occasion. The brand is known for its classic and timeless designs that never go out of style. Van Heusen shirts are a bit on the pricier side, but the quality justifies the cost.

2. Arrow

Arrow is another popular formal shirt brand that has been around for decades. Their shirts are known for their impeccable quality and attention to detail. Arrow offers a wide range of styles, from classic to modern, so there's something for everyone. The brand is also known for its innovative technology that ensures the shirts stay wrinkle-free even after multiple washes. Arrow shirts are priced reasonably, making them an affordable option for those on a budget.

3. Louis Philippe

If you're looking for a luxurious formal shirt brand, then Louis Philippe is the way to go. Their shirts are made with premium quality fabrics that are soft to the touch and look sophisticated. Louis Philippe is known for its superior craftsmanship and attention to detail, making their shirts a favorite among fashion enthusiasts. The brand offers a wide range of styles, from classic to trendy, so there's something for everyone. Louis Philippe shirts are on the pricier side, but the quality justifies the cost.

4. Peter England

Peter England is a well-known Indian formal shirt brand that offers a wide range of styles and sizes. Their shirts are made with high-quality fabrics that are comfortable to wear and look elegant. Peter England is known for its affordable pricing, making it a popular choice among working professionals on a budget. The brand offers a variety of styles, from classic to trendy, so there's something for everyone.

5. Park Avenue

Park Avenue is a formal shirt brand that offers a range of classic and modern designs. Their shirts are made with premium quality fabrics that are comfortable to wear and look stylish. Park Avenue is known for its attention to detail and unique designs that stand out from the crowd. The brand offers a variety of styles, from solid colors to bold prints, so there's something for everyone. Park Avenue shirts are reasonably priced, making them an affordable option for those on a budget.

Why Should You Invest in High-Quality Formal Shirts?

Investing in high-quality formal shirts is essential for a working professional. A good quality shirt not only looks good but also feels good to wear. High-quality fabrics are soft to the touch, comfortable to wear, and last longer than their cheaper counterparts. They also require less maintenance and are less likely to shrink or fade after multiple washes. In addition, high-quality shirts are often more durable and can withstand wear and tear better than cheaper shirts.

How to Choose the Right Formal Shirt?

When choosing a formal shirt, it's essential to consider the fit, fabric, and style. The fit should be snug but not too tight, and the fabric should be comfortable to wear and of high quality. The style should match your personal preference and the occasion you'll be wearing it for. It's also important to consider the brand's reputation and customer reviews before making a purchase.

Conclusion of Top 5 Formal Shirt Brands in India

In conclusion, the top 5 formal shirt brands in India offer a wide range of styles and sizes that cater to different needs and budgets. Investing in high-quality formal shirts is essential for a working professional who wants to make a good impression and feel confident. Whether you're looking for a classic or trendy design, these brands are sure to meet your expectations and more!
