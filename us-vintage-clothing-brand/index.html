How to Care for Your Vintage Clothing

While vintage clothing is often made with high-quality materials, it can also be delicate and require special care. Always read the care label before washing or dry cleaning your vintage pieces. If the label is missing, hand washing in cold water is usually a safe bet. Avoid washing vintage pieces in hot water or putting them in the dryer, as this can damage the fabric.
