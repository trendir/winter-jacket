FAQs

Q. What is the minimum order quantity for wholesale stationery suppliers online in India?

A. The minimum order quantity varies from supplier to supplier. Some suppliers may require a minimum order quantity of 500 units, while others may require a minimum order quantity of 1000 units.

Q. Can I order customized stationery products from wholesale suppliers?

A. Yes, many wholesale suppliers offer customized stationery products. However, the minimum order quantity for customized products may be higher than for standard products.

Q. How long does it take for wholesale suppliers to deliver products?

A. Delivery time varies from supplier to supplier. Some suppliers may be able to deliver products within 2-3 days, while others may take up to 2 weeks.

Q. Can I return products if I am not satisfied with them?

A. This depends on the supplier's return policy. Some suppliers may offer a full refund for products that are returned within a certain time period, while others may only offer a partial refund or no refund at all.
