Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for when buying sports shoes?

A: When buying sports shoes, you should look for comfort, support, and durability. Make sure the shoes fit well and offer enough support for the type of activity you'll be doing.

Q: How often should I replace my sports shoes?

A: You should replace your sports shoes every 300-500 miles or every six months, whichever comes first. This will ensure that your shoes offer enough support and cushioning for your feet.

Q: Can I wear sports shoes for everyday use?

A: Yes, you can wear sports shoes for everyday use. Many sports shoes are designed to be stylish and offer enough support for everyday wear.

Q: What's the difference between running shoes and training shoes?

A: Running shoes are designed for forward motion and offer more cushioning, while training shoes are designed for lateral movements and offer more support.
