How To Put A Button On A Shirt Without Sewing A Step By Step Guide

Written by Christine Aug 06, 2023 · 3 min read

Do you have a shirt with a missing button and no sewing skills? Don't worry, you're not alone. Many people struggle with putting buttons on shirts, especially if they don't have access to a sewing kit. Fortunately, there is a way to put a button on a shirt without sewing. In this tutorial, we'll show you how.

Trying to sew a button onto a shirt without any knowledge of sewing can be frustrating. It's easy to get the thread tangled or sew the button on crooked. It can also be time-consuming and require a lot of effort. But don't worry, we have a solution for you.

The easiest way to put a button on a shirt without sewing is to use fabric glue. Fabric glue is a special type of adhesive that is designed to bond fabric together. It's easy to use and doesn't require any sewing skills. All you need is a few basic materials and some patience.

To put a button on a shirt without sewing, you'll need the following materials: a button, fabric glue, a toothpick, and a piece of cardboard or paper.

Step 1: Choose the Right Button

Before you start, make sure you choose the right button for your shirt. The button should match the color and size of the other buttons on your shirt. It's also important to choose a button that is the right thickness, so it will fit through the buttonhole.

Step 2: Prepare the Button and Shirt

Place the shirt on a flat surface and insert the cardboard or paper behind the buttonhole. This will prevent the glue from seeping through to the other side of the shirt. Apply a small amount of fabric glue to the back of the button and use the toothpick to spread it evenly over the surface.

Step 3: Attach the Button

Place the button on the shirt and press down firmly. Hold the button in place for a few minutes to allow the glue to bond. Once the button is secure, let it dry for at least 24 hours before wearing the shirt.

Step 4: Repeat for Additional Buttons

If you need to attach more than one button, repeat the process for each button. Make sure to allow each button to dry completely before attaching the next one.

Additional Tips:

When using fabric glue, it's important to follow the manufacturer's instructions. Some glues may require you to wash the shirt before wearing it, while others may require a specific drying time. It's also important to avoid getting the glue on your skin, as it can be difficult to remove.

Is it Safe to Use Fabric Glue on Shirts?

Yes, fabric glue is safe to use on shirts. It's designed to bond fabric together and is washable. However, it's important to follow the manufacturer's instructions and allow the glue to dry completely before wearing the shirt.

Can You Remove a Button Attached with Fabric Glue?

Yes, you can remove a button attached with fabric glue. Simply apply heat to the button with a hairdryer or iron, and it should come off easily. However, it's important to be careful when removing the button, as the glue may be hot.

Conclusion of How to Put a Button on a Shirt Without Sewing

Putting a button on a shirt without sewing is easy with fabric glue. By following our step-by-step guide, you can attach a button to your shirt in no time. Remember to choose the right button, prepare the shirt and button, attach the button, and let it dry completely. With a little patience and practice, you'll be able to add buttons to your shirts without any sewing skills. Happy buttoning!
