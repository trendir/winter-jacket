Most Popular Suit Brands In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Jun 10, 2023 Â· 5 min read

When it comes to dressing up for a special occasion or a formal event, wearing a suit is always a good idea. However, choosing the right suit brand can be quite overwhelming, especially in a country like India where there are numerous options available. In this blog post, we'll take a look at the most popular suit brands in India and help you find the perfect suit for your next event.

If you're looking for a suit in India, you might be faced with several pain points such as not knowing which brand to choose, not being familiar with the latest trends, and not knowing how to find the right fit. But don't worry, we've got you covered.

The target of this blog post is to provide you with a comprehensive guide to the most popular suit brands in India, their unique features, and what makes them stand out from the rest.

To summarize, this blog post will give you an insight into the most popular suit brands in India, their specialties, and what you should keep in mind when choosing a suit.

1. Raymond

Raymond is one of the oldest and most popular suit brands in India. Founded in 1925, Raymond offers a wide range of suits that cater to different occasions and budgets. Their suits are known for their quality, style, and durability, making them a popular choice among Indian men.

Personally, I had a great experience with Raymond when I had to attend a wedding. The staff at the store were very helpful in finding the right suit for me, and the suit I chose was comfortable and looked great.

2. Louis Philippe

Another popular suit brand in India is Louis Philippe. Known for their elegant and sophisticated suits, Louis Philippe offers a range of suits that are perfect for formal events and business meetings. Their suits are made from high-quality fabrics and are tailored to perfection.

I recently bought a suit from Louis Philippe for a corporate event, and I was impressed with the quality of the fabric and the fit. The suit was comfortable to wear all day and looked great.

3. Blackberrys

Blackberrys is a popular Indian brand that offers a range of suits for men. Their suits are known for their contemporary designs, slim fit, and attention to detail. They offer a range of suits that cater to different budgets, making them an affordable option for men who want to look stylish without breaking the bank.

When I needed a suit for a friend's wedding, I went to Blackberrys, and I was pleasantly surprised by the variety of designs they had to offer. The suit I chose was comfortable and looked great, and I received a lot of compliments on it.

4. Van Heusen

Van Heusen is a popular brand that offers a range of suits for men. Their suits are known for their modern designs and attention to detail. Their suits are made from high-quality fabrics and are tailored to fit perfectly. They offer a range of suits that cater to different budgets, making them an affordable option for men who want to look stylish and sophisticated.

I recently bought a suit from Van Heusen for a job interview, and I was impressed with the quality of the fabric and the fit. The suit was comfortable to wear, and I felt confident during the interview.

Question and Answer

Q: How do I choose the right suit size?

A: It's important to get measured by a professional tailor to ensure you get the right fit. Make sure to stand up straight, and don't suck in your stomach. The suit should fit well around the shoulders, and the sleeves should end where your wrist meets your hand.

Q: How much should I spend on a suit?

A: It depends on your budget and the occasion. If you're buying a suit for a wedding or a job interview, you might want to invest in a high-quality suit that will last you a long time. However, if you're on a tight budget, you can still find affordable suits that look great.

Q: What color suit should I wear?

A: It depends on the occasion and your personal style. If you're attending a wedding, a navy or charcoal suit is a good choice. For a job interview, a black or grey suit is a safe option. If you want to stand out, you can opt for a bold color like red or green.

Q: How do I take care of my suit?

A: It's important to dry clean your suit regularly to keep it looking fresh. Avoid washing it at home, as this can damage the fabric. Hang your suit on a wooden hanger to maintain its shape, and store it in a garment bag to protect it from dust and dirt.

Conclusion of Most Popular Suit Brands in India

Choosing the right suit brand can be overwhelming, but it's important to find a brand that suits your style and budget. Raymond, Louis Philippe, Blackberrys, and Van Heusen are some of the most popular suit brands in India, each offering unique features and designs. By considering your personal style, the occasion, and your budget, you can find the perfect suit that will make you look and feel confident.
