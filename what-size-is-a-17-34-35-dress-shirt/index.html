What Size is a 17 34/35 Dress Shirt?

Shopping for dress shirts can be overwhelming, especially when it comes to finding the right size. If you're someone who's looking for a 17 34/35 dress shirt, you may have experienced some frustration in finding the perfect fit. In this article, we will answer all your questions related to what size is a 17 34/35 dress shirt and make your shopping experience easier.

A 17 34/35 dress shirt refers to the neck size and sleeve length of the shirt. The number 17 represents the neck size, which measures the circumference of your neck. The number 34/35 represents the sleeve length, which measures from the center of your back to the end of your wrist. This size is considered to be on the larger side, and it may be challenging to find in some stores. However, many brands offer this size, and you can also find it online.

Many department stores and men's clothing stores offer dress shirts in this size. However, if you're having trouble finding it in-store, you can always turn to online shopping. How to Ensure the Right Fit

When shopping for a dress shirt, it's crucial to ensure the right fit. A shirt that's too tight or too loose can be uncomfortable and unflattering. To ensure the right fit, measure your neck and sleeve length accurately. You can do this at home or have a professional measure you at a store. Additionally, pay attention to the shirt's fabric and style. Some fabrics may shrink after washing, so it's essential to read the care instructions before purchasing.

Tips for Caring for Your Dress Shirt

To keep your dress shirt looking great, it's crucial to care for it properly. Always follow the care instructions on the label, and avoid using harsh detergents or bleach. Additionally, iron your shirt on a low heat setting to prevent damage to the fabric. Proper care will keep your shirt looking new for longer, and you won't have to replace it as often.
