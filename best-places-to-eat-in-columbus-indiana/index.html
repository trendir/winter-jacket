So, without further ado, let's dive into our list of the best places to eat in Columbus Indiana.

1. Zaharakos Ice Cream Parlor and Museum

If you're looking for a unique dining experience in Columbus Indiana, Zaharakos Ice Cream Parlor and Museum is a must-visit. This historic ice cream parlor has been serving up delicious treats since 1900, and it's a great place to take the whole family. With its vintage decor and old-fashioned soda fountain, Zaharakos is a step back in time. And the ice cream is delicious, too!

2. 450 North Brewing Company

If you're a fan of craft beer, you won't want to miss 450 North Brewing Company. This local brewery is known for its delicious and unique beers, and it's a great spot to hang out with friends or catch a game. The food is also excellent, with a menu that features everything from burgers and sandwiches to salads and pizzas.

3. The Garage Pub and Grill

The Garage Pub and Grill is a popular spot for locals, and it's not hard to see why. This casual eatery serves up delicious pub food, like burgers, wings, and nachos, and it's a great place to grab a drink and watch a game. The atmosphere is laid-back and friendly, making it a great spot to unwind after a long day.

4. Henry Social Club

If you're looking for a more upscale dining experience, Henry Social Club is a great choice. This restaurant has a sophisticated atmosphere and a menu that features everything from steak and seafood to pasta and salads. The cocktails are also excellent, and the service is top-notch.
