Curry House

Curry House is one of the most popular Indian restaurants in North Goa. Located in Calangute, this restaurant serves a wide range of Indian dishes, including vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. The atmosphere is cozy and welcoming, and the staff is friendly and attentive.

My personal experience at Curry House was fantastic. I tried their chicken tikka masala, which was cooked to perfection and had just the right amount of spice. The naan bread was also fresh and delicious. Overall, I would highly recommend Curry House for anyone looking for a great Indian dining experience.

Saraya Art Cafe

If you are looking for a more unique dining experience, Saraya Art Cafe is the perfect choice. This restaurant is not only known for its delicious Indian cuisine, but also for its artistic ambiance. The walls are adorned with beautiful paintings and artwork, making it a great spot for a romantic dinner or a night out with friends.

During my visit to Saraya Art Cafe, I tried their butter chicken and was blown away by the flavor. The chicken was tender and juicy, and the sauce was rich and creamy. The restaurant also has a great selection of cocktails and mocktails, making it a great spot for a night out.

The Fisherman's Wharf

While The Fisherman's Wharf is known for its seafood dishes, they also serve some of the best Indian food in North Goa. The restaurant is located in Panaji and has a beautiful waterfront view. The menu features a mix of Indian and Goan cuisine, with a focus on fresh seafood.

During my visit to The Fisherman's Wharf, I tried their prawn curry and was blown away by the flavor. The prawns were cooked to perfection and the curry had just the right amount of spice. The restaurant also has a great selection of cocktails and mocktails, making it a great spot for a night out with friends.
