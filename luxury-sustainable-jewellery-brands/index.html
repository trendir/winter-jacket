Luxury Sustainable Jewellery Brands Making A Statement While Saving The Planet

Written by Aprilia Sep 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you someone who loves to make a statement with your jewellery, but also wants to do your part in saving the planet? If you're tired of the fast fashion industry and its harmful impact on the environment, you're not alone.

Many people are looking for ways to shop sustainably, and when it comes to jewellery, luxury sustainable jewellery brands are the answer.

So, what exactly are luxury sustainable jewellery brands? These are jewellery brands that use sustainable and ethical practices in the production of their products. This means that they use recycled or ethically sourced materials, and they work with fair trade practices to ensure that their workers are treated fairly.

In this article, we'll be discussing some of the best luxury sustainable jewellery brands out there, as well as why it's important to support them.

The Importance of Luxury Sustainable Jewellery Brands

When it comes to sustainability, every little bit counts. By supporting luxury sustainable jewellery brands, you're making a conscious effort to reduce your carbon footprint and support ethical practices.

Additionally, by investing in high-quality sustainable jewellery pieces, you're reducing the need to continuously purchase cheaper, lower-quality pieces that need to be replaced frequently. This not only saves you money in the long run, but it also reduces the amount of waste that ends up in landfills.

Eco-Friendly Jewellery Brands to Consider

1. Monica Vinader - This British brand uses recycled silver and gold in their pieces, and they work with suppliers who adhere to strict ethical standards. They're also committed to using sustainable packaging.

2. Mejuri - This Canadian brand uses ethical and sustainable practices in their production, and they're committed to reducing their carbon footprint. Their pieces are also made to last, reducing the need for constant replacement.

3. Article22 - This brand uses upcycled materials, including shrapnel from bombs in Laos, to create unique and sustainable pieces. They also work with artisans in developing countries to support fair trade practices.

Why We Love Vrai & Oro

One of our favourite luxury sustainable jewellery brands is Vrai & Oro. This California-based brand is committed to using only ethically sourced materials and fair trade practices in their production.

They also have a unique business model - they sell their pieces directly to consumers, cutting out the middleman and reducing costs. This allows them to offer high-quality pieces at a lower price point.

But what we love most about Vrai & Oro is their commitment to transparency. They share all of their production costs and markups on their website, so customers can see exactly where their money is going.

A Guide to Shopping Sustainably

If you're new to shopping sustainably, it can be overwhelming to know where to start. Here are some tips for shopping for sustainable jewellery:

1. Look for brands that use recycled or ethically sourced materials.

2. Consider the brand's commitment to sustainability and ethical practices.

3. Choose high-quality pieces that are made to last.

4. Consider purchasing from brands that offer transparency about their production costs and markups.

Question and Answer

Q: Are luxury sustainable jewellery brands more expensive than traditional jewellery brands?

A: While some luxury sustainable jewellery brands can be more expensive than traditional brands, it's important to remember that you're investing in high-quality pieces that are made to last. Additionally, by reducing the need for constant replacement, you can actually save money in the long run.

Q: Are there any sustainable jewellery brands that use diamonds?

A: Yes, there are sustainable jewellery brands that use ethically sourced diamonds, such as Vrai & Oro and Brilliant Earth.

Q: How can I ensure that a brand is truly sustainable?

A: Look for brands that are transparent about their production processes and materials. Additionally, look for certifications such as Fairtrade and B Corp, which indicate a commitment to ethical and sustainable practices.

Q: Can I recycle my old jewellery?

A: Yes, many jewellery brands offer recycling programs for old jewellery. Additionally, you can sell or donate your old jewellery to reduce waste.

Conclusion of Luxury Sustainable Jewellery Brands

Investing in luxury sustainable jewellery brands is not only a way to make a statement with your accessories, but it's also a way to support ethical and sustainable practices. By choosing high-quality pieces that are made to last, you're reducing waste and supporting brands that are committed to doing their part in saving the planet.
