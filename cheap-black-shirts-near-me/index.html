Question and Answer

Q: How can I tell if a cheap black shirt is good quality?

A: Look for shirts that are made from high-quality materials, such as cotton or rayon. Check the seams and stitching to make sure they are strong and well-made. You may also want to read reviews or ask friends for recommendations to ensure you're getting a good product.

Q: Can I wear a black shirt to work?

A: In many industries, black shirts are considered appropriate work attire. However, it's always a good idea to check your company's dress code policy before wearing one to work.
