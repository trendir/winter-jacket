East Indian dresses are colorful and intricately designed garments that are worn by women and men for special occasions, such as weddings and religious ceremonies. They are a cultural symbol of India and are beloved by many who appreciate their beauty and cultural significance.

For those who are not familiar with Indian culture, the term "East Indian dresses" can be confusing and intimidating. Many people are unsure of what these dresses are called and how they are worn, which can make it difficult to appreciate their beauty and cultural significance.

So, what are East Indian dresses called? The answer is not simple, as there are many different types of dresses that are worn in India, each with their own unique style and name. Some of the most popular types of East Indian dresses include sarees, salwar kameez, and lehenga choli.

In this article, we will explore the world of East Indian dresses and provide you with a comprehensive guide to these beautiful garments. We will discuss the different types of East Indian dresses, their cultural significance, and how to wear them properly.

Understanding Sarees

One of the most popular types of East Indian dresses is the saree. This is a long piece of fabric that is draped around the body and worn with a blouse and petticoat. Sarees come in many different colors and designs, and they are often made from silk or cotton.

When I was a young girl, I remember watching my mother and grandmother wear sarees for special occasions. I was always amazed by the way they could wrap the fabric around their bodies so elegantly, and I loved the way the fabric flowed as they walked.

Wearing a saree can be a bit tricky for those who are not familiar with the garment. It takes practice to get the draping just right, but once you have mastered the technique, the result is stunning.

Exploring Salwar Kameez

Another popular type of East Indian dress is the salwar kameez. This is a more casual outfit that consists of a long tunic (the kameez) and loose-fitting pants (the salwar). The outfit is often paired with a scarf (the dupatta) that is draped over the head and shoulders.

I remember wearing salwar kameez as a child when I visited my relatives in India. It was a comfortable and practical outfit that was perfect for playing and exploring.

Today, salwar kameez is a popular choice for women who want to be comfortable while still looking stylish. The outfit comes in many different colors and designs, and it can be dressed up or down depending on the occasion.

Understanding Lehenga Choli

Lehenga choli is a type of East Indian dress that is often worn for weddings and other formal events. It consists of a long skirt (the lehenga), a short blouse (the choli), and a scarf (the dupatta).

The lehenga choli is designed to be elegant and sophisticated, and it often features intricate embroidery and beading. When I wore a lehenga choli for my cousin's wedding, I felt like a princess.

Wearing a lehenga choli can be a bit challenging, as the skirt is quite heavy and requires some practice to walk in gracefully. However, once you get the hang of it, the outfit is truly stunning.

Tips for Wearing East Indian Dresses

If you are new to wearing East Indian dresses, there are a few tips that can help you feel more comfortable and confident:

  • Practice draping a saree or dupatta before the event so that you feel confident in your ability to wear it.
  • Choose styles and colors that you feel comfortable in, rather than trying to follow the latest fashion trends.
  • Accessorize with jewelry, shoes, and a clutch purse to complete the look.
  • Consider wearing a bindi (a small dot of color on the forehead) to add a touch of traditional Indian style to your outfit.

Question & Answer

Q: What are some other types of East Indian dresses?

A: Other types of East Indian dresses include anarkali suits, patiala suits, and dhoti kurtas.

Q: Can men wear East Indian dresses?

A: Yes, men can wear traditional East Indian garments such as kurta pajama and sherwani.

Q: Are there any cultural considerations when wearing East Indian dresses?

A: It is important to be respectful of Indian culture and customs when wearing East Indian dresses. For example, it is customary to cover your head during religious ceremonies.

Q: Where can I purchase East Indian dresses?

A: East Indian dresses can be purchased online or in specialty stores that cater to Indian clothing. It is important to choose a reputable retailer that sells high-quality garments.

Conclusion of What Are East Indian Dresses Called

East Indian dresses are a beautiful and important part of Indian culture. They come in many different styles and designs, and they are worn for special occasions and everyday wear. Whether you are a longtime fan of these garments or are just learning about them for the first time, we hope this guide has helped you understand what East Indian dresses are called and how to wear them with confidence.
