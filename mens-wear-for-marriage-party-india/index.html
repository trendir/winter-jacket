Men s Wear For Marriage Party In India A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Petter Nov 04, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for the perfect outfit for a marriage party in India? Do you want to look stylish and traditional at the same time? Choosing the right attire for a marriage party can be a daunting task, especially for men who are not well-versed in Indian fashion. In this article, we will guide you through the process of selecting the perfect men's wear for a marriage party in India.

The first challenge that men face while selecting their outfit for a marriage party is to strike a balance between style and comfort. Indian weddings are long, and you don't want to be stuck in an uncomfortable outfit for hours. At the same time, you want to make a statement with your outfit and look your best. Another challenge is to select an outfit that matches the theme of the wedding and the dress code.

The target of men's wear for a marriage party in India is to choose an outfit that is comfortable, stylish, and appropriate for the occasion. The outfit should match the theme of the wedding and the dress code. It should also reflect your personal style and taste.

In conclusion, when selecting men's wear for a marriage party in India, it is essential to keep in mind the comfort, style, appropriateness, and personal taste. Now, let's dive deeper into the topic and explore some popular men's wear options for a marriage party in India.

1. Sherwani

Sherwani is one of the most popular and traditional outfits for men at a marriage party in India. A sherwani is a long coat-like garment that is worn over a kurta and churidar or pajama. It is usually made of silk or brocade and is adorned with intricate embroidery and embellishments. A sherwani gives a regal and sophisticated look to the wearer.

My personal experience with sherwani is that it is a perfect outfit for a formal and traditional marriage party. I wore a black sherwani with gold embroidery to my cousin's wedding, and I received many compliments for my outfit.

2. Bandhgala

Bandhgala, also known as Jodhpuri suit, is a modern take on the traditional Indian outfit. It is a coat-like garment that is worn over a shirt and trousers. It is usually made of silk or wool and is adorned with buttons and pockets. A bandhgala gives a modern and elegant look to the wearer.

My personal experience with bandhgala is that it is a perfect outfit for a semi-formal and modern marriage party. I wore a navy blue bandhgala with silver buttons to my friend's wedding, and I felt comfortable and stylish at the same time.

3. Kurta Pyjama

Kurta pyjama is a traditional Indian outfit that is popular among men of all ages. It is a long tunic-like garment that is worn over a pair of loose pants. It is usually made of cotton or silk and is adorned with simple embroidery or prints. A kurta pyjama gives a comfortable and casual look to the wearer.

If you are attending a casual or daytime marriage party, kurta pyjama is a perfect outfit choice. You can choose a cotton kurta pyjama in pastel colors for a summery look or a silk kurta pyjama in darker colors for a more formal look.

4. Indo-Western Outfits

If you want to experiment with your outfit and add a modern twist to the traditional Indian outfit, you can opt for Indo-western outfits. Indo-western outfits are a fusion of Indian and western wear, and they give a contemporary and trendy look to the wearer. You can choose from a range of options such as fusion jackets, dhoti pants, and Nehru jackets.

If you want to stand out from the crowd and make a statement with your outfit, Indo-western outfits are a perfect choice. You can choose a fusion jacket in bold colors or a dhoti pant with a modern print to add a unique touch to your outfit.

Question and Answer

Q. What colors should I avoid wearing to a marriage party in India?

A. It is best to avoid wearing black and white to a marriage party as they are considered inauspicious. You can opt for vibrant colors such as red, blue, or green to add a pop of color to your outfit.

Q. Can I wear a suit to a marriage party in India?

A. It is not common to wear a suit to a marriage party in India as it is considered a western outfit. However, if you want to wear a suit, you can opt for an Indo-western suit that has a fusion of Indian and western elements.

Q. Should I wear a turban with my outfit?

A. If you are attending a Sikh wedding, it is customary to wear a turban as a sign of respect. However, if you are attending a Hindu wedding, it is not necessary to wear a turban.

Q. Can I wear jeans to a marriage party in India?

A. It is best to avoid wearing jeans to a marriage party in India as they are considered too casual. You can opt for trousers or churidar instead.

Conclusion of Men's Wear for Marriage Party in India

Choosing the right men's wear for a marriage party in India can be a challenging task, but with the right guidance, you can select the perfect outfit that suits your style and taste. Whether you opt for a traditional sherwani or a modern bandhgala, make sure that your outfit is comfortable, appropriate, and reflects your personal style. By following the tips and suggestions mentioned in this article, you can make a statement with your outfit and look your best at the marriage party.
