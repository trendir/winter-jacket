Most Ethical Mens Clothing Brands

Written by William Aug 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you concerned about the impact your clothing choices have on the environment and workers involved in the manufacturing process? Do you want to support companies that prioritize sustainability, fair labor practices, and ethical sourcing? If so, you're not alone. Many consumers are seeking out more ethical options when it comes to fashion, and this includes men's clothing brands.

It can be difficult to navigate the world of ethical fashion, with so many different certifications, standards, and claims being made. Additionally, some ethical brands may be more expensive or harder to find than their fast fashion counterparts. However, by making conscious choices and investing in quality pieces, you can build a wardrobe that aligns with your values.

So, what should you look for in a men's clothing brand that prioritizes ethics? First and foremost, transparency. Brands that are truly committed to ethical practices will be upfront about their sourcing, production, and labor practices. They will likely have certifications from reputable organizations such as Fair Trade, GOTS (Global Organic Textile Standard), or the Better Cotton Initiative. Additionally, they will prioritize sustainable materials and processes, such as using organic cotton or recycled fibers, and minimizing water usage and carbon emissions.

In this article, we'll explore some of the most ethical men's clothing brands on the market, and what makes them stand out. By supporting these brands, you can feel good about your fashion choices and contribute to a more sustainable and just industry.

Patagonia

Patagonia is a well-known outdoor brand that has long been committed to environmental and social responsibility. They prioritize fair labor practices, using organic cotton and recycled materials, and minimizing waste and emissions. In addition to their popular outerwear and activewear, they also offer a range of men's clothing such as t-shirts, sweaters, and pants. I personally love their fleeces and wear them all the time in colder weather.

Everlane

Everlane is a brand that is all about transparency. They break down the cost of each item on their website, showing customers exactly how much they pay for materials, labor, and transportation. They also prioritize ethical factories and fair wages for workers. Their men's clothing includes basics like t-shirts and jeans, as well as more elevated pieces like cashmere sweaters and leather jackets.

Veja

Veja is a French sneaker brand that has gained a following for its sustainable materials and fair labor practices. Their sneakers are made from organic cotton, wild rubber from the Amazon rainforest, and leather that is produced in a way that minimizes water usage and chemical waste. They also prioritize working directly with small-scale producers and cooperatives, to ensure fair wages and working conditions. I love my Veja sneakers and they're a great conversation starter.

Organic Basics

Organic Basics is a brand that focuses on sustainable and ethical underwear, socks, and activewear. They use organic cotton, recycled nylon, and Tencel (a sustainably produced wood fiber) in their products, and prioritize transparent and ethical supply chains. Their men's underwear and socks are some of the softest and most comfortable I've ever worn.

Question and Answer

Q: Why is it important to support ethical men's clothing brands?

A: By supporting ethical men's clothing brands, you are contributing to a more sustainable and just fashion industry. You are also sending a message to fast fashion companies that you care about the impact of your clothing choices on people and the planet.

Q: Are ethical men's clothing brands more expensive than fast fashion brands?

A: In some cases, yes. However, investing in quality pieces that are made to last can actually save you money in the long run. Additionally, some ethical brands may offer sales or discounts, and there are also secondhand options available.

Q: How can I find ethical men's clothing brands?

A: Look for certifications from reputable organizations such as Fair Trade, GOTS, or the Better Cotton Initiative. Do some research on a brand's website to learn about their sourcing and labor practices. You can also look for recommendations from ethical fashion bloggers and influencers.

Q: Can I still be stylish while wearing ethical men's clothing?

A: Absolutely! Many ethical brands offer on-trend and stylish pieces, while also prioritizing sustainability and fair labor practices. Plus, by investing in quality pieces, you may find that your wardrobe is more versatile and timeless.

Conclusion of Most Ethical Mens Clothing Brands

By supporting ethical men's clothing brands, you can feel good about your fashion choices and contribute to a more sustainable and just industry. Brands such as Patagonia, Everlane, Veja, and Organic Basics prioritize transparency, sustainable materials, and fair labor practices. By investing in quality pieces that are made to last, you can build a wardrobe that aligns with your values and looks great too.
