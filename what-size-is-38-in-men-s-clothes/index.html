What Size Is 38 In Men s Clothes

Written by Frank Aug 10, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you confused about what size is 38 in men's clothes? You're not alone. Many men struggle with finding the right size when shopping for clothes, especially when it comes to international sizes. It can be frustrating to buy something that doesn't fit properly, and it can also be time-consuming to exchange or return items. However, with a little bit of knowledge, you can find the perfect fit for your body type.

Size 38 in men's clothes is usually considered a medium or large size, depending on the brand. It's important to note that sizes can vary between different clothing manufacturers, so always check the size chart before making a purchase. This can help you avoid the hassle of returning or exchanging items that don't fit properly.

In general, a size 38 in men's clothes is equivalent to a size medium in the US and UK, and a size large in European sizes. However, it's always best to check the size chart of the specific brand you're interested in to ensure the best fit for your body type.

To summarize, size 38 in men's clothes is typically a medium or large size, depending on the brand. Always check the size chart before making a purchase to ensure the best fit for your body type.

How to Measure Yourself for Size 38 in Men's Clothes?

Measuring yourself for size 38 in men's clothes is easy. All you need is a tape measure and a few minutes of your time. Here's how to do it:

1. Measure your chest: Wrap the tape measure around the fullest part of your chest, under your armpits and across your shoulder blades. Make sure the tape measure is parallel to the ground and not too tight or too loose.

2. Measure your waist: Wrap the tape measure around your natural waistline, which is located above your belly button and below your rib cage. Make sure the tape measure is snug but not too tight.

3. Measure your hips: Wrap the tape measure around the widest part of your hips, usually around your buttocks. Make sure the tape measure is parallel to the ground and not too tight or too loose.

4. Compare your measurements to the size chart: Once you have your measurements, compare them to the size chart of the brand you're interested in. This will help you determine the best size for your body type.

Tips for Buying Clothes in Size 38 for Men

Here are some tips for buying clothes in size 38 for men:

- Always check the size chart before making a purchase.

- Try on clothes before buying them if possible.

- Consider the fabric of the clothing, as some materials may shrink or stretch over time.

- Don't be afraid to ask for help from a sales associate or tailor.
