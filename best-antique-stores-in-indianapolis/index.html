Discover The Best Antique Stores In Indianapolis For Your Next Treasure Hunt

Written by Luffy Jul 03, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you an antique enthusiast looking for the best antique stores in Indianapolis? Do you enjoy the thrill of the hunt and finding unique treasures from the past? Finding the right antique store can be challenging and overwhelming, especially if you’re new to the area. But don’t worry, we’ve got you covered!

Indianapolis is home to some of the best antique stores that offer a diverse range of antique items from furniture, jewelry, collectibles, and more. However, with so many options, it can be difficult to know where to start your search.

In this blog post, we’ll guide you through some of the best antique stores in Indianapolis and help you find your next hidden gem.

Antique Mall of Irvington

The Antique Mall of Irvington is a must-visit for antique lovers in Indianapolis. This antique mall offers a wide variety of antique items from different eras, including furniture, jewelry, and vintage clothing. The mall has over 70 vendors, making it one of the largest antique malls in the city.

What makes the Antique Mall of Irvington stand out is its unique location. The mall is located in the historic Irvington neighborhood, which adds to the charm and vintage appeal of the antique mall. You can spend hours browsing through the different booths and vendors, each offering something unique and one-of-a-kind.

The Southport Antique Mall

The Southport Antique Mall is another popular antique store in Indianapolis. This antique mall has been in business for over 20 years and offers a wide range of antique items, including furniture, lamps, and vintage collectibles. The mall has over 200 vendors, making it one of the largest antique malls in the state of Indiana.

What sets the Southport Antique Mall apart from other antique stores is its exceptional customer service. The staff is knowledgeable and friendly, always willing to help you find the perfect item for your home or collection.

Midland Arts & Antiques Market

The Midland Arts & Antiques Market is not your typical antique store. This store offers a wide range of antique and vintage items, including furniture, artwork, and collectibles, but also features a rotating selection of local art and handmade crafts.

What makes the Midland Arts & Antiques Market unique is its dedication to supporting local artists and makers. You can find unique pieces that you won’t find anywhere else, making it a great place to shop for gifts or to add something special to your own collection.

The Indy Antique Advertising Show

If you’re looking for something specific, like vintage advertising signs or old soda bottles, the Indy Antique Advertising Show is the perfect place to start your search. This annual show features over 70 vendors from across the country, each offering a unique selection of antique advertising items.

The Indy Antique Advertising Show is held at the Boone County Fairgrounds in Lebanon, Indiana, about 30 minutes from downtown Indianapolis. The show is held twice a year, in March and October, and is a must-visit for antique advertising enthusiasts.

Conclusion of Best Antique Stores in Indianapolis

Indianapolis is a treasure trove for antique lovers, with some of the best antique stores in the country. From antique malls to unique boutiques, there’s something for everyone in Indianapolis. Whether you’re a seasoned collector or just starting your collection, you’re sure to find something special at one of these antique stores.
