Types of Kurta Pajama

There are several types of kurta pajama worn by Indian men, depending on the occasion and region. Some of the most popular types include:

Sherwani Kurta Pajama

The sherwani kurta pajama is a more formal version of the traditional outfit. It is typically made of silk or satin and is heavily embroidered with gold or silver thread. This attire is typically worn for weddings and other formal events.

Pathani Kurta Pajama

The pathani kurta pajama is a popular style worn by men in northern India. It is a loose-fitting outfit that is comfortable and easy to wear. This attire is typically made of cotton and is often worn for casual events.

Dhoti Kurta Pajama

The dhoti kurta pajama is a traditional outfit worn by men in southern India. It consists of a long tunic and a wrap-around cloth called a dhoti. The dhoti is typically made of cotton and is draped around the waist and legs. This attire is typically worn for weddings and other formal events.
