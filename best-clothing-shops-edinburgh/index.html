The Best Clothing Shops In Edinburgh

Written by Bardi Jul 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you planning a trip to Edinburgh and looking for the best clothing shops? Perhaps you're a local looking to update your wardrobe? Either way, finding the perfect clothing shop can be a daunting task. With so many options available, it's easy to feel overwhelmed and unsure of where to start.

When it comes to finding the best clothing shops in Edinburgh, there are a few things to consider. Firstly, think about your personal style and what kind of clothing you're looking for. Are you after high-end designer labels or more affordable high-street options? Do you prefer vintage or modern styles? Secondly, consider the location of the shops. Are you looking for a specific area or street? Finally, think about your budget. Edinburgh has a range of clothing shops to suit all budgets, so it's important to set a realistic one.

In this article, we'll take a look at some of the best clothing shops in Edinburgh. From designer boutiques to vintage shops, we've got you covered. Keep reading to find out more.

The Best Clothing Shops in Edinburgh for Designer Labels

One of the best places to start your search for designer clothing in Edinburgh is Multrees Walk. Located in the heart of the city, this luxury shopping destination is home to a range of high-end designer brands such as Louis Vuitton, Gucci, and Burberry. If you're looking for something a little more niche, head to Harvey Nichols. This department store stocks a range of designer labels including Alexander McQueen, Givenchy, and Stella McCartney. You can also find designer labels in some of the high-end boutiques on George Street.

The Best Clothing Shops in Edinburgh for Vintage Clothing

Edinburgh is home to a range of vintage clothing shops, perfect for those looking for unique and one-of-a-kind pieces. Armstrong's on Grassmarket is a popular choice for vintage lovers. This shop stocks a range of vintage clothing, accessories and even vinyl records. Another great option is Herman Brown on Broughton Street. This vintage shop stocks a range of clothing from the 1940s to the 1980s, as well as a selection of vintage jewellery and accessories.

The Best Clothing Shops in Edinburgh for Affordable High-Street Fashion

If you're on a budget, don't worry. Edinburgh has a range of high-street shops to suit all budgets. Princes Street is home to a range of high-street shops including H&M, Topshop and Zara. Alternatively, head to the St James Centre where you'll find a range of shops including New Look and River Island.

The Best Clothing Shops in Edinburgh for Scottish Fashion

Looking for something a little more traditional? Head to a shop that stocks Scottish fashion. One of the best is Walker Slater on Victoria Street. This shop stocks a range of traditional Scottish clothing including kilts, tweed jackets and cashmere scarves. Alternatively, head to ScotlandShop on Queensferry Street where you can design your own tartan.

Conclusion of the Best Clothing Shops in Edinburgh

Edinburgh has a range of clothing shops to suit all styles and budgets. Whether you're looking for designer labels or affordable high-street fashion, you're sure to find something to suit your taste. So, what are you waiting for? Start exploring Edinburgh's clothing shops today!

Question and Answer

Q: Where can I find designer labels in Edinburgh?

A: Head to Multrees Walk or Harvey Nichols for high-end designer labels.

Q: What are some of the best vintage clothing shops in Edinburgh?

A: Armstrong's on Grassmarket and Herman Brown on Broughton Street are both great options for vintage clothing.

Q: Where can I find affordable high-street fashion in Edinburgh?

A: Princes Street and the St James Centre are both home to a range of high-street shops.

Q: Where can I find traditional Scottish clothing in Edinburgh?

A: Walker Slater on Victoria Street and ScotlandShop on Queensferry Street both stock traditional Scottish clothing.
