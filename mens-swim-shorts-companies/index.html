Top Mens Swim Shorts Companies To Check Out

Written by Oliver May 25, 2023 · 6 min read

Summer is here, and it's time to hit the beach or pool. But before you do, you need to get the perfect swim shorts that will make you feel comfortable, confident, and stylish. However, finding the right mens swim shorts companies can be overwhelming, as there are so many options out there. Don't worry, in this post, we'll guide you through the top mens swim shorts companies that you should check out.

When it comes to swim shorts, there are a few things that can be problematic for men. For instance, some shorts can be too tight or too loose, making it uncomfortable to wear or swim in. Others can be too short, revealing more than what you'd like to show. Plus, there's the issue of finding a design that suits your taste and personality. All of these factors can make shopping for men's swim shorts a daunting task.

The target of mens swim shorts companies is to provide men with high-quality, comfortable, and stylish swimwear that will make them feel confident and ready for any water activity. These companies specialize in creating swim shorts that are not only functional and practical but also fashionable and trendy. They use premium materials, innovative designs, and advanced technologies to ensure that their customers get the best value for their money.

To summarize, mens swim shorts companies aim to solve the pain points of finding the right swimwear for men. They offer a wide range of options that cater to different body types, preferences, and budgets. They also prioritize quality, comfort, and style, making sure that their products meet the demands and expectations of modern men.

1. Vilebrequin

One of the most popular mens swim shorts companies is Vilebrequin, a French brand that has been around since 1971. Vilebrequin offers a wide range of swimwear for men, women, and children, but it's most famous for its men's swim shorts. The brand is known for its vibrant colors, bold patterns, and playful designs that capture the essence of the French Riviera lifestyle.

I recently bought a pair of Vilebrequin swim shorts, and I was impressed by the quality and attention to detail. The shorts are made of a quick-drying fabric that feels soft and comfortable against the skin. They also have an adjustable waistband, a supportive mesh lining, and a handy back pocket. But what I love the most about these shorts is the unique design that features turtles swimming in the ocean. It's a fun and whimsical print that always gets compliments.

2. Orlebar Brown

Another iconic brand in the mens swim shorts industry is Orlebar Brown. Founded in 2007, Orlebar Brown has become synonymous with tailored swimwear that combines functionality and style. The brand's signature swim shorts are designed to resemble classic tailored shorts, with a fitted waistband, side fasteners, and a zip fly. This design allows men to wear the shorts not only for swimming but also for other outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, or playing beach volleyball.

I remember the first time I tried Orlebar Brown swim shorts, and I was blown away by the quality and versatility. The shorts are made of a quick-drying fabric that doesn't cling to the skin or chafe. They also have a flattering silhouette that accentuates the legs and buttocks without being too tight or too loose. I particularly like the Bulldog style, which has a shorter inseam and a more sporty look.

3. Sundek

If you're looking for a more affordable option, Sundek is a great mens swim shorts company that offers quality swimwear at a reasonable price. Sundek was founded in the 1950s in California and has since expanded globally, becoming a favorite among surfers, skaters, and beachgoers. Sundek's swim shorts are known for their bright colors, retro designs, and durable construction.

I tried Sundek swim shorts during my last beach vacation, and I was pleasantly surprised by how comfortable and stylish they were. The shorts are made of a lightweight fabric that dries quickly and doesn't weigh you down. They also have a comfortable elastic waistband, a supportive mesh lining, and a fun rainbow logo on the back. I would highly recommend Sundek swim shorts for anyone who wants a fun and functional swimwear option.

4. Boardies

Boardies is a British brand that offers a range of mens swim shorts that are perfect for the modern man. Boardies swim shorts are designed with a contemporary and minimalist aesthetic that appeals to men who prefer a sleek and understated look. The brand uses high-quality fabrics, such as recycled polyester and organic cotton, to create swimwear that is eco-friendly and sustainable.

I recently discovered Boardies swim shorts, and I was impressed by the simplicity and elegance of their design. The shorts are made of a soft and stretchy fabric that moves with your body and doesn't restrict your movements. They also have a hidden drawstring, a comfortable mesh lining, and a minimal logo on the leg. I would recommend Boardies swim shorts for anyone who wants a timeless and versatile swimwear option.

Question and Answer Section about Mens Swim Shorts Companies

Q1: How do I know if a mens swim shorts company is reputable?

A1: You can check the brand's reviews, ratings, and customer feedback online to get a sense of their reputation. You can also look for certifications or awards that the brand has received for quality, sustainability, or innovation.

Q2: What should I look for when buying mens swim shorts?

A2: You should look for factors such as fit, fabric, design, and features. Make sure the shorts fit your body type and style preferences, and that the fabric is quick-drying, lightweight, and comfortable. Look for designs that reflect your personality and taste, and features such as pockets, waistbands, and linings that enhance the functionality and practicality of the shorts.

Q3: How much should I spend on mens swim shorts?

A3: The price of mens swim shorts depends on various factors such as brand, style, fabric, and features. You can find swim shorts that range from $20 to $500, depending on your budget and requirements. However, it's important to invest in high-quality swimwear that will last you for multiple seasons and withstand the wear and tear of saltwater, chlorine, and sun exposure.

Q4: Can I wear mens swim shorts for activities other than swimming?

A4: Yes, many mens swim shorts companies design their shorts to be versatile and suitable for other outdoor activities such as running, hiking, or playing sports. You can also wear swim shorts as casual or beachwear, paired with a t-shirt, tank top, or flip flops.

Conclusion of Mens Swim Shorts Companies

Shopping for mens swim shorts can be overwhelming, but with the right guidance and knowledge, you can find the perfect pair that will make you feel confident and ready for any water activity. Make sure to check out the top mens swim shorts companies such as Vilebrequin, Orlebar Brown, Sundek, and Boardies, and choose the brand that suits your style, budget, and needs. Remember to prioritize quality, comfort, and style, and invest in swimwear that will last you for multiple seasons and make you look and feel your best.
