The Best Fashion School In Ghana A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Oct 28, 2023 · 3 min read

Are you passionate about fashion and looking to turn your dreams into a reality? Choosing the right fashion school is crucial to achieving success in this industry. However, with so many options available, it can be challenging to determine what is the best fashion school in Ghana.

If you are struggling with finding the right fashion school in Ghana, you are not alone. Many individuals face this challenge and often end up making the wrong choice, which can lead to wasted time and money.

So, what is the best fashion school in Ghana?

After thorough research and analysis, Radford University College School of Fashion and Design (RUFD) stands out as the best fashion school in Ghana.

Why RUFD is the Best Fashion School in Ghana

As a graduate of RUFD, I have first-hand experience of the high-quality education and training provided at the school. RUFD offers a wide range of fashion programs, including fashion design, fashion marketing, and fashion merchandising.

The school boasts of modern facilities and equipment, experienced lecturers, and a conducive learning environment. RUFD also has partnerships with international fashion schools, providing students with opportunities to study abroad and gain global exposure.

RUFD's curriculum is designed to equip students with practical skills and knowledge to excel in the fashion industry. The school also provides a platform for students to showcase their designs through various fashion shows organized on and off-campus.

RUFD Admission Requirements

To gain admission into RUFD, applicants must have a minimum of six credits, including English and Mathematics, in their WASSCE or its equivalent. Applicants must also pass the school's entrance examination and submit a portfolio of their work.

Cost of Studying at RUFD

Studying at RUFD is affordable compared to other fashion schools in Ghana. The school offers various payment options, including installment payments, to ease the financial burden on students.

Conclusion

Choosing the right fashion school is essential to achieving success in the fashion industry. RUFD stands out as the best fashion school in Ghana, offering students quality education, practical training, and global exposure.
