Interior Designer Average Salary Australia A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Robby Oct 25, 2023 · 4 min read

If you're considering a career as an interior designer in Australia, you may be wondering about the average salary for this profession. As with any job, salary is an important factor to consider when making career decisions, and it can be a major pain point for many people.

So, what is the average salary for interior designers in Australia? The answer to this question can vary depending on a range of factors, including your level of experience, your location, and the type of employer you work for.

Overall, however, the average salary for interior designers in Australia is around $65,000 per year. This figure can vary depending on a range of factors, but it gives you a good starting point for understanding what you might expect to earn in this profession.

In this article, we'll break down the factors that influence interior designer salaries in Australia, and we'll give you a better understanding of how much you can expect to earn if you choose this career path.

Factors Affecting Interior Designer Salaries in Australia

When it comes to interior designer salaries in Australia, there are a number of factors that can influence how much you can expect to earn. Some of the key factors to consider include:

Experience

As with any profession, your level of experience can have a significant impact on your earning potential as an interior designer. Generally speaking, the more experience you have, the higher your salary will be. This is because experienced designers are able to command higher rates due to their expertise and skills.

Location

The location where you work can also have a significant impact on your earning potential as an interior designer. In general, interior designers who work in major cities such as Sydney or Melbourne can expect to earn more due to the higher cost of living in these areas.

Type of Employer

Finally, the type of employer you work for can also affect your salary as an interior designer. For example, designers who work for large architecture or design firms may earn more than those who work for small boutique firms or freelancers.

My Personal Experience with Interior Designer Salaries in Australia

As an interior designer with several years of experience, I can attest to the fact that salaries in this profession can vary widely based on a range of factors. In my experience, designers who work in major cities such as Sydney or Melbourne can expect to earn more than those who work in smaller regional areas.

Additionally, designers who work for large firms or corporations may earn more than those who work for small boutique firms or freelancers. However, it's important to note that this can vary depending on the specific employer and the designer's level of experience.

How to Increase Your Interior Designer Salary in Australia

If you're looking to increase your earning potential as an interior designer in Australia, there are a few steps you can take:

1. Gain Experience

As mentioned earlier, experience is a key factor when it comes to earning potential in this profession. By gaining more experience and building your skills as a designer, you'll be able to command higher rates and earn more money.

2. Work for a Large Firm

Designers who work for large architecture or design firms may earn more than those who work for smaller companies or freelancers. This is because larger firms often have more resources and can pay their employees higher salaries.

3. Specialize in a High-Demand Area

If you specialize in a high-demand area such as sustainable design or healthcare design, you may be able to command higher rates due to your expertise in these areas.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the average starting salary for interior designers in Australia?

A: The average starting salary for interior designers in Australia is around $50,000 per year.

Q: How much can experienced interior designers in Australia expect to earn?

A: Experienced interior designers in Australia can expect to earn upwards of $100,000 per year, depending on their level of experience, location, and employer.

Q: Is it possible to earn a six-figure salary as an interior designer in Australia?

A: Yes, it is possible to earn a six-figure salary as an interior designer in Australia, particularly if you specialize in a high-demand area and work for a large firm in a major city.

Q: How does the average salary for interior designers in Australia compare to other countries?

A: The average salary for interior designers in Australia is generally higher than in many other countries, including the United States and the United Kingdom.

Conclusion of Interior Designer Average Salary Australia

Overall, the average salary for interior designers in Australia is around $65,000 per year, but this figure can vary depending on a range of factors. By gaining experience, working for a large firm, and specializing in a high-demand area, you can increase your earning potential and enjoy a successful career as an interior designer.
