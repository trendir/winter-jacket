How to Enjoy Crown Royal?

Crown Royal is a versatile whiskey that can be enjoyed in many different ways. It can be sipped neat, on the rocks, or mixed into a cocktail. One of my favorite ways to enjoy Crown Royal is in a classic Manhattan cocktail.

To make a Manhattan with Crown Royal, you'll need:

- 2 oz. Crown Royal

- 1 oz. sweet vermouth

- Dash of Angostura bitters

- Maraschino cherry for garnish

Fill a shaker with ice and add Crown Royal, sweet vermouth, and bitters. Shake well and strain into a chilled glass. Garnish with a maraschino cherry and enjoy!

The History of Crown Royal
