How Much Do Interior Designers Make In Canada

Written by Daniel Aug 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you wondering how much interior designers make in Canada? Are you considering a career in interior design, but aren't sure about the salary prospects? The answer to this question is not straightforward, as interior designers' salaries vary depending on several factors. However, this post will provide you with a general idea of how much interior designers make in Canada.

Interior design is a competitive industry, and it can be challenging to earn a decent living. However, Canadian interior designers can expect to make a median salary of around $50,000 per year, according to PayScale. Factors such as experience, education, location, and employer can impact an interior designer's salary.

In summary, while the salary for interior designers in Canada can vary widely, the median annual salary is approximately $50,000. This post will now delve deeper into understanding what affects an interior designer's salary in Canada.

Experience and Education

Experience and education are significant factors that affect an interior designer's salary. Senior interior designers with more extensive experience and education generally earn higher salaries than entry-level designers. Interior designers with a bachelor's degree or higher typically earn more than those with an associate's degree or diploma.

Personally, I have noticed that my salary has increased significantly since I earned my Bachelor of Interior Design. Having a degree has opened up more opportunities for me and has allowed me to take on more significant projects that pay better.

Location

The location of an interior designer also affects their salary. Interior designers working in major cities, such as Toronto or Vancouver, generally earn higher salaries than those working in smaller cities or rural areas. This is because living expenses are higher in bigger cities, and the cost of living is reflected in salaries.

When I moved from a small town to Toronto, I noticed a significant increase in my salary. However, it's worth noting that the cost of living in Toronto is much higher than in smaller towns, so it's essential to consider living expenses when comparing salaries in different locations.

Employer

The employer an interior designer works for can also affect their salary. Interior designers working for large design firms or corporations generally earn more than those working for small businesses or as self-employed designers. Design firms and corporations typically have more significant budgets and can pay higher salaries.

Personally, I have worked for both a small design firm and a large corporation. While I enjoyed the more personalized experience of working for a small business, the larger corporation paid me a higher salary and had more benefits.

How to Increase Your Salary as an Interior Designer in Canada

If you're an interior designer in Canada looking to increase your salary, there are a few things you can do. Firstly, you can gain more experience and education. Consider pursuing a master's degree or taking on larger projects to gain more experience. Secondly, consider relocating to a larger city or a location with a higher demand for interior designers. Finally, consider working for a larger design firm or corporation.

Conclusion of How Much Do Interior Designers Make in Canada

In conclusion, interior designers in Canada can expect to earn a median salary of around $50,000 per year. However, several factors, such as experience, education, location, and employer, can impact an interior designer's salary. By gaining more experience and education, relocating to a larger city or location with a higher demand for interior designers, and working for a larger design firm or corporation, interior designers in Canada can increase their salary prospects.
