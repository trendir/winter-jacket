What are Sarees Called?

A saree is a long piece of cloth that is draped around the body and over the shoulder. It is one of the most popular Indian garments and is worn by women of all ages. In relaxed English language, sarees are also called "Indian wrap dresses." When you are looking for a saree, you can ask for an Indian wrap dress or simply a wrap dress.

What is a Salwar Kameez Called?

A salwar kameez is a traditional Indian garment worn by women. It consists of a long tunic top (kameez) and baggy pants (salwar) with a scarf (dupatta). In relaxed English language, a salwar kameez is also called "Indian tunic and pants" or "Indian outfit with scarf."

What is a Lehenga Choli Called?

A lehenga choli is a traditional Indian outfit worn by women for weddings and other formal occasions. It consists of a long skirt (lehenga), a short blouse (choli), and a dupatta. In relaxed English language, a lehenga choli is also called "Indian skirt and top with scarf."

How to Wear a Dhoti Kurta?

A dhoti kurta is a traditional Indian outfit worn by men. It consists of a long piece of cloth wrapped around the legs (dhoti) and a long shirt (kurta). In relaxed English language, a dhoti kurta is also called "Indian pants and shirt." To wear a dhoti kurta, you need to wrap the dhoti around your legs and tuck it into the waistband. Then, you wear the kurta over it like a regular shirt.

What is a Sherwani Called?

A sherwani is a traditional Indian outfit worn by men for weddings and other formal occasions. It is a long coat-like garment that is worn over a kurta and churidar pants. In relaxed English language, a sherwani is also called "Indian suit jacket."

Question and Answer

Q: What is a churidar?

A: A churidar is a type of pants worn by both men and women in India. It is similar to leggings but has extra fabric around the ankles that create a bunching effect.

Q: What is a dupatta?

A: A dupatta is a long scarf that is worn by women with traditional Indian outfits such as salwar kameez and lehenga choli. It is draped over the head or around the neck and shoulders.

Q: What is a kurta?

A: A kurta is a long tunic top worn by both men and women in India. It is usually made of cotton or silk and can be worn with pants or a skirt.

Q: What is a bindi?

A: A bindi is a small decorative dot or sticker that is worn on the forehead by women in India. It is a traditional symbol of marriage and is often worn as part of a woman's makeup.
