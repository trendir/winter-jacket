What To Wear To A Garden Wedding 2020 Tips And Ideas

Written by Frank Jun 15, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Attending a garden wedding can be exciting, but figuring out what to wear can be a daunting task. You want to look stylish and comfortable while also adhering to the dress code and practical considerations such as the weather and setting. This blog post offers some tips and ideas on what to wear to a garden wedding in 2020.

If you're wondering what to wear to a garden wedding, the answer depends on several factors such as the time of day, season, formality, and personal style. For a daytime garden wedding, you can opt for a light-colored or floral dress, a skirt and blouse combo, or a jumpsuit with wedges or sandals. For an evening garden wedding, you can choose a more formal dress, such as a long flowy gown or a cocktail dress, with heels or dressy flats. Make sure to check the invitation for any dress code guidelines, and avoid wearing white or anything too revealing or flashy that could upstage the bride or groom.

To sum up, when it comes to what to wear to a garden wedding in 2020, you should aim for comfort, style, and appropriateness. Take into account the time of day, season, and dress code, and choose an outfit that reflects your personal taste while also complementing the surroundings. Now let's dive deeper into some specific tips and ideas.

Tip 1: Embrace Floral Prints and Light Colors

When it comes to garden weddings, floral prints and light colors are always a safe bet. They reflect the natural beauty of the surroundings and add a touch of romanticism to your outfit. You can choose a maxi dress with a floral print, a pastel-colored jumpsuit, or a flowy skirt with a blouse in a light hue. Pair your outfit with wedges, sandals, or block heels to ensure comfort and stability on grassy or uneven surfaces.

Tip 2: Opt for Breathable Fabrics and Layers

Garden weddings can be hot and humid, especially in the summer months. To avoid feeling uncomfortable and sweaty, choose breathable fabrics such as cotton, linen, or silk. You can also consider wearing layers, such as a light cardigan or a scarf, that you can easily remove if the temperature rises. Keep in mind that the weather can change quickly, so it's better to be prepared than sorry.

Tip 3: Consider the Footwear

When it comes to footwear for a garden wedding, you want to strike a balance between style and practicality. Avoid stilettos or any shoes with thin heels that can sink into the ground or get stuck in between the grass. Instead, choose wedges, block heels, or sandals that provide stability and comfort. If you're not a fan of open-toe shoes, you can opt for dressy flats or pumps with a wider heel.

Tip 4: Accessorize with Statement Pieces

To elevate your garden wedding outfit and make it more memorable, you can add some statement accessories that reflect your personality and style. You can choose a bold necklace, a pair of chandelier earrings, or a wide-brimmed hat that matches your dress or jumpsuit. However, make sure not to overdo it and keep the focus on the overall look rather than individual pieces.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I wear black to a garden wedding?

A: While black is a versatile and chic color, it's not typically associated with garden weddings, which tend to be more colorful and light-hearted. If you must wear black, make sure to add some pops of color or metallic accents to brighten up the outfit.

Q: Is it appropriate to wear a pantsuit to a garden wedding?

A: Yes, a pantsuit can be a stylish and comfortable option for a garden wedding, especially if you're not a fan of dresses or skirts. Just make sure to choose a lightweight and flowy fabric that complements the surroundings and accessorize with dressy shoes and jewelry.

Q: Can I wear flip flops to a garden wedding?

A: While flip flops are comfortable and practical for everyday wear, they're not appropriate for a garden wedding. Opt for dressy sandals or wedges that offer more support and elegance.

Q: Can I wear a fascinator to a garden wedding?

A: Yes, a fascinator can be a stylish and fun accessory for a garden wedding, especially if it matches your outfit and adds some flair to your hair. Just make sure not to choose a fascinator that's too big or overwhelming, and avoid blocking the view of other guests.

Conclusion of What to Wear to a Garden Wedding 2020

Attending a garden wedding can be a delightful experience, as long as you're dressed appropriately and comfortably. By following these tips and ideas on what to wear to a garden wedding in 2020, you can ensure that you look stylish and feel confident while also respecting the couple's wishes and the surroundings. Remember to have fun, enjoy the festivities, and capture some beautiful memories with your loved ones!
