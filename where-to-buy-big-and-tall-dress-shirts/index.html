Are you tired of struggling to find dress shirts that fit your big and tall frame? Do you find yourself settling for ill-fitting shirts that leave you uncomfortable and self-conscious? Finding the right dress shirt can be a daunting task, but fear not! This guide will provide you with all the information you need to know about where to buy big and tall dress shirts.

The struggle of finding dress shirts that fit well and look good is a common pain point for big and tall men. Most stores only carry a limited selection of sizes, leaving men with larger frames feeling frustrated and excluded. This can lead to settling for shirts that don't fit properly, which can be uncomfortable and unflattering.

The good news is that there are plenty of options available for big and tall men looking for dress shirts. There are several stores and online retailers that specialize in extended sizes, making it easier than ever to find shirts that fit well and look great.

To summarize, finding dress shirts for big and tall men can be a challenge, but there are plenty of options available. In the following sections, we will explore some of the best places to buy big and tall dress shirts, as well as some tips and tricks for finding the perfect fit.

The Best Places to Buy Big and Tall Dress Shirts

One of the best places to start your search for big and tall dress shirts is at a store that specializes in extended sizes. These stores typically carry a wide selection of dress shirts in sizes that are difficult to find elsewhere. Some of the top stores for big and tall dress shirts include:

1. Destination XL

Destination XL, also known as DXL, is one of the largest retailers of men's big and tall clothing. They have a wide selection of dress shirts in extended sizes, as well as a variety of styles and brands to choose from. They also offer free shipping on orders over $100, making it easy to stock up on all your favorite styles.

2. J.C. Penney

J.C. Penney is another great option for big and tall dress shirts. They have a dedicated section on their website for extended sizes, making it easy to find what you're looking for. They also offer a variety of brands and styles to choose from, so you're sure to find something that fits your style and budget.

3. Men's Wearhouse

Men's Wearhouse is a popular retailer for men's clothing, and they also offer a wide selection of dress shirts in extended sizes. They offer a variety of brands and styles, and they also have a custom tailoring service to ensure the perfect fit.

Tips for Finding the Perfect Fit

When shopping for big and tall dress shirts, it's important to pay attention to the fit. Here are some tips to help you find the perfect shirt:

1. Know your measurements

Take your measurements before you start shopping. This will help you find the right size and ensure a comfortable fit.

2. Look for shirts with stretch

Shirts with stretch are a great option for big and tall men. They provide extra room in the shoulders and chest, while still maintaining a slim fit.

3. Consider custom tailoring

If you have trouble finding shirts that fit well off the rack, consider investing in custom tailoring. This can be more expensive, but it will ensure a perfect fit.

FAQs

Q: Can I buy big and tall dress shirts online?

A: Yes, there are several online retailers that specialize in big and tall clothing, including dress shirts.

Q: How do I know what size to buy?

A: Take your measurements before you start shopping and refer to the store's size chart to find the right size.

Q: Can I return a dress shirt if it doesn't fit?

A: Yes, most stores and online retailers have a return policy that allows you to return items that don't fit.

Q: How do I care for my dress shirts?

A: Follow the care instructions on the label to ensure your shirts last as long as possible.

Conclusion

Shopping for dress shirts when you're big and tall can be a challenge, but there are plenty of options available. By shopping at stores that specialize in extended sizes, knowing your measurements, and considering custom tailoring, you can find the perfect shirt that fits well and looks great. Happy shopping!
