What Is Indigenous Fashion

Written by Aprilia Jul 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Indigenous fashion is a growing trend that has been gaining popularity in recent years. It is a way for Indigenous people to showcase their culture, traditions, and creativity through clothing and accessories. In this blog post, we will explore what indigenous fashion is, its importance, and how it is different from mainstream fashion.

Many Indigenous people have faced cultural appropriation and assimilation throughout history. Indigenous fashion is a way for them to reclaim their culture and showcase their identity in a positive way. It is a way for them to share their stories, traditions, and beliefs with the world.

Indigenous fashion can be defined as clothing, accessories, and textiles that are created by Indigenous artists and designers. It often incorporates traditional designs, materials, and techniques, but can also incorporate modern elements.

In summary, Indigenous fashion is a way for Indigenous people to express their culture and identity through clothing and accessories. It is a way for them to reclaim their culture and share it with the world.

Why is Indigenous Fashion Important?

Indigenous fashion is important for several reasons. Firstly, it is a way for Indigenous people to express their culture and identity. It is a way for them to showcase their traditions, beliefs, and stories through clothing and accessories.

Secondly, Indigenous fashion is a way for Indigenous people to reclaim their culture. It is a way for them to take back what has been taken from them through cultural appropriation and assimilation. Indigenous fashion allows them to showcase their culture in a positive way and share it with the world on their terms.

Lastly, Indigenous fashion is important for the fashion industry as a whole. It brings diversity, creativity, and authenticity to an industry that has often been criticized for its lack of representation and inclusivity.

The Difference Between Indigenous Fashion and Mainstream Fashion

The main difference between Indigenous fashion and mainstream fashion is the cultural significance. Indigenous fashion is not just clothing or accessories, but a way to express culture, traditions, and beliefs. It is more than just a trend, but a way of life.

Another difference is the use of materials and techniques. Indigenous fashion often incorporates traditional materials such as fur, leather, and beads, and traditional techniques such as beadwork, embroidery, and quillwork. Mainstream fashion tends to use synthetic materials and mass production techniques.

How to Support Indigenous Fashion

Supporting Indigenous fashion is a way to support Indigenous communities and their culture. Here are some ways to support Indigenous fashion:

  • Buy from Indigenous designers and artists
  • Attend Indigenous fashion shows and events
  • Learn about the culture and traditions behind Indigenous fashion
  • Share and promote Indigenous fashion on social media

Personal Experience with Indigenous Fashion

As an Indigenous person myself, I have always been drawn to Indigenous fashion. It is a way for me to connect with my culture and express my identity through clothing and accessories. I love how Indigenous fashion incorporates traditional designs and techniques while also incorporating modern elements.

One of my favorite Indigenous fashion designers is Bethany Yellowtail, who is a Crow and Northern Cheyenne designer based in Los Angeles. Her designs are a beautiful blend of traditional and modern elements, and she incorporates sustainable practices into her work.

Question and Answer

Q: What are some common materials used in Indigenous fashion?

A: Some common materials used in Indigenous fashion include fur, leather, beads, quills, and feathers.

Q: What is the significance of traditional designs in Indigenous fashion?

A: Traditional designs in Indigenous fashion are a way to express culture, history, and identity. They often incorporate symbols and motifs that have been passed down through generations.

Q: How can I learn more about Indigenous fashion?

A: You can learn more about Indigenous fashion by attending Indigenous fashion shows and events, following Indigenous designers on social media, and reading books and articles about Indigenous fashion.

Q: How can I support Indigenous fashion as a non-Indigenous person?

A: You can support Indigenous fashion by buying from Indigenous designers and artists, attending Indigenous fashion shows and events, and promoting Indigenous fashion on social media.

Conclusion of What is Indigenous Fashion

Indigenous fashion is a way for Indigenous people to express their culture, traditions, and creativity through clothing and accessories. It is an important way for them to reclaim their culture and share it with the world. By supporting Indigenous fashion, we can support Indigenous communities and their culture.
